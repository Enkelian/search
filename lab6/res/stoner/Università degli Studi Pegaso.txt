Università degli Studi Pegaso
The Pegaso University (Italian: Università degli Studi Pegaso), often simply abbreviated as "Unipegaso" is a private university founded in 2006 in Naples, Italy. Method of Study Provides e-learning courses and it has centers in several Italian cities.
