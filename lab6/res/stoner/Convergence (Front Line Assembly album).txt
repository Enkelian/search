Convergence (Front Line Assembly album)
Convergence is a compilation album by Canadian industrial artist Front Line Assembly. This release contains all but two tracks from the Corrosion and Disorder releases, "The Wrack Part III - Wisdom", and "Aggression" respectively. The latter would later be re-released on Corroded Disorder. Track listing All tracks are written by Bill Leeb and Michael Balch, except where noted.Tracks 1, 3–5, 11–13 from Corrosion. Tracks 2, 7, 8, 14 from Disorder. Tracks 6, 9, 10 previously unreleased.
