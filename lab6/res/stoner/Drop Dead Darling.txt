Drop Dead Darling
Drop Dead Darling (US title: Arrivederci, Baby!) is a 1966 British-American comedy crime film directed by Ken Hughes and starring Tony Curtis, Rosanna Schiaffino, Lionel Jeffries and Zsa Zsa Gabor.
