Autonomy (Drenge EP)
Autonomy is an extended play released by British blues rock band, Drenge. The extended play was released on 5 October 2018 through Infectious, in between their second and third studio albums, Undertow and Strange Creatures, respectively. Track listing Critical reception Autonomy received general positive reviews from contemporary music critics. On review aggregator website, Album of the Year, the album has an average rating of 73 out of 100 based on three critic reviews.
