Eagle Aviation France
Eagle Aviation France was a charter airline based in Saint-Nazaire, France. Its wet lease operations were based in Paris at Charles de Gaulle Airport. History The airline started operations in 2002. Eagle Aviation has operated 3 B757-2Q8 for Saudi Arabian Airlines in ACMI.
