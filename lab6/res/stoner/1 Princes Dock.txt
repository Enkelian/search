1 Princes Dock
1 Princes Dock (also known as Liverpool City Lofts) is a 22-storey residential complex located alongside Prince's Dock, in Liverpool, England. It was completed in 2006 and at 73 metres (240 ft) is the city's joint-tenth-tallest building. The building is home to 162 flats and 99 parking spaces. 1 Princes Dock was first proposed in 2003 and was quickly approved with construction commencing in the next year, the building was designed by AFL Architects and developed by City Lofts Group PLC.
