Konstantinos Tasoulas
Konstantinos Tasoulas (Greek: Κωνσταντίνος Τασούλας; born 17 July 1959, Ioannina, Greece) is a Greek politician serving as Speaker of the Hellenic Parliament from 18 July 2019. He has also served as Minister of Culture and Sports, Deputy Minister of National Defence and Mayor of Kifissia. He also worked as a lawyer in Athens and London, while in 1981-1990 he was special secretary of Evangelos Averoff, before being elected alderman of Kifissia.
