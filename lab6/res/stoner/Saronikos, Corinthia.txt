Saronikos, Corinthia
Saronikos (Greek: Σαρωνικός) is a former municipality in Corinthia, Peloponnese, Greece. Since the 2011 local government reform it is part of the municipality Corinth, of which it is a municipal unit. The municipal unit has an area of 136.578 km2. Population 5,260 (2011). The seat of the municipality was in Athikia. == References ==
