Brierley, Gloucestershire
Brierley is a village in the Forest of Dean, Gloucestershire, United Kingdom. It has a petrol station, shop, and bus services to other places. Brierley was the birthplace of Winifred Foley (25 July 1914 â€“ 21 March 2009), author of the autobiographical A Child in the Forest and other later works, including No Pipe Dreams for Father.
