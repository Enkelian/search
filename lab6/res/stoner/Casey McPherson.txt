Casey McPherson
Casey McPherson (born September 15, 1978) is a singer and songwriter based in Austin, Texas. He is currently the frontman of the band Alpha Rev and formerly sang in the band Endochine. Casey grew up in Jones Creek, Texas where he was home-schooled. When he was 18, his father committed suicide. He was classically trained on the piano.In 2011 he was invited to join Flying Colors, a supergroup with ex-Dream Theater drummer Mike Portnoy, guitarist Steve Morse and bassist Dave LaRue of the Dixie Dregs, and keyboardist Neal Morse, formerly of Spock's Beard.
