Show Me Love (Alicia Keys and Miguel song)
"Show Me Love" is a song by American singers and songwriters Alicia Keys and Miguel. It was written by Keys, Miguel, Daystar Peterson and Morgan Matthews, while production was handled by Keys and Matthews. The song was released on September 17, 2019 through RCA Records with UMPG as the lead single from Keys' upcoming seventh studio album Alicia, which is set for a May 15, 2020 release. It is an R&B ballad involving feelings of desire . A remix version of the song featuring American rapper 21 Savage was released on November 4, 2019. The song received positive reviews from music critics, who went on to compliment the chemistry between the duo, as well as praising its sultriness and charm. Commercially, the song debuted on the US Billboard Hot 100 chart at number 90, becoming Keys' first single as a lead artist to debut on the charts in seven years; the last one being "Girl on Fire" (2012). It would also become her eleventh chart topper on Adult R&B Songs, extended her record as the artist with the most number ones on the chart.
