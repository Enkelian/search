Get It On (Kingdom Come song)
"Get It On" is the first single by hard rock/glam metal band Kingdom Come from their self-titled debut album. It reached number four on the Billboard Album Rock Tracks chart and number 69 on the Billboard Hot 100 singles chart. Track listing "Get It On" (Music - L. Wolf / Words - M. Wolff, L.
