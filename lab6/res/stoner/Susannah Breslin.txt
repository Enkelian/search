Susannah Breslin
Susannah Breslin is a journalist and writer. She is the editor of Forbes' Vices section. She has been a reporter for the Playboy TV program Sexcetera. Her blogging and television work deals with sexual and pornography-related topics. She has also written for periodicals including Playboy and appeared on Politically Incorrect as well as CNN and Fox News. Her published works include You're a Bad Man, Aren't You?, a book of short stories.Her blog The Reverse Cowgirl was named by Time.com as one of the 25 best blogs in 2008.
