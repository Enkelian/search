Immortalized (song)
"Immortalized" is a song by heavy metal band Disturbed, from their sixth studio album Immortalized. It was released on June 29, 2015 as a promotional single from the album. Track listing Personnel David Draiman – lead vocals, background vocals Dan Donegan – lead and rhythm guitars, EBow, keyboards, bass, background vocals Mike Wengren – drums, percussion, background vocals Charts In popular culture Immortalized was released as a downloadable content for the video game, Rock Band 4, on January 12, 2016.
