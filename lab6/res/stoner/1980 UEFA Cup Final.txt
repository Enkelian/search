1980 UEFA Cup Final
The 1980 UEFA Cup Final was played on 7 May 1980, and 21 May 1980, between Eintracht Frankfurt and Borussia Mönchengladbach, both of Germany. All 4 semifinalists were German. Eintracht Frankfurt won on away goals after the tie ended 3–3 on aggregate.
