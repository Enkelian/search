Close as You Get
Close as You Get is sixteenth solo album by Northern Irish blues guitarist and singer Gary Moore. The album reunited Moore with his former Thin Lizzy colleague, drummer Brian Downey who played on all the tracks. Track listing Personnel Gary Moore - vocals, guitar, dobro on "Sundown" Brian Downey - drums Vic Martin - keyboards Pete Rees - bass Mark Feltham - harmonica on "Hard Times" and "Checkin' Up on My Baby" Produced by Gary Moore and Ian Taylor Engineered and mixed by Ian Taylor, assisted by Jonathan Tayler-Webb.
