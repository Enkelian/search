Central New Mexico
Central New Mexico is the central region of the U.S. state of New Mexico. In the center of this region is Albuquerque, New Mexico, the largest city in the state. The top 7 largest cities in Central New Mexico are: 1. Albuquerque 2. Rio Rancho 3. Los Lunas 4. Bernalillo 5. Socorro 6. Corrales 7.
