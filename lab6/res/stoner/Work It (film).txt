Work It (film)
Work It is an upcoming American coming of age dance comedy film directed by Laura Terruso and produced by Alicia Keys, Leslie Morgenstein and Elysa Koplovitz Dutton. The film stars Sabrina Carpenter, Liza Koshy, Keiynan Lonsdale, Drew Ray Tanner, Michelle Buteau and Jordan Fisher. Plot The film follows Quinn Ackerman, who recruits dancers to teach her dance in order to win a competition and get into the school of her dreams. Cast Sabrina Carpenter as Quinn Ackerman Liza Koshy Keiynan Lonsdale Drew Ray Tanner Michelle Buteau Jordan Fisher Production On April 2, 2019, Adam Fogelson of STX announced the film along with Alicia Keys producing it. On May 2, 2019, It was announced that Sabrina Carpenter, Liza Koshy and Keiynan Lonsdale would star in the film. As well as Laura Terruso, Alison Peck and Elysa Koplovitz Dutton's involvement in the film. On July 2, 2019, Drew Ray Tanner, Michelle Buteau and Jordan Fisher all joined the cast and it was announced that Netflix would distribute the film.
