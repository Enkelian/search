Dominican Summer League Dodgers
The Dominican Summer Dodgers are a minor league baseball team in the Dominican Summer League. The team plays in the Boca Chica Northwest division and is affiliated with the Los Angeles Dodgers. They have operated since 1989. Occasionally the Dodgers have fielded two teams in the DSL.
