The Peel Sessions (Prong EP)
The Peel Sessions is Prong's 2nd EP. It was released in 1990.Recorded in 1989 and released by Strange Fruit Records, the EP features four tracks from Primitive Origins and Force Fed, but produced with a "cleaner" sound. Track listing "Defiant" "Decay" "Senseless Abuse" "In My Veins"The session was broadcast on February 1, 1989, on "The John Peel Show" on BBC Radio 1. == References ==
