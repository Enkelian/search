California's 74th State Assembly district
California's 74th State Assembly district is one of 80 California State Assembly districts. It is currently represented by Democrat Cottie Petrie-Norris of Laguna Beach. District profile The district encompasses coastal central Orange County. Anchored by Irvine, the district is primarily suburban and affluent.
