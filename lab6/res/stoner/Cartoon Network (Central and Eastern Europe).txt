Cartoon Network (Central and Eastern Europe)
Cartoon Network is a European pay television channel broadcasting in Czech Republic, Hungary, Moldova, Romania and Slovakia. It launched on 1 October 2008 as a separate feed from the Polish feed. It is owned by Warner Bros. Europe.A Czech audio track started on 20 September 2017. On 20 October 2017, Cartoon Network started broadcasting 24 hours a day. On 26 February 2018, the channel launched a high-definition feed. In 17 January 2019, Cartoon Network changed its aspect ratio from 4:3 to 16:9.
