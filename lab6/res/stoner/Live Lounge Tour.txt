Live Lounge Tour
The Live Lounge Tour was a tour taken by BBC Radio 1 from 2006, until 2011 This features Jo Whiley/Fearne Cotton going to the Live Lounge artists' house, houseboat, local pub, mum's house, etc. to have a look around at a place that means something to the artist. In 2010 the Live Lounge Tour was rebranded the "Radio 1's Student Tour" with Fearne Cotton and Zane Lowe. 2006 2007 2008 2009 2010 Student Tour 2011 Student Tour == References ==
