1927 UCLA Grizzlies football team
The 1927 UCLA Grizzlies football team was an American football team that represented the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) during the 1927 college football season. In their third year under head coach William H. Spaulding, the Grizzlies compiled a 6–2–1 record and outscored their opponents by a combined total of 144 to 54. Schedule == References ==
