BBC Urdu
BBC Urdu (Urdu: بی بی سی اردو‎) is the Urdu language station of the BBC World Service, accompanied by its website, which serves as a news portal and provides online access to radio broadcasts. The radio service is broadcast from Broadcasting House in London as well as from a BBC bureau in Islamabad. The target audience are primarily Pakistanis and a considerable amount of Indians.
