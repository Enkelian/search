Extreme music (disambiguation)
Extreme music may refer to any of the following musical categories, or their overlap: Extreme metal, a group of transgressive heavy metal subgenres Hardcore punk, and other punk rock genres characterized by speed and aggression Experimental music, which radically challenges existing musical conventions Noise music, which blurs the distinction between musical and non-musical sound See also Extreme Music, a music production company Extreme (disambiguation) Extreme Records, a record label
