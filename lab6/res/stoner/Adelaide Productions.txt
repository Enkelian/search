Adelaide Productions
Adelaide Productions is a television animation division of Sony Pictures Television that was founded on April 12, 1993, by Columbia Pictures Television and was incorporated seven days later. It has also been titled Columbia TriStar Animation and Columbia TriStar Children's Television. As of 2019, Adelaide Productions has been dormant of making animated TV shows, and had not made any new ones since 2009, but its status is still listed as "active".
