GreatNonprofits
GreatNonprofits is a 501(c)-3 registered non-profit organization based in the United States that describes itself as "the leading developer of tools that allow people to find, review, and share information about great – and perhaps not yet great – nonprofits". The site allows donors, volunteers, and clients to share their personal experiences with and reviews of charitable organizations, essentially providing crowdsourced information about the reputability of these organizations. Media coverage GreatNonprofits has been covered in The Economist, Christian Science Monitor, Good (magazine), and other news sources.
