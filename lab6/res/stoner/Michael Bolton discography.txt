Michael Bolton discography
This article presents the discography of American pop singer Michael Bolton. Since 1975, Bolton has released 18 studio albums and 35 singles. Nine of Bolton's singles have peaked at #1 on either the Billboard Hot 100 or the Hot Adult Contemporary Tracks charts in the United States.
