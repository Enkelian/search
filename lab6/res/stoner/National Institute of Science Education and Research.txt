National Institute of Science Education and Research
The National Institute of Science Education and Research (NISER) is a premier fully residential public science education and research institution in Jatani (Khordha), Odisha, India under the umbrella of Department Of Atomic Energy, Govt. of India (DAE-GOI). The institute is one of the best in the country in the field of basic science and research mainly known for its quality education and infrastructure, and is intended to be the leading institute in the country in the field of basic sciences along with its other sister institutes. The physics and mathematics departments of NISER are one of the best in the country due to their association with HBNI and BARC (under DAE). It was established to provide quality collegiate education in basic sciences coupled with cutting-edge research at the undergraduate level.
