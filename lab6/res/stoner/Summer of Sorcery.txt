Summer of Sorcery
Summer of Sorcery is a studio album by American singer-songwriter Steven Van Zandt's solo band Little Steven and The Disciples of Soul. It was released on May 3, 2019 under Universal Music Enterprises. Critical reception Summer of Sorcery was met with generally favorable reviews from critics noted at review aggregator Metacritic.
