MusicRadar
MusicRadar is a music website that offers information pertaining to artists and their music, with interviews, product news and reviews, and online music lessons. It is owned by British media company Future plc, which incorporates monthly music-making titles including Total Guitar, Guitarist, Rhythm and Computer Music. The site's editor-in-chief is Will Groves. One of Future's music portals besides Louder, it identifies itself as "The No. 1 website for musicians". The site was launched in December 2007 and originally included "major user-generated content areas for amateur and professional musicians".Since 2010, its contributors have included Joe Bosso and Record Collector contributor Terry Staunton. == References ==
