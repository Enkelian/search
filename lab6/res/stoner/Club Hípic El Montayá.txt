Club Hípic El Montayá
The Club Hípic El Montanyà (English: El Montanyà Horse Club) is a Spanish horse club. For the 1992 Summer Olympics in neighboring Barcelona, it hosted the equestrian dressage and the cross country part of the eventing competitions. The venue was constructed on a golf course in 1991 over 200 ha (490 acres). References 1992 Summer Olympics official report. Volume 2. pp. 324–6.
