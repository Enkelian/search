1896 Brooklyn Bridegrooms season
The 1896 Brooklyn Bridegrooms finished the season in tenth place in the crowded National League race. Offseason November 18, 1895: Tommy McCarthy was purchased by the Bridegrooms from the Boston Beaneaters.
