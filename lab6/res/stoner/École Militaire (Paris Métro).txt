École Militaire (Paris Métro)
École Militaire ([ekɔl militɛʁ]) is a station on line 8 of the Paris Métro near the École Militaire (military school). The station was opened on 13 July 1913 as part of the original section of Line 8 between Beaugrenelle (now Charles Michels, which is now on line 10) and Opéra.
