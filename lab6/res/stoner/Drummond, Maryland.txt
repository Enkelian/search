Drummond, Maryland
Drummond is a village and special taxing district in Montgomery County, Maryland, United States. The village was founded in 1903 and officially chartered in 1916. Approximately one-third mile long, the village consists of Drummond Avenue between Wisconsin Avenue and Little Falls Stream Valley Park.The population was 120 as of 1990.
