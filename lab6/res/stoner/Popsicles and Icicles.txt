Popsicles and Icicles
"Popsicles and Icicles" is a song written by David Gates and performed by The Murmaids. It reached No. 2 on the Middle-Road Singles chart, No. 3 on the Billboard Hot 100 and Cash Box Top 100, and No. 12 in Australia in 1964. The single was arranged by Nestor La Bonte and produced by Kim Fowley."Popsicles and Icicles" was ranked No. 31 on Cash Box's "Top 100 Chart Hits of 1964". Other versions Percy Faith and His Orchestra released an instrumental version of the song on his 1964 EP More Themes for Young Lovers. Billy Vaughn released a version of the song on his 1964 album Blue Velvet & 1963's Great Hits. The Boone Girls released a version of the song on the 2017 re-release of the 1977 Debby Boone's album You Light Up My Life. == References ==
