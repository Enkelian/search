2007–08 Moldovan National Division
The Moldovan National Division 2007-08 was the 17th season of the Moldovan National Division, the top division of association football in Moldova. The season started on 4 July 2007. FC Sheriff Tiraspol won the league for the eighth consecutive season. The champions also qualified for the Commonwealth of Independent States Cup 2009. Rapid Ghidighici withdrew from the league in November after the conclusion of round 16.
