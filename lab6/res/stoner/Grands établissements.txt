Grands établissements
The grands établissements (French: [ɡʁɑ̃.z‿e.tab.lis.mɑ̃]; "great establishments") are French public institutions under ministerial charter under the administrative category referred to as Établissements publics à caractère scientifique, culturel et professionnel (EPCSP).
