Diamond Head (Diamond Head album)
Diamond Head is the seventh studio album by heavy metal band Diamond Head, released on 22 April 2016. This is the first album from the band to feature Rasmus Bom Andersen as the lead vocalist. The album reached number 15 on the UK Rock & Metal Albums Chart.
