In the Maybe World
In the Maybe World is an album by folk musician Lisa Germano. It was released in 2006 through Young God Records.The album peaked at #3 on Billboard's Top New Age Albums in the summer of 2006, becoming her biggest chart hit in the US. "Red Thread" was a college radio hit. Track listing All songs written by Lisa Germano.
