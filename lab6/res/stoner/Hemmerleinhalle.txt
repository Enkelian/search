Hemmerleinhalle
Hemmerleinhalle was a 3,500-capacity music venue located in Neunkirchen am Brand, Germany. Many well-known artists performed at the venue such as Kiss, Bon Jovi, Black Sabbath, Slayer, Metallica, Iron Maiden in 1980, 1982, 2x in 1983, Rush, AC/DC, The Kinks, Whitesnake, Ozzy Osbourne, Frank Zappa and Thin Lizzy. It opened in 1973 and closed in 1988. == References ==
