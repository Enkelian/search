A.k.a. Cartoon
a.k.a. Cartoon is a Canadian animation studio located in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. It was founded on April 1, 1994, by Danny Antonucci. The company's motto is "Dedicated to producing animation for everyone, whether they want it or not!" a.k.a. Cartoon created and produced The Brothers Grunt and Cartoon Sushi — both for MTV — but is best known for Ed, Edd n Eddy, an animated series that used to air on Cartoon Network, as well as an animated pilot, Snotrocket.
