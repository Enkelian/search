Super Concert '70
Super Concert '70 was a one-day music festival held at the Deutschlandhalle in Berlin, Germany, on September 4, 1970. The festival was headlined by Jimi Hendrix. Other artists on the bill included Canned Heat (whose harmonica/guitar player and singer, Alan "Blind Owl" Wilson, died the day before), Procol Harum, Ten Years After, Cat Mother, Cold Blood, and Murphy Blend.A notable fact about this festival is that it was Hendrix's next to last performance.
