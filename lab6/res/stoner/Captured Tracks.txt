Captured Tracks
Captured Tracks is an American independent record label based in Brooklyn, New York. About Current flagship artists include: Chris Cohen, Craft Spells, DIIV and Wild Nothing.Captured Tracks has reissued recordings by influential British artists the Monochrome Set, the Wake, the Servants, Stockholm Monsters and Cleaners from Venus.The label has also reissued recordings from New Zealand's Flying Nun Records, L.A.'s Medicine and Nebraska's For Against, among others.
