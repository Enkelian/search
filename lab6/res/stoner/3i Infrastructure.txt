3i Infrastructure
3i Infrastructure plc (LSE: 3IN) is an investment business headquartered in Jersey. It is listed on the London Stock Exchange and is a constituent of the FTSE 250 Index. About 34% of the company is owned by 3i Group plc. History The company was launched by way of an Initial Public Offering in 2007. It was announced that on 2 November 2016 Philip Austin MBE, the Senior Independent Director of the Board of Directors, formally resigned as a Director of the Company, with effect from 31 December 2016. Operations The company's focus is on assets in Europe, North America and Asia.
