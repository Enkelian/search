Nine Lives (Def Leppard song)
"Nine Lives" is a 2008 song by English hard rock band Def Leppard from their 2008 album, Songs from the Sparkle Lounge as a duet with American country singer Tim McGraw. It was the first single from the album, released on 27 April 2008, two days after the album release. Music video The music video featuring Tim McGraw was shot somewhere around April 2008 and was directed by Sherman Halsey.
