Single Cuts
Single Cuts is a limited edition box set by English heavy metal band Judas Priest consisting of 20 of the band's singles on 20 CDs including all original b-sides, making a total of 51 tracks. It was announced on 7 June 2011, for release on 25 August, later delayed to 17 October for "technical reasons", with free magnets being offered as a compensation for the delay to anyone who pre-ordered the box set.
