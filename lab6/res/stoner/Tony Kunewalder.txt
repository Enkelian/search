Tony Kunewalder
Tony Kunewalder (Anthony Paul Kunewalder) (October 17, 1962 – May 14, 1995) was a music video director and screenwriter. He began composing songs, poems, stories and screen plays at an early age. After graduating from the University of Colorado, he led a rock band, directed music videos and sold an original screen play to Oliver Stone. His poetry was collected into the book-length POETRYTIME. He was just beginning work on his first feature film when (at age 32) he was killed in a house fire.
