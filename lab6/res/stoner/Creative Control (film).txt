Creative Control (film)
Creative Control is a 2015 science fiction drama film, directed by Benjamin Dickinson from a screenplay written by Micah Bloomberg and Dickinson. It stars Dickinson, Nora Zehetner, Dan Gill, Alexia Rasmussen, Gavin McInnes and Reggie Watts. The film had its world premiere at South by Southwest on March 14, 2015. It was released on March 11, 2016, by Amazon Studios and Magnolia Pictures. Plot In the near future, an ad executive uses a new reality technology to conduct an affair with his best friend's girlfriend. Cast Post-production A campaign was set up on Kickstarter.com to raise funds for the visual effects on the film, $32,000 was raised against a $30,000 goal. Release The film had its world premiere at South by Southwest on March 14, 2015. Shortly after, Amazon Studios and Magnolia Pictures acquired distribution rights to the film. The film was released in a limited release on March 11, 2016. It was released on video on demand on April 12, 2016. Reception Creative Control received mixed reviews from film critics.
