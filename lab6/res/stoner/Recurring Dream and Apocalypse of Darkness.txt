Recurring Dream and Apocalypse of Darkness
Recurring Dream and Apocalypse of Darkness is an album by Acid Mothers Temple & The Melting Paraiso U.F.O., released in 2008 by Important Records. Release The album was released on CD and 1000 copy limited edition LP. The LP was available in two colors (orange and white) limited to 200 copies each or on standard black vinyl, limited to 800 copies. The LP version also contained extra two bonus tracks. Track listing CD All tracks are written by Atsushi and Kawabata.
