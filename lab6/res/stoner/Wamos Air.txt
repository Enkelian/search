Wamos Air
Wamos Air, formerly Pullmantur Air, is a Spanish airline headquartered in Madrid. It mostly operates leisure charter flights, several of them on behalf of sister company Pullmantur Cruises from its main base at Adolfo Suárez Madrid–Barajas Airport. As of 2016, it is 81% owned by Springwater Capital and 19% owned by Royal Caribbean Cruises. The British Government charted a Wamos Air 747 multiple times to repatriate British citizens in Wuhan, China and Japan during the beginning of the 2019-2020 Coronavirus Pandemic.
