2007–08 Polish Cup
The Polish Cup 2007-08 or Puchar Polski 2007-08' is a knockout competition for teams playing under the PZPN. The first game was played on 1 August 2007 and the final is to be played between Legia Warszawa and Wisła Kraków, with the final date to be determined.
