Derrty Entertainment
Derrty is a record label founded by Nelly who is the CEO. The president of the label is Ali. Derrty is a division of Motown. The 1st studio album released under Derrty was Nelly's Country Grammar. In recent years, the label has collaborated with So So Def, Star Trak and Sho'Nuff.
