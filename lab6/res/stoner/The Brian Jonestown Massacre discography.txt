The Brian Jonestown Massacre discography
The discography of The Brian Jonestown Massacre (a San Francisco, CA based, neo-psychedelic band) consists of 18 studio albums, 14 EPs, five live albums, six compilation albums and 18 singles, as well as appearing on various artist releases and soundtracks. They have one rockumentary (Dig!) to their credit and a DVD release of their music videos entitled Book of Days. Their music has been released by Bomp!, TVT and Tee Pee Records, among others. The bands were apparently handpicked by Anton Newcombe himself, and it is rumored that he also produced the music.
