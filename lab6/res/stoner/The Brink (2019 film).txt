The Brink (2019 film)
The Brink is a 2019 documentary film, directed by Alison Klayman. The film is produced by Marie Therese Guirgis, and Alison Klayman under the banner of AliKlay Productions, Claverie Films, and RYOT Films. The film stars Steve Bannon. Plot The film follows Bannon from his latter days in the Trump administration to just after the 2018 midterms as he attempts to form a global populist movement with like minded individuals such as Nigel Farage, Marie Le Pen, Jair Bolsonaro, Viktor Orban, etc.. The fecklessness of the effort seems to reflect the contradiction of a global anti-globalist program.
