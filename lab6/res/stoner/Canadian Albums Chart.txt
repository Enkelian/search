Canadian Albums Chart
The Canadian Albums Chart is the official album sales chart in Canada. It is compiled every Monday by U.S.-based music sales tracking company Nielsen SoundScan, and published every Tuesday by Billboard.The chart consists of the top 200 positions, like the U.S. Billboard 200 chart; however, Billboard publishes only the top 100 for the public.
