Putri Raemawasti
Gracia Putri Raemawasti (born December 5, 1986 in Blitar, East Java) is Indonesian public figure and journalist. She was the winner of Puteri Indonesia (Miss Indonesia Universe) 2007 from East Java. She has attended the Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember, Surabaya, majoring in industrial engineering. Miss Universe 2008 Raemawasti represented Indonesia at the Miss Universe 2008, but was unplaced. At the Miss Universe, she was the first Miss Indonesia to wear a two-piece bathing suit rather than the usual maillot one piece during the final presentation show.
