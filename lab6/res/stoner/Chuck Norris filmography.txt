Chuck Norris filmography
Chuck Norris is an American martial artist and actor. The following is a filmography of his work. Norris appeared in a number of action films, such as Way of the Dragon, in which he starred alongside Bruce Lee, and was The Cannon Group's leading star in the 1980s. He played the starring role in the television series Walker, Texas Ranger, from 1993 to 2001.
