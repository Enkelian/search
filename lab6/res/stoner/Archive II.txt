Archive II
Archive II is a three disc compilation of live and demo material by Japanese band, Boris. Volume 4 was recorded on 2003/5/15 in Tokyo and only contains songs from Heavy Rocks and Akuma no Uta, while volume 5 was recorded on 2006/5/31 in New York City and contains songs from Pink, Akuma no Uta, Dronevil, and Sound Track from Film "Mabuta no Ura".
