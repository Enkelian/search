1941–42 La Liga
The 1941–42 La Liga was the 11th season of Spanish football league. Valencia won their first title ever. Team locations Real Sociedad came back after a three-season absence. Granada, Deportivo La Coruña and Castellón made their debut in La Liga. For this season, Hércules played with the denomination of Alicante CF. The namesake club acted as its reserve team with the name of Lucentum CF. League table Results Relegation play-offs Both matches were played at Estadio Chamartín in Chamartín de la Rosa.
