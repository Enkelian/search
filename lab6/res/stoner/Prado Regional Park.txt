Prado Regional Park
Prado Regional Park is a 2,000-acre (8.1 km2) park in Chino, California within the jurisdiction of San Bernardino County. It offers fishing, a shooting range (site of the 1984 Olympic shooting events), archery, camping, and a golf course. Horseback riding References 1984 Summer Olympics official report. Volume 1.
