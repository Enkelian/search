PopRocks
PopRocks is a music channel that plays 1990s and 2000s Pop rock, and airs on Sirius XM Radio and Dish Network. It airs on Channel 17 on Sirius XM Radio. History PopRocks debuted on August 17, 2017 as a full-time channel devoted to the pop-rock genre of music from the 1990s, 2000s, and occasionally, songs from the 2010s.
