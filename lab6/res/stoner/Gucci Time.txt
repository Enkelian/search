Gucci Time
"Gucci Time" is a song by American rapper Gucci Mane featuring vocals and production from Swizz Beatz. The song premiered on Gucci Mane's official MySpace page on August 6, 2010 and was released on August 30, 2010 as the lead single from Gucci Mane's 's third studio album The Appeal: Georgia's Most Wanted. The song samples French electronic music duo Justice's song "Phantom Pt. II". Music video The music video was released on September 4, 2010 on Myspace. The Chris Robinson-directed music video show Gucci Mane and Swizz Beatz perform throughout various location in Los Angeles, from the streets to a location above to city's skyline to the club.
