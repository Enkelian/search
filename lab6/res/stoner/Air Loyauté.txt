Air Loyauté
Air Loyauté is a French airline company which operates in New Caledonia. It is the main airline service for the Loyalty Islands and provides scheduled, medivac and charter services. Destinations Air Loyauté flies to: Nouméa, Koumac, Belep, Touho on Grande Terre and Maré, Tiga, Lifou and Ouvéa in the Loyalty Islands.
