University of Angers
The University of Angers (French: Université d'Angers) is an institution of higher education in the town of Angers in western France. History It began as the School of Angers in the 11th century and became a university in 1337.In 1432, the Faculties of Medicine, Arts, and Theology were added to the Faculty of Law.The university was closed in 1793 during the French Revolution, and in 1971 a successor was established with little ties to the past institutions.
