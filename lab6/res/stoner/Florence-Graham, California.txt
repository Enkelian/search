Florence-Graham, California
Florence-Graham (locally known as Florence-Firestone) is a census-designated place (CDP) in Los Angeles County, California. The population was 63,387 at the 2010 census, up from 60,197 at the 2000 census. The census area includes separate communities of Florence, Firestone Park, and Graham. Geography Florence-Graham is located at 33°58′10″N 118°14′38″W.According to the United States Census Bureau, the CDP has a total area of 3.6 square miles (9.3 km2), all land. Florence-Firestone, also known as Florence-Graham, is an unincorporated neighborhood in the South Los Angeles region of Los Angeles County. The neighboring communities are Central-Alameda, Florence, Green Meadows, Huntington Park, Lynwood, South Gate, South Park, Vernon, Walnut Park and Watts.
