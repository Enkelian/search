Mean Deviation (book)
Mean Deviation: Four Decades of Progressive Heavy Metal is a non-fiction book written by former Metal Maniacs magazine editor Jeff Wagner. The book was illustrated by Voivod drummer Michel "Away" Langevin and has a foreword by Porcupine Tree frontman Steven Wilson. Mean Deviation was published in December 2010 by Bazillion Points under supervision of Ian Christe. References External links Official website Mean Deviation at Google Books
