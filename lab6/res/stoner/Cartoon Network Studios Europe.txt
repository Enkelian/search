Cartoon Network Studios Europe
Cartoon Network Studios Europe (also known as Great Marlborough Productions, Inc. and formerly known as Cartoon Network Development Studio Europe) is a British animation studio based in London that is owned by Warner Bros. Europe and is the European equivalent to Cartoon Network Studios, based in Burbank, California.
