Boomerang (Italian TV channel)
For the original Boomerang channel, see Boomerang (TV network). For Boomerang in other countries, see Boomerang around the world.Boomerang is a television channel of old and new cartoons, including Hanna-Barbera series and others. It is the sister channel of Cartoon Network, a division of WarnerMedia. Since 2003, Boomerang also has an Italian version available on SKY Italia. For the most part, the network's schedule matches that of the main Boomerang in the United Kingdom and Ireland, with local scheduling variations and dubbing and subtitling of programmes into Italian.
