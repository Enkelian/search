Claudia Moro
Claudia Moro Fernández (born November 10, 1985 in Santiago de Compostela) is a Spanish model and beauty pageant titleholder who competed in the 2008 Miss Universe pageant, placing seventh overall. Miss Spain She represented Madrid at Miss Spain 2008 and placed 1st Runner-Up to Patricia Yurena Rodríguez. Miss Universe Moro was sent to Miss Universe 2008 due to Rodríguez being underage and finished in the Top 10 (7th overall).
