Israeli-American Council
The Israeli-American Council (IAC; Hebrew: ארגון הקהילה הישראלית-אמריקאית‎) is an American nonprofit organization that represents and serves more than 250,000 Israeli-Americans across the country. Its mission is to preserve and strengthen the Israeli and Jewish identities of future generations, strengthen the American Jewish community, and strengthen the relationship between citizens of the United States and the State of Israel. As of 2017, the IAC is the fastest-growing Jewish organization in the world. History Israeli Leadership Council (2007–2012)In 2007, the Israeli Leadership Council (ILC) was founded in Los Angeles by a group of Israeli-American business leaders and philanthropists.
