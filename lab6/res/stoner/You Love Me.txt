You Love Me
You Love Me is a 1999 promotional EP by the Meat Puppets. This was the first release from the then new line-up of the Meat Puppets. It was available free only through the official Meat Puppets website to promote the band's new line-up. "Armed and Stupid" and the title track were re-released on the 2000 album Golden Lies.
