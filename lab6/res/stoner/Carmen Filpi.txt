Biography His first acting job was in the 1969 film Wild Gypsies, he also starred in The Ice Pirates (1984), Pee-wee's Big Adventure (1985), Life Stinks (1991), and The Wedding Singer (1998). Carmen also acted in the 2000 made-for-TV film Goodbye Casanova with Yasmine Bleeth. Carmen's final film was in 2002 in the film Eight Crazy Nights. He also starred in the 1988 horror film Halloween 4: The Return of Michael Myers as the Reverend Jackson P. Sayer.Filpi made many guest appearances in TV series. Some of those appearances range from Baretta, Barney Miller, Quantum Leap, Married... with Children, Growing Pains, and Sabrina, the Teenage Witch.
