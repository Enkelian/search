Fuzz (Ty Segall album)
Fuzz is the debut studio album by native Californian band Fuzz. released on October 1, 2013, by the independent record company In the Red Records. The album features a traditional heavy metal and hard rock sound in the vein of Blue Cheer, High Tide, Black Sabbath, and Deep Purple Track listing Personnel Roland Cosio - bass, harmony on "What's in My Head" Ty Segall - drums / vocals Charles Moothart - guitar, main vocals on "Raise"Recording, mixing & wizardry : Chris Woodhouse, May 25-31 2013 @ The Dock, Sacramento, CA.
