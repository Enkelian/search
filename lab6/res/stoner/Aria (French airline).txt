Aria (French airline)
Aria is an airline based in Mulhouse, France. It operates ad hoc passenger and cargo flights, as well as medevac flights. Its main base is Paris - Le Bourget Airport History The airline was established in March 2004 and started operations in September 2004. It operated a scheduled service between Basle/Mulhouse and Toulouse, but this was suspended on 25 January 2005. The airline hoped to resume and build a regional network, but this has not happened.
