National Register of Historic Places listings in Palau
This is a list of the buildings, sites, districts, and objects listed on the National Register of Historic Places in the Republic of Palau. There are currently six listed sites located in only four of the sixteen states of Palau. This National Park Service list is complete through NPS recent listings posted April 10, 2020.
