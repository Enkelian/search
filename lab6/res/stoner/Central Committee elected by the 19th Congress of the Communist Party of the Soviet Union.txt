Central Committee elected by the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union
The 19th Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union was elected by the 19th Congress, and was in session from 1952 until 1956. It elected, at its 1st Plenary Session, the 19th Presidium, the 19th Secretariat and the 19th Party Control Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. Plenums Membership == References ==
