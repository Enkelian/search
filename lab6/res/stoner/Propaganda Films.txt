Propaganda Films
Propaganda Films was a music video and film production company founded in 1983 by producers Steve Golin and Sigurjón Sighvatsson and directors Greg Gold, David Fincher, Nigel Dick and Dominic Sena. By 1990, the company was producing almost a third of all music videos made in the U.S.Propaganda Films was acquired by PolyGram Filmed Entertainment in 1991.
