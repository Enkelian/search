Big Decisions
"Big Decisions" is a song by American rock band My Morning Jacket for its seventh studio album, The Waterfall (2015). It was released as the lead single from the record on March 2, 2015. It represents the group's highest peak on the Adult Alternative Songs chart in the U.S., where it reached number seven. Background "Big Decisions" is about people refraining to change things in their lives that make them unhappy. It was co-written by frontman Jim James and musician Dan Wilson.
