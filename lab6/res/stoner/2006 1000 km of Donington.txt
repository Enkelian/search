2006 1000 km of Donington
The 2006 1000 km of Donington was the fourth race of the 2006 Le Mans Series season run by the ACO. It was run on 27 August 2006 Official results Class winners in bold. Cars failing to complete 70% of winner's distance marked as Not Classified (NC).
