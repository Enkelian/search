2007–08 Azerbaijan Cup
The Azerbaijan Cup 2007–08 was the 16th season of the annual cup competition in Azerbaijan. Sixteen teams competed in this year's competition. Khazar-Lenkoran were the defending champions. Round of 16 NotesGänclärbirliyi Sumqayit was excluded from the competition. ABN Bärdä was excluded from the competition. Quarterfinals The first legs were played on March 6 and 07 while the second legs were played on March 18 and 19, 2008. Semifinals The first legs were played on April 9, 2008.
