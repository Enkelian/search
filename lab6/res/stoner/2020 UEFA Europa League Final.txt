2020 UEFA Europa League Final
The 2020 UEFA Europa League Final will be the final match of the 2019–20 UEFA Europa League, the 49th season of Europe's secondary club football tournament organised by UEFA, and the 11th season since it was renamed from the UEFA Cup to the UEFA Europa League. It is scheduled to be played at the Stadion Energa Gdańsk in Gdańsk, Poland. The final was originally scheduled to take place on 27 May 2020. On 23 March 2020, UEFA announced that the final was postponed indefinitely due to the 2020 coronavirus pandemic in Europe. A working group was set up to decide the calendar of the remainder of the season.The winners will earn the right to play against the winners of the 2019–20 UEFA Champions League in the 2020 UEFA Super Cup. They will also qualify to enter the group stage of the 2020–21 UEFA Champions League, and if they have already qualified through their league performance, the berth reserved will be given to the third-placed team of the 2019–20 Ligue 1, the 5th-ranked association according to next season's access list.
