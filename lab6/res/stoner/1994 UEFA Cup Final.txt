1994 UEFA Cup Final
The 1994 UEFA Cup Final was a two-legged match that took place on 26 April 1994 and 11 May 1994 at the Ernst-Happel-Stadion in Vienna and San Siro in Milan between Internazionale of Italy and Austria Salzburg of Austria. Inter won both games 1–0 to record a 2–0 aggregate victory.
