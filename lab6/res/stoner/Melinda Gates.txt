Gates has consistently been ranked as one of the world's most powerful women by Forbes. Early life Melinda Ann French was born on August 15, 1964 in Dallas, Texas. Melinda has an older sister and two younger brothers.Melinda, a Catholic, attended St. Monica Catholic School, where she was the top student in her class.
