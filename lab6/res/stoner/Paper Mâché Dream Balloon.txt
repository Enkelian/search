Paper Mâché Dream Balloon
Paper Mâché Dream Balloon is the seventh studio album by Australian psychedelic rock band King Gizzard & the Lizard Wizard. It was released on 13 November 2015 on the band's Bandcamp, and then a week later on Heavenly Records. The album was recorded with almost entirely acoustic instruments. Track listing Vinyl releases have tracks 1–6 on side A, and tracks 7–12 on side B. All tracks are written by Stu Mackenzie unless otherwise specified.
