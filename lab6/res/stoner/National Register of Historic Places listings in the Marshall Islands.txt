National Register of Historic Places listings in the Marshall Islands
This is a list of the buildings, sites, districts, and objects listed on the National Register of Historic Places in the Marshall Islands. There are currently 4 listed sites located in only three of the 24 atolls that make up the Marshall Islands. This National Park Service list is complete through NPS recent listings posted April 10, 2020.
