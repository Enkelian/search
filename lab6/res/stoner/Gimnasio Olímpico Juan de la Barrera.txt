Gimnasio Olímpico Juan de la Barrera
The Gimnasio Olímpico Juan de la Barrera is an indoor arena located in Mexico City, Mexico. At the 1968 Summer Olympics, it hosted the volleyball competitions, and it is located next to the Olympic pool. It is currently the home of the professional basketball team Capitanes de Ciudad de México. The arena seats 5,242 people in two stand levels. For years, it has been the traditional home of professional basketball in Mexico City. References 1968 Summer Olympics official report. Volume 2. Part 1. pp. 72, 74.
