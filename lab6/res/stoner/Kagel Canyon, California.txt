Kagel Canyon, California
Kagel Canyon is a rural unincorporated community in Los Angeles County, California. Geography The community and canyon are located in the southwestern San Gabriel Mountains foothills within the Angeles National Forest. It is located east above the City of San Fernando, and north above the community of Lakeview Terrace. The area is above the northeastern San Fernando Valley, and overlooks the western Crescenta Valley. In 2000, Kagel Canyon and the neighboring Lopez Canyon community had a combined population of 697.
