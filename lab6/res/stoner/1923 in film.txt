1923 in film
The following is an overview of 1923 in film, including significant events, a list of films released and notable births and deaths. Events April 4 – Warner Bros. Pictures Inc. incorporated. April 15 – Lee De Forest demonstrates the Phonofilm sound-on-film system at the Rivoli Theater in New York with a series of short musical films featuring vaudeville performers.
