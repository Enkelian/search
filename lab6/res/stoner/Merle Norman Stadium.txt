Merle Norman Stadium
Merle Norman Stadium is a beach volleyball facility located in Los Angeles. The on-campus USC facility, built in 2013, serves as the home of the USC Trojans women's beach volleyball team. The facility has 3 sand courts where USC plays its home matches and holds practices. The stadium is named for Merle Nethercutt Norman. Events The Women's beach volleyball team played their first dual match in the stadium on March 10, 2013 versus Loyola Marymount.
