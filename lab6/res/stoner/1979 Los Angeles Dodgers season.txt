1979 Los Angeles Dodgers season
The 1979 Los Angeles Dodgers finished the season in third place in the Western Division of the National League. Near the end of the season, owner Walter O'Malley died and the ownership of the team went to his son Peter. Offseason February 15, 1979: Brad Gulden was traded by the Dodgers to the New York Yankees for Gary Thomasson.
