2007 in film
The following is an overview of events in 2007 in film, including the highest-grossing films, award ceremonies and festivals, a list of films released and notable deaths. The highest-grossing film of the year was Pirates of the Caribbean: At World's End, which was just ahead of Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix. 2007 is often considered one of the greatest years for film in the 21st century. As of 2019, this was the last year in which no film grossed at least $1 billion at the worldwide box office. Evaluation of the year Many have considered 2007 to be one of the greatest years for film in the 21st century.
