1999 Deildabikar
The Deildabikar 1999 was the fourth staging of the Finnish League Cup. It featured 36 teams. The competition started on 11 March 1999 and concluded on 8 June 1999 with ÍA beating Fylkir 1-0 in the final. Details The 36 teams were divided into 6 groups of 6 teams. Each team plays one match with other teams in the group once. The top 2 teams from each group qualified for the 2nd round along with the best four 3rd placed teams.
