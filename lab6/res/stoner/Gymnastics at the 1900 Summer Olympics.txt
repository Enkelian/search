Gymnastics at the 1900 Summer Olympics
At the 1900 Summer Olympics one gymnastics event for men was contested. The competition was held on Sunday, July 29, 1900, and on Monday, July 30, 1900. Medal summary Results Gymnasts performed in 16 exercises, many of them being two competitions of the same discipline. There was a maximum of 20 points in each exercise, leading to a total maximum of 320 points.
