Erdre-en-Anjou
Erdre-en-Anjou is a commune in the Maine-et-Loire department of western France. Vern-d'Anjou is the municipal seat. History It was established on 28 December 2015 and consists of the former communes of Brain-sur-Longuenée, Gené, La Pouëze and Vern-d'Anjou.
