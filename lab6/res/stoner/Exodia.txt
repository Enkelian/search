Exodia
Exodia is the annual techno-cultural fest of Indian Institute of Technology Mandi. It is a three-day-long event held every year. The first edition was held in 2012 and since then it has grown to be one of the largest student-run college fests of the Himalayas. It is one of the biggest events of the Himalayan region, and attracts a lot of crowd from all over North India. For its first two years, the fest was organized in the transit Campus. The year 2014 saw for the first time, the fest moving to the permanent campus at Kamand, Mandi.
