It Came From Outer Space
It Came from Outer Space is a 1953 American black-and-white science fiction horror film, the first in the 3D process from Universal-International and the first 3D film in widescreen. It was produced by William Alland and directed by Jack Arnold. The film stars Richard Carlson and Barbara Rush, and features Charles Drake, Joe Sawyer, and Russell Johnson. Before long, odd things begin to happen, and the disbelief turns hostile.
