Live (Deftones album)
Live is an extended play by American alternative metal band Deftones, released on April 10, 1998 by Maverick Records. It features all of the live tracks that were previously featured on the two-part CD single "My Own Summer (Shove It)". Track listing "My Own Summer (Shove It)" - 3:34 "Root (Live)" - 4:35 "Nosebleed (Live)" - 4:22 "Lifter (Live)" - 4:48 "Lotion (Live)" - 3:54 "Fireal/Swords (Live)" - 6:22 "Bored (Live)" - 5:15
