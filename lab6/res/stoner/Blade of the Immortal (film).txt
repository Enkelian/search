Blade of the Immortal (film)
Blade of the Immortal (Japanese: 無限の住人, Hepburn: Mugen no jūnin) is a 2017 samurai film starring Takuya Kimura and Hana Sugisaki and directed by Takashi Miike. It is based on the successful manga series by Hiroaki Samura, covering the first two arcs of the series. The film premiered out of competition at the 2017 Cannes Film Festival, and was released theatrically in Japan by Warner Bros. Pictures on 29 April 2017. The theme song of the film, "Live to Die Another Day", is performed by Miyavi. Plot Manji is a samurai on the run after following his superior's order to kill a corrupt lord and his followers. Manji was deceived as it was his superior who is corrupt and it turned out that one of the followers Manji killed was the husband of his sister, Machi. As a result of her husband's death at the hands of her brother, Machi lost her mind from grief. Rather than commit ritual suicide as honor dictates, Manji chose to care for his now insane sister.
