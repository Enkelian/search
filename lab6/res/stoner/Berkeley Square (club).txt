Berkeley Square (club)
The Berkeley Square was a small music club located at 1333 University Avenue in Berkeley, California, operating in the 1970s through 1990s. Nationally known bands played at the club, including Primus, The Ramones, Red Hot Chili Peppers, Dread Zeppelin, Lords of the New Church, Crash Worship, Translator (band) and No Doubt.It was located at 1333 University Avenue. A small strip of businesses now stands on its former site. == References ==
