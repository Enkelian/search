Grim Tower
Grim Tower is collaboration between Imaad Wasif and Stephen McBean "Death Folk" songs written from an exploration into detunings on acoustic guitars. A full-length album entitled Anarchic Breezes is slated for a 2012 release. The 10 song album was recorded in Los Angeles, CA at House of Pie & the Committee HQ by Stephen McBean and Rob Campanella and mixed by Mark Nevers in Nashville, TN at Beech House Studios.
