1933–34 La Liga
1933–34 La Liga season started on November 5, 1933, and finished on March 4, 1934. Athletic Bilbao reconquered the title three seasons after. Oviedo made its debut in La Liga and due to the expansion of the league to twelve teams, there were not any relegation at the end of the season.
