Bulb Records
Bulb Records is a Michigan-based record label that was formed in 1993 by Peter Larson (now of the two-piece metal rock band 25 Suaves), and James Magas (now known simply as Magas).The label had originally started putting out records by local Michigan avant-garde musical acts, but had later added extended its roster to nationally and internationally known artists.
