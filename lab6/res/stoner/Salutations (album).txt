Salutations (album)
Salutations is the eighth solo studio album by American musician Conor Oberst, released on March 17, 2017 on Nonesuch Records. Ten of the tracks originally appeared on Ruminations in acoustic form. Track listing All tracks are written by Conor Oberst, except as indicated.
