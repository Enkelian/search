KCWX
KCWX, virtual channel 2 (VHF digital channel 5), is a MyNetworkTV-affiliated television station licensed to Fredericksburg, Texas, United States. Although Fredericksburg is within the Austin DMA, the station is officially assigned by Nielsen to the larger San Antonio market, and its signal covers the San Antonio and Bexar County area. Owned by Austin-based Corridor Television, KCWX maintains offices on South Piedras Drive in northwest San Antonio, and its transmitter is located on the Gillespie–Kendall county line. On cable, the station is available on Charter Spectrum channel 4, and Grande Communications and AT&T U-verse channel 2. History As a UPN affiliate The station first signed on the air on August 3, 2000 as KBEJ. Although nowadays, owners of most TV/radio stations ask for a specific set of calls, the call sign was sequentially assigned within the May 1998 FCC callsign change report. It originally operated as the UPN affiliate for two markets,San Antonio and Austin–a rarity by modern standards.
