Live from Radio City Music Hall
Live from Radio City Music Hall is a double album by the British heavy metal group Heaven & Hell which was released in 2007. The set is a chronicle of the group's performance on 30 March 2007 at Radio City Music Hall in New York City. Live from Radio City Music Hall is also available as a DVD. The DVD was certified Gold by the RIAA on 5 October 2007 selling 100,000 units. It was released on Blu-ray Disc in 2011. Track listing All songs written by Ronnie James Dio, Tony Iommi, Geezer Butler and Bill Ward except where noted. The special 3-disc set for the US contains a DVD, tour program, backstage laminate, glossy photos of the band and other tour items available only through the Rhino Records website.
