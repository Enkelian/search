1943–44 La Liga
The 1943–44 La Liga was the 13th season since its establishment. Valencia achieved its second title. Team locations League table Results Relegation play-offs Match between Deportivo La Coruña and Constancia was played at Estadio Chamartín in Chamartín de la Rosa, while the other one was held at Camp de Les Corts, Barcelona.
