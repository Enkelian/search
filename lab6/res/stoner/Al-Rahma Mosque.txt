Al-Rahma Mosque
The Al-Rahma Mosque (Arabic: مسجد الرحمة‎) is a mosque located on Hatherley Street in Toxteth, Liverpool, England, which can accommodate between 2,000 and 2,500 people and serves as the main place of worship and focus point for Liverpool's Muslim population, estimated at 25,000 people. Today The current building is used primarily by the city's Yemenis Syrians and Somali populations who constitute the vast majority of Liverpool's Muslim population.
