Ultra Electronics
Ultra Electronics Holdings (LSE: ULE) is a sub-system and systems provider operating across defence, security, critical detection & control markets. Ultra is a British company serving the defence, security, critical detection & control markets. It is listed on the London Stock Exchange and is a constituent of the FTSE 250 Index. Ultra operates mainly as a Tier 3 (sub-system) and occasionally a Tier 2 (systems) provider in the maritime, C4ISTAR-EW (command, control, communications, computers, intelligence, surveillance, acquisition and reconnaisance - electronic warfare), military and commercial aerospace, nuclear and industrial sensors markets. The company's core markets are the 'five-eyes' nations: Australia, Canada, New Zealand, UK and USA. This gives them access to the largest and most sophisticated addressable defence budgets in the world. They work with the US Department of Defense (DoD), UK Ministry of Defence (MoD) and other aerospace, defence and critical infrastructure providers both directly and through prime contractors. The group has approximately 4,400 employees across the UK, North America and Australia. In January 2020, Ultra launched its new brand, including a new logo.
