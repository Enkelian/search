A Special Life
A Special Life is a studio album by British blues musician John Mayall. It was released on 30 May 2014 by Forty Below Records and it is his first studio album since 2009's Tough. Track listing Personnel John Mayall – vocals, guitars, harmonica, organ, piano Rocky Athas – guitars Greg Rzab – bass guitar, percussions Jay Davenport – drums C. J.
