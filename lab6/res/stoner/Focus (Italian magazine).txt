Focus (Italian magazine)
Focus is an Italian monthly popular science magazine published in Milan, Italy. History and profile Focus was established in 1992. The magazine is published monthly by Arnoldo Mondadori Editore. It includes articles about science, technology, history, health, and social issues.Since 1999 the magazine offers Focus Extra, an occasional special supplement. Jacopo Loredan is the editor of the monthly. Circulation The circulation of Focus was 748,000 copies in 2004. Between February 2006 and January 2007 the magazine sold 608,600 copies. Its circulation was 616,080 copies in 2007. The magazine had a circulation of 477,366 copies in 2010.
