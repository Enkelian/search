Birmingham Perry Barr (UK Parliament constituency)
Birmingham, Perry Barr is a constituency of part of the city of Birmingham represented in the House of Commons of the UK Parliament. Members of Parliament Boundaries The constituency covers a broad area of north-west Birmingham. 2010–present: The City of Birmingham wards of Handsworth Wood, Lozells and East Handsworth, Oscott, Perry Barr, Birchfield 1997–2010: The City of Birmingham wards of Handsworth, Oscott, Perry Barr, and Sandwell. 1983–1997: The City of Birmingham wards of Handsworth, Kingstanding, Oscott, and Perry Barr. 1974–1983: The County Borough of Birmingham wards of Kingstanding, Oscott, and Perry Barr. 1950–1974: The County Borough of Birmingham wards of Kingstanding and Perry Barr.
