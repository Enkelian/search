Radio 1's Live Lounge – Volume 5
Radio 1's Live Lounge – Volume 5 is a collection of live tracks played on Fearne Cotton's Radio 1 show. The album is the fifth in a series of Live Lounge albums. It consists of both covers and the bands' own songs. The album was released on 25 October 2010.
