Digimortal World Tour
The Digimortal World Tour was a concert tour headlined by American industrial metal band Fear Factory. It started on March 18, 2001 at Northampton, England and ended on December 18, 2001 at Auckland, New Zealand, making it the band's shortest headlining tour, lasting almost 9 months.
