Friend's Friend's Friend
Friend's Friend's Friend is the second album by the British art rock band Audience, released in 1970. It was originally intended to be produced by Shel Talmy; however, he dropped out, leading the band to produce it themselves. Track listing Unless noted, all tracks credited to Werth, Williams.
