Administrative divisions of Kosovo
Kosovo is administratively subdivided into districts (Albanian: rajone, Serbian: окрузи / okruzi). They are further subdivided into municipalities (Albanian: komuna, Serbian: општина / opština). The entities were created by UNMIK and based on the entities in the Autonomous Province of Kosovo and Metohija.
