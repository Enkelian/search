Kampfbereit
Kampfbereit is a live video album by Canadian electro-industrial band Front Line Assembly, recorded in 2011 and released on April 7, 2015, through MVD Visual. It was shot during the band's 2011 Improvised.Electronice.Device Tour at the Kinetik Festival in Montreal and a number of North American cities.
