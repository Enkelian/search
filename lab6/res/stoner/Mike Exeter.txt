Mike Exeter
Mike Exeter is an English sound engineer and record producer who came to prominence via his work with British rock bands Cradle of Filth, Judas Priest Black Sabbath and more.He is also known for his work with the British guitar player Tony Iommi, being his longstanding creative studio partner. In 2013 he received a Grammy for his work on the Black Sabbath album 13. == Discography ==
