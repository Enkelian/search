Uproar (Lil Wayne song)
"Uproar" is a song by American rapper Lil Wayne. The song features intro vocals and production from American record producer Swizz Beatz. Young Money Entertainment and Republic Records released it to rhythmic radio on October 5, 2018, as the lead single from Wayne's twelfth studio album, Tha Carter V. "Uproar" contains a sample of "Special Delivery", performed by G. Dep and P. Diddy. Music video The song's music video was released on October 18, 2018.
