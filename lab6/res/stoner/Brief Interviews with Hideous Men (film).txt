Brief Interviews with Hideous Men (film)
Brief Interviews with Hideous Men is a 2009 American comedy-drama film written, produced, and directed by John Krasinski, in his directorial debut based on a short story collection of the same name by David Foster Wallace. Plot Sara Quinn (Julianne Nicholson) copes with a recent breakup by interviewing men as part of her graduate studies.
