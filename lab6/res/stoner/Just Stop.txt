Just Stop
"Just Stop" is a song by the American heavy metal band Disturbed. The song was released on February 7, 2006 as the third single from their third studio album, Ten Thousand Fists. Track listing All tracks are written by Dan Donegan, Mike Wengren, David Draiman.
