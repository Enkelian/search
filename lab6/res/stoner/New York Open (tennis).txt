New York Open (tennis)
The New York Open is an ATP indoor hard court tennis tournament held in Uniondale, New York, United States. The event takes place at the Nassau Veterans Memorial Coliseum. The tournament was created in 2018 to replace the Memphis Open, which was canceled due to lack of sponsorship.
