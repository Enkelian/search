1999–2000 Czech Cup
The 1999–2000 Czech Cup was the seventh season of the annual football knock-out tournament of the Czech Republic. Winners Slovan Liberec qualified for the 2000–01 UEFA Cup. Teams Preliminary round 44 teams took part in the preliminary round. Round 1 74 teams entered the competition at this stage. Along with the 22 winners from the preliminary round, these teams played 48 matches to qualify for the second round. Round 2 Round 3 Round 4 Čelákovice were the only team from the fourth level to take part in round 4. Quarterfinals The quarterfinals were played on 12 April 2000.
