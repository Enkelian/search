Time to Die (Electric Wizard album)
Time to Die is the eighth studio album by English doom metal band Electric Wizard. It was released on 29 September 2014. It is their debut on Spinefarm, having left Rise Above. The album is the longest Electric Wizard studio album to date (excluding bonus tracks and the added silence at the end of Dopethrone).
