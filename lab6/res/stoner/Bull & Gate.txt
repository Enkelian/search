Bull & Gate
The Bull & Gate is a Grade II listed public house at 389 Kentish Town Road, Kentish Town, London.It was built in 1871.The pub had a long history as music venue, with bands such as Coldplay, Blur and Nirvana playing there towards the start of their careers. It ceased operations as a venue in 2013, after being sold by previous owners to the Young's pub chain. == References ==
