Whiskey Woman Tour
The Whiskey Woman Tour was a concert tour by British heavy metal band Judas Priest. It ran from 1 January 1972 until 9 January 1973 through England, Scotland, Wales and a date at the Isle of Man. Setlist "Spanish Castle Magic" (The Jimi Hendrix Experience cover) "Winter" "Voodoo Rag" "Never Satisfied" "Joey" "Victim of Changes" "Mind Conception" "Black Sheep of the Family" (Quatermass cover) "Holy Is the Man" "Caviar and Meths" Tour Dates The band toured with Budgie, Thin Lizzy and Trapeze sometimes being the opening act of their shows. == References ==
