Lake Medina Shores, Texas
Lake Medina Shores is a census-designated place in Bandera and Medina counties, Texas, United States. Its population was 1,235 as of the 2010 census. This was a new CDP for the 2010 census. Geography Lake Medina Shores is located at 29°38′17″N 98°59′19″W (29.637992, -98.991496). According to the United States Census Bureau, the CDP has a total area of 3.4 square miles (8.8 km2), of which, 3.1 square miles (8.0 km2) of it is land and 0.3 square miles (0.78 km2) is water. == References ==
