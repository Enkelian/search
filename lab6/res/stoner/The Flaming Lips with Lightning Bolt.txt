The Flaming Lips with Lightning Bolt
The Flaming Lips with Lightning Bolt is a collaborative EP by The Flaming Lips and Lightning Bolt, released in 2011. The four-track EP includes four tracks ranging in length from six to eleven minutes. The first two tracks are credited to The Flaming Lips with Lightning Bolt, while the latter two are credited to Lightning Bolt with The Flaming Lips.
