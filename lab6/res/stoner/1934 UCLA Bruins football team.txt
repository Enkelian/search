1934 UCLA Bruins football team
The 1934 UCLA Bruins football team was an American football team that represented the University of California, Los Angeles during the 1934 college football season. In their 10th year under head coach William H. Spaulding, the Bruins compiled a 7-3 record (2-3 conference) and finished in sixth place in the Pacific Coast Conference (PCC). Regular season Schedule The team's schedule consisted of 10 games, 5 in-conference, and 5 with teams outside the PCC. While the Bruins went undefeated versus non-conference opponents, they only managed a 2-3 record within the Pacific Coast Conference.
