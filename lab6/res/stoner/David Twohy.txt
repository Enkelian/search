David Twohy
David Neil Twohy ( TOO-ee; born October 18, 1955) is an American film director and screenwriter. Early life Twohy was born in Los Angeles County, California. He attended college at California State University, Long Beach, graduating with a degree in radio/television/film. Career His most notable filmmaking credits have been writing The Fugitive, starring Harrison Ford, and writing and directing Pitch Black and its sequels The Chronicles of Riddick and Riddick, all starring Vin Diesel.He has a cameo in Below as the British captain of the rescue ship.
