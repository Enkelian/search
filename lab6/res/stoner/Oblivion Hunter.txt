Oblivion Hunter
Oblivion Hunter is the first EP by the noise rock band Lightning Bolt. It was released in September 2012 by Load Records. The record was advertised as an EP, although it has the length of an average Lightning Bolt album. The EP is a collection of unreleased material recorded in 2008. Track listing All tracks are written by Lightning Bolt.
