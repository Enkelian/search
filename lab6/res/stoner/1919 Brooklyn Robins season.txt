1919 Brooklyn Robins season
The 1919 Brooklyn Robins finished the season in fifth place. Offseason February 1, 1919: Jake Daubert was traded by the Robins to the Cincinnati Reds for Tommy Griffith. Regular season Season standings Record vs. opponents Notable transactions April 14, 1919: Ed Konetchy was purchased by the Robins from the Boston Braves. April 18, 1919: Lee Magee was purchased by the Robins from the Cincinnati Reds. June 2, 1919: Lee Magee was traded by the Robins to the Chicago Cubs for Pete Kilduff.
