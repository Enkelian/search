From 2004 to 2006, Hynes was a member of the band Test Icicles, playing guitar, synth, and occasionally performing vocals. They released one full-length album in 2005. Hynes went on to release two solo studio albums as Lightspeed Champion and subsequently four more as Blood Orange, between 2008 and 2018. He has written, played or produced for artists such as Tinashe, Solange Knowles, Tei Shi, Sky Ferreira, FKA twigs, Haim, Florence and the Machine, Carly Rae Jepsen, Diana Vickers, The Chemical Brothers, Kylie Minogue, A$AP Rocky, Mac Miller, Blondie and Mariah Carey. Early life David Hynes was born and raised in Ilford, East London to a Guyanese mother and a Sierra Leonean father. He was educated at the Chadwell Heath Foundation School, now known as Chadwell Heath Academy.
