Loose Nut
Loose Nut is the fifth full-length album by American band Black Flag, released in 1985 on SST Records. Track listing All tracks are written by Greg Ginn, except where noted. Personnel Henry Rollins – lead vocals Greg Ginn – guitars Kira Roessler – bass, backing vocals Bill Stevenson – drums Raymond Pettibon – artwork Milo Aukerman – backing vocals on "Loose Nut" == References ==
