The Observer (Gladstone)
The Observer is a daily newspaper serving Gladstone, Queensland in Australia. The newspaper is owned by News Corp Australia and is published from Monday to Saturday.The Observer is circulated to Gladstone and the area from the west to Biloela and south to Agnes Water and The Town Of 1770. The circulation of The Observer is 7,171 Monday to Friday and 9,701 on Saturday.The Observer website is part of News Corp Australia's News Regional Media network.
