1913 Brooklyn Dodgers season
The 1913 team saw the team named shortened to the Brooklyn Dodgers and the team moved into the new stadium at Ebbets Field. Jake Daubert won the Chalmers Award as the leagues Most Valuable Player but the team finished only in sixth place. Offseason November 1912: Elmer Brown was purchased by the Dodgers from the St. Louis Browns.
