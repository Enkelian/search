Soft Cushions
Soft Cushions is a 1927 American comedy film directed by Edward F. Cline and featuring Boris Karloff. It is a comic take by actor and producer Douglas MacLean on the 1911 Kismet play and 1920 silent film. It's listed as being lost by Arne Andersen's Lost Film Files website.
