1962 in film
The year 1962 in film involved some very significant events, with Lawrence of Arabia the year's top-grossing film as well as winner of the Academy Award for Best Picture. August 5 – Hollywood legend Marilyn Monroe is found dead of a drug overdose. September 7 – Filming of Sergei Bondarchuk's War and Peace begins and will continue for another 5 years. October 5 – Dr. No launches the James Bond series, the second longest-running film franchise of all time (next to Godzilla), still running more than 50 years later (Spectre, 2015). It also launches the career of Sean Connery.
