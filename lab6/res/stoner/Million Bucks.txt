Million Bucks
"Million Bucks" is a song by American hip hop recording artist Maino, taken from his debut studio album, If Tomorrow Comes.... The song, released July 3, 2009, serves as the third single and features vocals and production from Swizz Beatz. Background When asked about the record Maino said: Music video A music video was shot in Brooklyn, New York with director Edwin Decena. Rappers Red Cafe, Kayto, Lil Mama, Uncle Murda & Grafh made cameo appearances. A Behind the scenes preview was released on June 15, 2009. The video was released July 9 on Maino's Twitter account.
