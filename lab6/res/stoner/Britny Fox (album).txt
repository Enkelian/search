Britny Fox (album)
Britny Fox is the debut album by the American glam metal band Britny Fox, released in 1988. The album contains the hits "Long Way to Love", "Girlschool" and the power ballad "Save the Weak". Track listing All tracks are written by Dean Davidson, except where indicated.
