Holy Diver – Live
Holy Diver – Live is a live album by the heavy metal band Dio recorded on tour in 2005. On Disc 1, the band performs Holy Diver in its entirety, while disc 2 contains live versions of other songs from Dio's back catalog. It was released in, CD, DVD and blu-ray formats.
