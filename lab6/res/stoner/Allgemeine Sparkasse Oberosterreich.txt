Allgemeine Sparkasse Oberosterreich
Allgemeine Sparkasse Oberosterreich is a bank based in Austria. Its head office is situated at Linz, Austria. In August 2015, the bank acted as one of the lead managers in the $613.5 million loan securing by the Türk Ekonomi Bankası in an international syndicate of 32 more banks.
