1999–2000 Liechtenstein Cup
The 1999–2000 Liechtenstein Cup was the fifty-fifth season of Liechtenstein's annual cup competition. Seven clubs competed with a total of fifteen teams for one spot in the qualifying round of the UEFA Cup. Defending champions were FC Vaduz, who have won the cup continuously since 1998.
