Canton of Angers-7
The canton of Angers-7 is an administrative division of the Maine-et-Loire department, in western France. It was created at the French canton reorganisation which came into effect in March 2015. Its seat is in Angers.It consists of the following communes: Angers (partly) Loire-Authion La Ménitré Le Plessis-Grammoire Sarrigné Trélazé == References ==
