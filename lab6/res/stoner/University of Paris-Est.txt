University of Paris-Est
The University of Paris-Est (Université Paris-Est) is an association of universities and higher education institutions (ComUE) federating two universities and other institutions of higher education and research in the Paris-Est (eastern Paris) region. Originally organized as a Pôle de recherche et d'enseignement supérieur (PRES) in March 2007, the founding members were University of Marne-la-Vallée and École Nationale des Ponts et Chaussées.In application of the Law on Higher Education and Research (France), the University of Paris-Est became an association of universities and higher education institutions (ComUE) on March 1, 2015.
