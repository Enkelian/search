Bordeaux Montaigne University
Bordeaux Montaigne University (French: Université Bordeaux Montaigne) is a French university, based in Bordeaux (Gironde). It is under the Academy of Bordeaux. From 1 January 2014, the university of Bordeaux were reunited, except for "Bordeaux 3]" (Bordeaux Montaigne University) which chose not to take part to the merger.
