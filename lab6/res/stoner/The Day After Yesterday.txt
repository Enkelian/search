The Day After Yesterday
The Day After Yesterday is the thirteenth studio album by Australian musician, singer-songwriter, and actor Rick Springfield, released in 2005. It spent a single week on Billboard's album chart, peaking at #197. The album is composed largely of covers of his favorite songs written by other contemporary artists.
