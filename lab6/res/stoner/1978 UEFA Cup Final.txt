1978 UEFA Cup Final
The 1978 UEFA Cup Final was a football match played on 26 April 1978 and 9 May 1978 between PSV Eindhoven of Netherlands and Bastia of France. PSV Eindhoven won the tie 3–0 on aggregate, with a 3–0 victory at home following a goalless draw in Bastia.
