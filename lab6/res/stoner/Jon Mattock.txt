Jon Mattock
Jonny Mattock is a drummer and percussionist from Northampton who was a member of, or played with, Massive Attack, Spacemen 3, Spiritualized, The Perfect Disaster, Slipstream, Lupine Howl, Cranes, Baxter Dury, The Breeders, The Jazz Butcher, Honey Tongue, Josephine Wiggs Experience, Freelovebabies.He is currently a tutor at Northampton College, working with Music Practice and Music Production students.
