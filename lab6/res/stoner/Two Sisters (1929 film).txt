Two Sisters (1929 film)
Two Sisters is a 1929 American drama film directed by Scott Pembroke and featuring Boris Karloff. The film is one of the last produced in the sound-on-film process Phonofilm. The film is now considered to be lost. Cast Viola Dana as Jean / Jane Rex Lease as Allan Rhodes Claire Du Brey as Rose Thomas G.
