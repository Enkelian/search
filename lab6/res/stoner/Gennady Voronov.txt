Gennady Voronov
Gennady Ivanovich Voronov (Russian: Генна́дий Ива́нович Во́ронов; Rameshki, Tver Governorate, 31 August [O.S. 18 August] 1910 – Moscow, 1 April 1994) was a Soviet-Russian statesman who was from 1962 to 1971 the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Russian SFSR, literally meaning Premier or Prime Minister. Voronov was awarded two Orders of Lenin.
