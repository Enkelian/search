Power of the Damager
Power of the Damager is the seventh studio album by the metal band Prong. It was released through 13th Planet Records on October 2, 2007. The album peaked at #47 on the Billboard Top Heatseekers chart. Track listing All tracks are written by Tommy Victor, except where noted.
