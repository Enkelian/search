Crisis States Research Centre
The Crisis States Research Centre is based within the Development Studies Institute (DESTIN) of the London School of Economics and is funded by a grant from the UK Department for International Development (DFID). Its research concerns the management of conflict. See also Fragile state Failed state References [1] Putzel, J.
