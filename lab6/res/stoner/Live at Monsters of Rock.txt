Live at Monsters of Rock
Live at Monsters of Rock is a live album and DVD Northern Irish blues guitarist and singer Gary Moore. It was recorded live on May 21, 2003 at Sheffield Hallam Arena in England, during Gary Moore's appearances on the 2003 Monsters of Rock tour. The DVD, released in 2003 by Sanctuary Visual Entertainment, contains no overdubs and includes a bonus sound check and bonus interview footage of Gary Moore.
