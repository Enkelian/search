April Skies
"April Skies" is a song by the Scottish alternative rock group The Jesus and Mary Chain and the first single from the group's album Darklands. It was released by Blanco y Negro Records in April 1987. Chart performance The single reached #8 in the UK single charts, making it their highest charting single within the UK.
