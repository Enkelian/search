José Prat (politician)
José Diosdado Prat García (10 August 1905 – 17 May 1994) was a Spanish politician and lawyer from 1 March 1979 to 17 November 1982. He was from Partido Socialista Obrero Español. He was deputy of Madrid on 17 May 1994 and deputy of Albacete on 10 August 1905. He also served as the 46th Solicitor General of Spain. He died on 17 May 1994 due a illness.
