1999–2000 Austrian Cup
The 1999–2000 Austrian Cup (German: ÖFB-Cup) was the 66th season of Austria's nationwide football cup competition. It commenced with the matches of the Preliminary Round in July 1999 and concluded with the Final on 16 May 2000. The competition was won by Grazer AK after beating Austria Salzburg 4–3 on penalties and hence qualifying for the 2000-01 UEFA Cup.
