We're All Alright!
We're All Alright! is the eighteenth studio album by American rock band Cheap Trick. It was released on June 16, 2017. The album's title refers to a line from the band's 1978 hit, "Surrender", as well as the theme song to the television series That '70s Show, which the band performed. Singles The album's lead single, "Long Time Coming", was released on April 27, 2017.
