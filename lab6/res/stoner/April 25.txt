April 25
April 25 is the 115th day of the year (116th in leap years) in the Gregorian calendar. 250 days remain until the end of the year. Events 404 BC – Admiral Lysander and King Pausanias of Sparta blockade Athens and bring the Peloponnesian War to a successful conclusion. 775 – The Battle of Bagrevand puts an end to an Armenian rebellion against the Abbasid Caliphate.
