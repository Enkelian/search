Lynyrd Skynyrd discography
Lynyrd Skynyrd is an American band formed in 1964 that was named after the group's mutual high school gym teacher Leonard Skinner. The band has released many studio, live, and other albums, along with several singles and video discs. The band has sold 28 million albums in the US since 1991 when Nielsen SoundScan started tracking sales, not including album sales for the band's first 17 years.
