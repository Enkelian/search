1922 Brooklyn Robins season
The 1922 Brooklyn Robins struggled all season, finishing in sixth place. Offseason December 1921: Ferdie Schupp was purchased from the Robins by the Chicago White Sox. January 24, 1922: Sam Crane was purchased by the Robins from the Cincinnati Reds. March 14, 1922: Possum Whitted was purchased by the Robins from the Pittsburgh Pirates.
