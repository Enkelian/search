I'm in Your Mind Fuzz
I'm In Your Mind Fuzz is the fifth studio album by Australian psychedelic rock band King Gizzard & the Lizard Wizard. It was released on 31 October 2014 on Heavenly Records. Music The album has been described as garage rock and psychedelic rock. Track listing Vinyl releases have tracks 1–6 on side A, and tracks 7–10 on side B. All tracks are written by Stu Mackenzie alone, except where otherwise noted.
