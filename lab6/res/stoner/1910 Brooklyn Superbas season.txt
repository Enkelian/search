1910 Brooklyn Superbas season
The 1910 Brooklyn Superbas hired Bill Dahlen as the new manager, but still finished in a dismal sixth place in the National League. Regular season Season standings Record vs. opponents Notable transactions April 13, 1910: Harry McIntire was traded by the Superbas to the Chicago Cubs for Bill Davidson, Tony Smith and Happy Smith.
