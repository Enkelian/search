The Agenda with Tom Bradby
The Agenda is a political discussion programme on British television network ITV, broadcast on Monday evenings from 10.45pm. The programme is presented by ITV News anchor Tom Bradby and was first broadcast on 27 February 2012. In each episode, Bradby is joined by four guests to discuss the biggest news stories of the previous week and then talk about the week ahead.
