Moving on the Edges of Things
Moving on the Edges of Things is an EP by the American band This Will Destroy You. It was released on August 10, 2010 through Magic Bullet Records, although it was available on the label's website from July. It was released on 12" vinyl, with the first, and currently only, pressing limited to 1000 copies. Track listing All tracks are written by This Will Destroy You.
