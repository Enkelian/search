Toonami (France)
Toonami is a television channel that launched in France on 11 February 2016. It is operated and distributed in France by AT&T through its WarnerMedia subsidiary. It is named after the late Saturday night programming block seen in the United States, using the logo introduced in 2004 but with different branded promos, also using in Asia. On 3 February, it was confirmed by Toonami Squad in an Email with Turner that Toonami would be launching in France on 11 February 2016. == References ==
