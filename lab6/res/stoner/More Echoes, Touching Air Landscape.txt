More Echoes, Touching Air Landscape
More Echoes, Touching Air Landscape is a split album by Japanese bands Boris and Choukoku no Niwa. This release originally appeared in 1999, but was re-released in 2006 on Inoxia Records with different packaging. Listed as one song but split into two tracks, "Kanau" begins with 15 minutes of drone exploration, then explodes into a punk/drone/psychedelic style prominently featured on Amplifier Worship.
