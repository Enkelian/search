SV Horn
SV Horn is an Austrian association football club playing in the city of Horn. The club plays in the Austrian Second League. The team was champion of the Landesliga Niederösterreich in 1991, 1998 and 2007. In 2008, SV Horn won the Austrian Cup by beating SV Feldkirchen in the final. The club lost the unofficial Austrian Supercup match 2008 against SK Rapid Wien 1–7. On June 8, 2015, Keisuke Honda's management company, "Honda ESTILO" owned by his two brothers Hiroyuki and Youji, bought 49% of the shares in the club.
