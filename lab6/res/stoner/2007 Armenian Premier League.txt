2007 Armenian Premier League
The 2007 Armenian Premier League season is the sixteenth since its establishment, and started on 14 April 2007. The last matches were played on 10 November 2007. FC Pyunik were the defending champions. Participating teams Lernayin Artsakh are promoted. FC Mika are relocated from Ashtarak to Yerevan.
