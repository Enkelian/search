In2ition (album)
In2ition is the second studio album by Croatian cello duo, 2Cellos. It was released on November 9, 2012 in Japan, and January 15, 2013 in the rest of the world, through Sony Masterworks. The album peaked at number 1 on the Croatian and Japanese Albums charts.
