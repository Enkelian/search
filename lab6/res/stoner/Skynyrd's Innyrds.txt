Skynyrd's Innyrds
Skynyrd's Innyrds: Their Greatest Hits is a Lynyrd Skynyrd greatest hits album, released in 1989. The tracks were recorded between 1973 and April 1977. The album was certified 5x platinum in July 2001 by the RIAA.
