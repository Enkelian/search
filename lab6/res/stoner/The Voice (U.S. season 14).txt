The Voice (U.S. season 14)
The fourteenth season of the American reality The Voice premiered on February 26, 2018, on NBC with Blake Shelton and Adam Levine returning as coaches once again, making it their fourteenth season on the show. Returning coach Alicia Keys returned to the panel for her third season and New coach Kelly Clarkson joined the coaching panel.On May 22, 2018, Brynn Cartelli was crowned the winner of The Voice. With her win, the fifteen-year-old became the youngest winner in the show's history. Sawyer Fredericks and Danielle Bradbery at sixteen were the youngest previously. With her victory, Kelly Clarkson became the first new coach to win on her first season, and overall, the third female winning coach, behind Alicia Keys and Christina Aguilera. Additionally, runner-up Britton Buchanan became the highest-placing artist who advanced via an Instant Save, following Joshua Davis of season eight and Chris Jamison of season seven, who both placed third. Coaches and hosts The coaching lineup changed once again for the fourteenth season. Adam Levine and Blake Shelton returned as coaches, making them the only members of the coaching panel to be part of all fourteen seasons.
