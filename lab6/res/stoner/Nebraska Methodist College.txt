Nebraska Methodist College
Nebraska Methodist College of Nursing and Allied Health is a private Methodist college in Omaha, Nebraska that focuses on degrees in healthcare. Academics The institution offers associate's, bachelor's, master's and doctoral degrees in nursing and allied health professions. Accreditation Nebraska Methodist College is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission of the North Central Association of Colleges and Schools. Some of the school's programs are accredited through the Commission on Accreditation of Allied Health Education Programs, the American Association of Medical Assistants and the American Registry of Radiologic Technologists.
