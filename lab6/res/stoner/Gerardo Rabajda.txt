Gerardo Rabajda
Gerardo Rabajda (born 13 August 1967 in Montevideo) is a former Uruguayan football goalkeeper. He played for clubs Peñarol, Unión Española, Puebla FC, Sevilla FC, Rosario Central Danubio F.C., and Centro Atlético Fénix. External links http://elsevilla.com/articles/29514-que-fue-de-gerardo-rabajda
