Picture This – The Essential Blondie Collection
Picture This – The Essential Blondie Collection is a compilation album of recordings by the band Blondie released by EMI in 1998. It contains a selection of album tracks, singles, one b-side, one demo and two remixes taken from 1995 remix compilation Beautiful: The Remix Album.
