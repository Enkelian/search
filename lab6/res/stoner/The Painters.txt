The Painters
The Painters is the eighth EP by American experimental pop band Animal Collective, released on February 17, 2017. It is the first extended play released by the band since Monkey Been to Burn Town (2013). The EP serves as a companion to their 2016 studio album, Painting With. Reception The Independent rated the album 4 stars, calling it "possibly the perfect Animal Collective release".
