Hellenic Space Agency
The Hellenic Space Agency (Greek: Ελληνικός Διαστημικός Οργανισμός), abbreviated as the HSA (Greek: ΕΛΔΟ, ELDO), was the national space agency of Greece. It was founded at 19 March 2018 as a public limited company by the Ministry of Digital Policy, Telecommunications and Media.It was dissolved at 9 August 2019, when it was replaced by the Hellenic Space Center (Greek: Ελληνικό Κέντρο Διαστήματος). Purpose Its purpose was to shape the country's space strategy, to promote the participation of Greece in space programs, and to participate in space events. == References ==
