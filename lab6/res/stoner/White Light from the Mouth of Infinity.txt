White Light from the Mouth of Infinity
White Light from the Mouth of Infinity is the seventh studio album by American experimental rock band Swans. It was released in 1991, through the record label Young God. In 2015, it was remastered and made available again digitally and in a 3CD (or 3LP) set along with Love of Life and an album of bonus tracks. Critical reception White Light from the Mouth of Infinity has generally received positive reviews. AllMusic described the album as "stunning" and a "clear starting point for the second half of Swans' unique career". Track listing All tracks are written by Michael Gira.
