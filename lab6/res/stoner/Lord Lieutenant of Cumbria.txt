Lord Lieutenant of Cumbria
This is a list of those who have held the position of Lord Lieutenant of Cumbria: Cumbria was formed on 1 April 1974 by combining Cumberland and Westmorland and some other areas. See Lord Lieutenant of Cumberland and Lord Lieutenant of Westmorland for Lord Lieutenants of those counties before 1974.
