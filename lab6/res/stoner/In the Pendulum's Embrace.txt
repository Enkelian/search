In the Pendulum's Embrace
In the Pendulum's Embrace is an album by the Australian ambient musician Oren Ambarchi. It was released by Touch Music on CD in 2007, and by Southern Lord Records in 2008 on 2LP. It was recorded at BJB Studios in Sydney. Track listing "Fever, A Warm Poison" - 17:54 "Inamorata" - 10:19 "Trailing Moss in Mystic Glow" - 12:36 References External links Southern Lord Records
