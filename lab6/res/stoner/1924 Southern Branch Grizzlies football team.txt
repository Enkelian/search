1924 Southern Branch Grizzlies football team
The 1924 Southern Branch Grizzlies football team was an American football team that represented the Southern Branch of the University of California during the 1924 college football season. In their second year under head coach James J. Cline, the Grizzlies compiled a 0–5–3 record and were outscored by their opponents by a combined total of 109 to 40. Schedule == References ==
