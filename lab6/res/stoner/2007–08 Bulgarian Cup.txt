2007–08 Bulgarian Cup
The 2007–08 Bulgarian Cup was the 26th official edition of the Bulgarian annual football tournament. The final match between PFC Litex Lovech and PFC Cherno More Varna was held on 14 May 2008 at Vasil Levski National Stadium in Sofia. Litex won their third Bulgarian Cup in their history after winning the match 1-0 thanks to a second-half goal from Stanislav Manolev. First round In this round entered winners from the preliminary rounds together with the teams from B Group. Second round This round featured winners from the First Round and all teams from A Group.
