Evening Star (Judas Priest song)
"Evening Star" is a song by British heavy metal band Judas Priest, originally released on their 1978 album Killing Machine, and released as a single in May 1979. Following the success of the previous single "Take on the World", it again charted in the UK but it only reached No. 53. Personnel Rob Halford – vocals K. K.
