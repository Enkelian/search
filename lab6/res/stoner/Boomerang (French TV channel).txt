Boomerang (French TV channel)
Boomerang is a French-English television channel broadcasting programmes to children in France and Belgium. The channel was launched on 23 April 2003. The channel is owned by Turner Broadcasting System France. Current Programming Baby Looney Tunes Be Cool, Scooby-Doo!
