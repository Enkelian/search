Southside Independent School District
Southside Independent School District is a public school district located in southern Bexar County, Texas (USA). It serves the far south side of the city of San Antonio. In 2009, the school district was rated "academically acceptable" by the Texas Education Agency. Origins Southside ISD began as a $1 sale of land to San Antonio from Julian C.
