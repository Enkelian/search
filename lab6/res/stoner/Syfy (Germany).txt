Syfy (Germany)
See Sci Fi Channel for a list of other Sci Fi channels.Syfy is a German pay television channel, launched on 1 September 2003. It was the third Sci Fi Channel to be launched, following the US and U.K. versions. From 2003 until 2010, it was known as the Sci-Fi channel.
