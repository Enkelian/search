Onwards to the Wall
Onwards to the Wall is an EP by the Brooklyn-based noise rock band A Place to Bury Strangers, released on February 7, 2012 on the Dead Oceans label. Reception According to Metacritic, Onwards to the Wall has an average score of 75 out of 100, indicating that it has received generally favorable reviews from critics.
