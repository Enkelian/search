Earthology Records
Earthology Records is the record label of Cloud Cult's Craig Minowa and Connie Minowa formed in 1997 on their organic farm The label's buildings are powered by geothermal energy and were partially constructed with reclaimed wood and recycled plastic. It is also a non-profit label that only uses recycled materials for its CDs and donates all its profits to charity. References Further reading Martens, Todd (2007-06-23). "Down to Earth". Billboard. 119 (25): 29.
