Airdrie & Coatbridge Advertiser
The Airdrie & Coatbridge Advertiser is a tabloid newspaper published by Scottish and Universal Newspapers, a division of Reach plc, in Airdrie and Coatbridge, North Lanarkshire. The Airdrie & Coatbridge Advertiser is a weekly newspaper, printed every Wednesday, the newspaper printed in Blantyre. The paper dates from 1855.
