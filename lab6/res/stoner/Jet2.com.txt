Jet2.com
Jet2.com Limited, often known simply as Jet2, is a British low-cost leisure airline offering scheduled and charter flights from the United Kingdom. As of 2018, it is the third-largest scheduled airline in the UK, behind EasyJet and British Airways. Its main base and headquarters is located at Leeds Bradford Airport, with further bases at Alicante-Elche, Belfast–International, East Midlands, Edinburgh, Glasgow, London–Stansted, Manchester, Newcastle, Birmingham, Palma de Mallorca and Tenerife–South. The company holds a United Kingdom Civil Aviation Authority Type A Operating Licence to carry passengers, cargo and mail on aircraft with 20 or more seats. Jet2.com also offers a charter service through its Jet2charters brand. Subsidiary company Jet2holidays offers ABTA and ATOL-protected package holidays with accommodation, transfers and Jet2.com flights to 82 sun, city and ski destinations from its nine UK bases. Jet2.com and Jet2holidays has around 13,000 permanent and seasonal employees as of Summer 2020. History Origins Jet2.com Limited is a subsidiary of the Dart Group, an aviation services and distribution group. The airline began operating commercial aircraft in 1983 under the name Channel Express, when it was purchased by the Dart Group's current Executive Chairman, Philip Meeson.
