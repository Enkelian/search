Loving You Is Sweeter Than Ever
"Loving You Is Sweeter Than Ever" is a 1966 song written by Ivy Jo Hunter and Stevie Wonder and performed by the Four Tops, on the Motown label. In addition to co-writing the song, Wonder also instrumentally contributed drums to the track. Credits Lead vocals by Levi Stubbs Background vocals by Abdul "Duke" Fakir, Renaldo "Obie" Benson, and Lawrence Payton Instrumentation by The Funk Brothers Drums by Stevie Wonder Chart Performance The song peaked at #12 on the Billboard,Hot Rhythm & Blues Singles chart and #45 on the Hot 100 singles charts. Cover versions Nick Kamen released his version in 1987, where it went to number one in Italy, and #16 in the UK singles chartThe Band released their version in 1989 on their anthology album - To Kingdom Come: The Definitive Collection.
