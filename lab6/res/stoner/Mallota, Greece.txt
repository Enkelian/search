Mallota, Greece
Mallota (Greek: Μαλλωτά) is a village in the municipality of Megalopoli, Arcadia, Greece. It is situated on a hillside, 1 km northeast of Rapsommati, 2 km west of Palaiochouni and 5 km east of Megalopoli. Mallota had a population of 46 in 2011.
