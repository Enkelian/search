Harvard Park, Los Angeles
Harvard Park is a 0.64-square-mile neighborhood in the South Los Angeles region of Los Angeles, California. It is centered on a municipal park by that name. Its population has grown to 10,888, the city estimated in 2008.The median age was 28, young for the city and the county, and the percentages of residents younger than age 18 were among the county's highest.
