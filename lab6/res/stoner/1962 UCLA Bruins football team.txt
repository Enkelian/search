1962 UCLA Bruins football team
The 1962 UCLA Bruins football team was an American football team that represented the University of California, Los Angeles during the 1962 NCAA University Division football season. In their fifth year under head coach Bill Barnes, the Bruins compiled a 4–6 record (1–3 AAWU) and finished in fifth place in the Athletic Association of Western Universities.UCLA's offensive leaders in 1962 were quarterback Larry Zeno with 458 passing yards, Kermit Alexander with 472 rushing yards, and Mel Profit with 229 receiving yards.
