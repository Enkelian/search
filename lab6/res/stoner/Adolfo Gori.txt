Adolfo Gori
Adolfo Gori (Italian pronunciation: [aˈdolfo ˈɡɔːri]; born 13 February 1939) is a retired Italian professional footballer who played as a defender. Club career Rochester Lancers In 1972, Gori became a player-coach for the Rochester Lancers of the North American Soccer League. International career Gori had his first and only appearance for the Italian national team on 25 June 1967 in a 1–0 win against Romania. Honours JuventusSerie A champion: 1966–67.
