The Essential Michael Bolton
The Essential Michael Bolton is a two-disc compilation album by American singer Michael Bolton, released on April 25, 2005. It is a repacked version of Michael Bolton's 2002 album The Ultimate Collection. In 2005, a DVD was released with the album containing all 32 music videos. The album only charted in Denmark.
