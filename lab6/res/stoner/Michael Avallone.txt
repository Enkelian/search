His lifetime output was over 223 works (although he boasted over 1,000), published under his own name and seventeen pseudonyms. Biography The son of Michael Angelo Avallone, Sr, Avallone was born in New York City on (1924-10-27)October 27, 1924 and died in Los Angeles on (1999-02-26)February 26, 1999. He was married in 1949 to Lucille Asero; they had one son before the marriage was dissolved. In 1960 he married Fran Weinstein, and together they had one son and one daughter. In addition to his writing, Avallone was a guest lecturer at New York University, Columbia University, and Rutgers University.
