Erin Mathews
Erin Mathews is a Canadian voice actress known as the voice of Pac-Man in the animated series Pac-Man and the Ghostly Adventures and Coop in Kid vs. Kat. She graduated from the Studio 58 theater program. She has worked in theaters across Canada, and is a founding member of the Theatre Melee collective.
