North Potomac, Maryland
North Potomac is a census-designated place and an unincorporated area in Montgomery County, Maryland, United States. It has a population of approximately 25,000 and a median household income of nearly $160,000. About 55 percent of the population speaks English at home, while nearly 27 percent speaks Asian/Pacific Islander. The community is located close to Shady Grove Hospital and the I-270 Technology Corridor, and is about 21 miles from Washington, D.C. History In 1970, the United States Census Bureau considered North Potomac an unincorporated place that was part of two county subdivisions. North Potomac had been part of the Darnestown and Travilah areas.
