New Song (Warpaint song)
"New Song" is a song by the American indie rock band Warpaint. It is the third track and lead single from the band's third studio album Heads Up, and was released on August 1, 2016 on Rough Trade Records.The single has been remixed by the band Soulwax and Mike D of the Beastie Boys. Reception "New Song" was generally well received by critics, but noted for being different from Warpaint's past material. NME described the single as "the poppiest thing [Warpaint has] ever done and probably the catchiest too". The Los Angeles Times called the single a "striking departure" from the band's previous work, but stated the song had "easy-to-memorize lyrics and a happy earworm of a melody". The Guardian called the song a "disco charmer".The band performed the song on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon.
