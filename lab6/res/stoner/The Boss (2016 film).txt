The Boss (2016 film)
The Boss is a 2016 American comedy film directed by Ben Falcone and written by Falcone, Melissa McCarthy and Steve Mallory. The film stars McCarthy, Kristen Bell, Ella Anderson, Tyler Labine, Kathy Bates, Annie Mumolo, Timothy Simons, and Peter Dinklage. The film follows a wealthy woman who, after being arrested and losing her fortune, uses her assistant's daughter's Dandelion Girls cookie sales to return to the top. The film had its premiere in Sydney on March 21, 2016, and was theatrically released in the United States on April 8, 2016, by Universal Pictures. Although It received generally negative reviews from critics , grossed $78 million worldwide. She is arrested by the FBI for insider trading, serves time, and is on parole throughout the film until the end.
