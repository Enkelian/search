Nicolas Pesce
Nicolas Pesce (born January 18, 1990) is an American filmmaker. He is best known for directing and writing the horror films The Eyes of My Mother (2016), Piercing (2018), and The Grudge (2020). Life and career Nicolas Pesce was born, and currently resides, in New York City. His theatrical debut, The Eyes of My Mother, premiered in the NEXT section at the 2016 Sundance Film Festival.
