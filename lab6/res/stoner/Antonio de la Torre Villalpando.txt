Antonio de la Torre Villalpando
Antonio de la Torre Villalpando (born 21 September 1951 in Mexico City) is a Mexican first division player, who played most of his career with Club América where he helped the club win the 1975-76 Primera División de México Cup along with 1978 CONCACAF Champions' Cup and the Copa Interamericana that same year. Antonio de la Torre was also part of the 1978 FIFA World Cup in Argentina. He played with the America 5 years which then after join Puebla F.C. where he won the 1982-83 First Division Title. After 2 years with puebla he decide to call it quits. He is the father of Antonio de la Torre García.
