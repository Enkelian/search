Baptist Health System School of Health Professions
The Baptist Health System School of Health Professions, established 1903, is an accredited professional health science school sponsored by the Baptist Health System. It is located at the South Texas Medical Center in San Antonio, Texas, US. Among the fields in which training is offered are specialties in Nursing and the Radiological Sciences.
