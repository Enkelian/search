Neighbor Singing
Neighbor Singing is the debut solo album of composer Brad Laner, released on November 6, 2007 through Hometapes. Although the Electric Company and Amnesia albums were primarily composed and performed by Laner, Neighbor Singing is the first album to be issued under his own name. Track listing All tracks are written by Brad Laner, except "Arlie" co-written with Alex Graham.
