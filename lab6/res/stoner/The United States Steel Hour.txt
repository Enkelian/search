The United States Steel Hour
The United States Steel Hour is an anthology series which brought hour long dramas to television from 1953 to 1963. The television series and the radio program that preceded it were both sponsored by the United States Steel Corporation (U. S. Steel). Theatre Guild on the Air The series originated on radio in the 1940s as Theatre Guild on the Air.
