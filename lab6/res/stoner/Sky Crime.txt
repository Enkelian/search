Sky Crime
Sky Crime is a British pay television channel owned and operated by Sky, a division of Comcast. The channel launched on 1 October 2019.The channel launched at 12:00 on 1 October 2019, replacing Real Lives. Sky Crime broadcasts shows from Oxygen, HBO, Jupiter Entertainment and Woodcut Media broadcasting crime dramas.
