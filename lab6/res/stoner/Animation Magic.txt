Animation Magic
Animation Magic (Russian: «Магия анимации», romanized: Magiya Animatsii) was a Russian-American animation company created in Gaithersburg, Maryland, with offices in Cambridge, Massachusetts and a 100% owned subsidiary located in St. Petersburg, Russia. It developed animations for CD-based software. In 1994 it had 90 employees, including 12 software engineers and approximately 60 animators, computer graphic, background and sprite artists. Its products include Link: The Faces of Evil, Zelda: The Wand of Gamelon, Hotel Mario, Mutant Rampage: Bodyslam, I.M. Meen, Chill Manor, Kings Quest VII and the cancelled Warcraft Adventures: Lord of the Clans. == References ==
