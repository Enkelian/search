Float Along – Fill Your Lungs
Float Along – Fill Your Lungs is the third studio album by Australian psychedelic rock band King Gizzard & the Lizard Wizard. It was released on 27 September 2013 on Flightless. It peaked at No. 12 on the ARIA Albums Chart after being re-released on vinyl in November 2018. Track listing Vinyl reissues have tracks 1–3 on side A, and tracks 4–8 on side B. All tracks are written by Stu Mackenzie, except where noted.
