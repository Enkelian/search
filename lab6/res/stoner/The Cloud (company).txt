The Cloud (company)
The Cloud is a provider of public access Wi-Fi hotspots in the UK and is a member of the Wireless Broadband Alliance. It has over 22,000 Wi-Fi hotspots network access points in the UK.The Cloud has offices in St Albans, Munich, and Stockholm.
