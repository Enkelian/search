Top Cat
Top Cat is an American animated sitcom produced by Hanna-Barbera Productions and originally broadcast in prime-time on the ABC network. The show aired 30 episodes in a weekly evening timeslot from September 27, 1961 to April 18, 1962. The show was a ratings failure in prime time, but found a receptive audience on Saturday morning children's programming. The show aired on Saturdays in 1962 and 1963 on ABC, and was then rerun in various Saturday morning slots on NBC from 1965 to 1969.
