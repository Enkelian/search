Out Stealing Horses (film)
Out Stealing Horses (Norwegian: Ut og stjæle hester) is a 2019 Norwegian drama film directed by Hans Petter Moland. It was selected to compete for the Golden Bear at the 69th Berlin International Film Festival. At Berlin, the film won the Silver Bear for Outstanding Artistic Contribution. It was selected as the Norwegian entry for the Best International Feature Film at the 92nd Academy Awards, but it was not nominated. Plot A 67-year-old recalls the summer of 1948, the year he turned fifteen. Cast Stellan Skarsgård Tobias Santelmann Danica Curcic Pål Sverre Hagen Anders Baasmo Christiansen Filming locations The film was shot in rural Norway, near the small town of Trysil, and also in Lithuania's nature parks, cities of Kaunas and Vilnius.
