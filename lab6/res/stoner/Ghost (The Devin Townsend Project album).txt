Ghost (The Devin Townsend Project album)
Ghost is the fourteenth studio album by Canadian musician Devin Townsend, and the fourth album in the Devin Townsend Project series. It was released on June 20, 2011, simultaneously with the third Devin Townsend Project album Deconstruction. Townsend has stated that the album "is the mellowest (by far) and prettiest record I've done so far".Ghost was performed in its entirety once, on November 13, 2011, at the Union Chapel in London, England.
