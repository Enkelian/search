UTSA Roadrunners football
The UTSA Roadrunners football program represents University of Texas at San Antonio (UTSA) in the sport of American football. The Roadrunners compete in the Football Bowl Subdivision (FBS) of the National Collegiate Athletic Association (NCAA) and the West Division of Conference USA (CUSA). They are coached by Jeff Traylor, who started in 2020. The Roadrunners play their home games at the Alamodome which has a seating capacity of 65,000.
