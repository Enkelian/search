La Sicilia
La Sicilia is an Italian daily newspaper for the island of Sicily. Published in Catania, it is the second best-selling newspaper in Sicily. History and profile La Sicilia was founded and first published in 1945, and legally registered at the court of Catania three years later. The paper had a conservative stance.The circulation of La Sicilia was 64,550 copies in 2008. Notes External links (in Italian) Official website
