Pack rat
A pack rat or packrat, also called a woodrat, can be any of the species in the rodent genus Neotoma. Pack rats have a rat-like appearance with long tails, large ears and large black eyes. Pack rats are noticeably larger than deer mice, harvest mice and grasshopper mice, and are usually somewhat larger than cotton rats.
