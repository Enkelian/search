No Rest for the Wicked Tour
The No Rest for the Wicked Tour was a tour by Ozzy Osbourne in 1988 and 1989, supporting his album No Rest for the Wicked. Overview Background Geezer Butler joined the band for the tour. Pre-tour North America (1st leg) Anthrax opened for the band on November 18, 1988. Asia leg Europe leg North America (final leg) White Lion and Vixen opened this leg.
