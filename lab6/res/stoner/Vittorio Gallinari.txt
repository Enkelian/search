Vittorio Gallinari
Vittorio Gallinari (born 22 October 1958) is an Italian former basketball player and current sports agent. He is the father of Danilo Gallinari, who plays for the Oklahoma City Thunder in the NBA. He was born in Sant'Angelo Lodigiano. During his playing career, Gallinari was known as a tough defensive player. He was also teammate of Houston Rockets coach Mike D'Antoni. == References ==
