Harry Sidhu
Harry S. Sidhu (born July 8, 1957) is an American Republican politician and businessman who is the 46th and current mayor of Anaheim, California, winning the office in the 2018 election. He is the first Sikh mayor of the city and the first person of color to serve as mayor of Anaheim. He is a former member of the Anaheim City Council and the former Mayor pro tempore of Anaheim. Sidhu earned a degree Mechanical Engineering from Drexel University. He worked as a consulting engineer. Sidhu served as director of Orange County Water District Board.
