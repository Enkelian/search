Tamil Nadu National Law University
Tamil Nadu National Law University (TNNLU), formerly Tamil Nadu National Law School (TNNLS), is a National Law University . Admissions to the university are done on the basis of the Common Law Admission Test. It was established in 2012 by the Government of Tamil Nadu through Tamil Nadu National Law School Act, 2012, with an initial contribution of ₹100 crore (equivalent to ₹152 crore or US$21 million in 2019).The Act was amended in the year 2018 and the word 'School' was replaced by the word ' University'.
