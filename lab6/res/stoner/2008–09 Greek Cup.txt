2008–09 Greek Cup
Greek Cup 2008–09 was the 67th edition of The Greek Football Cup, or Greek Cup for short. The competition started on 30 August 2008 with the First Round and concluded on 2 May 2009 with the Final, held at Athens Olympic Stadium "Spyridon Louis". Olympiacos claimed that season's Greek Cup with a 15–14 victory on penalties over AEK after a 4–4 draw.
