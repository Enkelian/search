Showdown (Electric Light Orchestra album)
Showdown is an Electric Light Orchestra (ELO) compilation album, covering their Harvest Records years. It is the first in a very long line of Electric Light Orchestra compilation albums. It was also the first time the hit single "Showdown" featured on an album in the UK. Track listing All songs written by Jeff Lynne, except where noted.
