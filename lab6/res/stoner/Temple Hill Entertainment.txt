Temple Hill Entertainment
Temple Hill Entertainment or Temple Hill Productions is an American film and television production company, established in February 2006 by producers Wyck Godfrey and Marty Bowen. The first film that the company produced was The Nativity Story in 2006 by director Catherine Hardwicke. The studio produced the Twilight film series.
