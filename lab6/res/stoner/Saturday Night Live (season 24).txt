Saturday Night Live (season 24)
The twenty-fourth season of Saturday Night Live, an American sketch comedy series, originally aired in the United States on NBC between September 26, 1998, and May 15, 1999. Cast Before the start of the season, both Jim Breuer and Norm Macdonald had left the show. After two years, the show hired three new cast members: stand-up comic Jimmy Fallon, Chris Parnell (of The Groundlings in Los Angeles) and Horatio Sanz of Second City in Chicago.
