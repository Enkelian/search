Gary Davies
Gary St John Aloitius Davies (born 13 December 1957, Manchester, England) is an English broadcaster. From 1982 to 1993 he was a BBC Radio 1 disc jockey and a regular presenter of Top of the Pops. As of 2018, he can be heard on BBC Radio 2 presenting a Friday night show Sounds of the 80s as well as standing in for O. J. Borg, Zoe Ball and Ken Bruce. Early career After working in marketing for a mail order company and managing a Manchester disco, Davies began his broadcasting career at Manchester's Piccadilly Radio in 1979 before joining BBC Radio 1 in 1982 to present a Saturday late night show, making his debut on 4 December 1982. Within weeks he was on the roster to present Top Of The Pops on BBC television alongside his Radio 1 colleagues. BBC Radio 1 The Bit in the Middle In 1984, Davies took over the Radio 1 lunchtime show.
