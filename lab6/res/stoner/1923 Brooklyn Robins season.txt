1923 Brooklyn Robins season
A poor season found the 1923 Brooklyn Robins in sixth place once more. Offseason January 2, 1923: Turner Barber was purchased by the Robins from the Chicago Cubs. February 11, 1923: Clarence Mitchell was traded by the Robins to the Philadelphia Phillies for George Smith. February 15, 1923: Hy Myers and Ray Schmandt were traded by the Robins to the St. Louis Cardinals for Jack Fournier.
