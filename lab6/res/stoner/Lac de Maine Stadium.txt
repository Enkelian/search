Lac de Maine Stadium
The Lac de Maine Stadium is a football stadium located at Angers in the district of Lac de Maine. Description The Lac de Maine stadium includes a number of facilities: Four football fields, A synthetic hockey field, A grassed rugby field, An athletic track 400 m with 8 lanes, A jumps runway (for track) with 2 landing pits. France 2009 championship athletics The Lac de Maine stadium hosted the athletics championships in France from July 23 to 25, 2009. 2014 athletics DecaNation The Lac de Maine stadium hosted the DecaNation August 30, 2014. == notes and references ==
