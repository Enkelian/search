Miss World 2005 Ingrid represented Puerto Rico at Miss World 2005 where she eventually placed 2nd runner-up. She also won the title of Miss World Caribbean. Miss Puerto Rico Universe 2008 On November 23, 2007, Rivera represented Dorado at Miss Puerto Rico Universe 2008. Rivera advanced to the five semifinalist phase, along participants representing Isabela, Utuado, Trujillo Alto and Santurce. As part of a "final question" segment she was asked "With what social labor do you identify yourself and why?" to which she responded that she supported women that are victims of domestic abuse.
