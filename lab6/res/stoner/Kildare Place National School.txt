Kildare Place National School
Kildare Place National School (KPNS) is a Church of Ireland primary school (a national school) in Rathmines, a suburb of Dublin, Ireland. The school is linked to the training college of the Church of Ireland College of Education. Formerly located on Kildare Street in Dublin's City Centre, the school moved to Upper Rathmines Road in 1969. Notable alumni Jonathan Sexton – Leinster and Ireland rugby player Kevin McLaughlin – Former Leinster and Ireland rugby player David McMillan – League of Ireland footballer with University College Dublin A.F.C.
