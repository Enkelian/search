Coca-Cola Museum
The Coca-Cola Museum (Chinese: 可口可樂博物館; pinyin: Kěkǒukělè Bówùguǎn) is a museum about the drink Coca-Cola, located on the Gueishan Industrial Park in Taoyuan District, Taoyuan City, Taiwan.The facility is run by Swire Group, which has an adjacent Coca-Cola bottling plant. The museum building is colored red, like Coca-Cola. Transportation The museum is accessible within walking distance South East from Taoyuan Station of the Taiwan Railways.
