2000 Commonwealth Youth Games
The 2000 Commonwealth Youth Games were held in August 2000, in the Scottish capital of Edinburgh. Sports The following sports were included in the 2000 games: Athletics () Fencing () Hockey () Squash () Swimming () Tennis () Weightlifting () Medal count This is the full table of the medal count of these Games. These rankings sort by the number of gold medals earned by a country. The number of silvers is taken into consideration next and then the number of bronze. If, after the above, countries are still tied, equal ranking is given and they are listed alphabetically.
