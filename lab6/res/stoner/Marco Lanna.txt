Marco Lanna
Marco Lanna (Italian pronunciation: [ˈmarko ˈlanna]; born 13 July 1968) is an Italian former professional footballer who played at both club and international levels as a defender. Career Born in Genoa, Lanna played at club level for Sampdoria, Roma, Salamanca and Real Zaragoza, as well earning two caps for Italy between 1992 and 1993, including in one FIFA World Cup qualifier. == References ==
