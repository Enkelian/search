1959–60 La Liga
The 1959–60 La Liga was the 29th season since its establishment. The season started on September 13, 1959, and finished on April 17, 1960. Team locations League table Results Relegation play-offs Play-off between Córdoba and Real Sociedad was decided after a tie-break match, where Real Sociedad won 1–0.
