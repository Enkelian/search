Howard Bison and Lady Bison
The Howard Bison are the intercollegiate athletic teams representing Howard University in Washington, D.C. The Bison compete in the NCAA's Division I Football Championship Subdivision (FCS) and are members of the Mid-Eastern Athletic Conference for most sports. On July 16, 2015, the Athletics Department unveiled new logos. Varsity teams A member of the Mid-Eastern Athletic Conference (MEAC), Howard University currently sponsors teams in eight men's and eleven women's NCAA sanctioned sports.
