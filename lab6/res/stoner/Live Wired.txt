Live Wired
Live Wired is a live album by Vancouver industrial band Front Line Assembly, released in 1996. A box set including the 2CD plus a VHS with all of FLA's video clips to date (except "Mindphaser" and "Body Count") and a live concert video (from the show in Chemnitz 1995, different from the one on CD) was released at the same time.
