Cisco McSorley
Cisco A. McSorley is a Democratic member of the New Mexico Senate, representing the 16th District since 1997. Earlier he was a member of the New Mexico House of Representatives from 1984 through 1996. McSorley was born and grew up in Albuquerque, New Mexico and attended St. Pius X High School. He graduated from the University of New Mexico with a bachelor's degree in 1974 and from the University of New Mexico School of Law with a J.D. in 1979.
