Lynyrd Skynyrd Twenty Tour
Lynyrd Skynyrd Twenty Tour was a concert tour by Lynyrd Skynyrd in support of their album, Twenty. The band performed shows with a number of other artists including Foghat, Kansas, and Bruce Dickinson.
