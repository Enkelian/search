Manhattan Beach, California
Manhattan Beach is a very affluent city in southwestern Los Angeles County, California, United States, on the Pacific coast south of El Segundo, and north of Hermosa Beach. As of the 2010 census, the population was 35,135, and in 2018 the population was estimated to be 35,532.Manhattan Beach is one of the three Beach Cities, others known as Hermosa Beach and Redondo Beach, that make up the South Bay region of Los Angeles County. The community is known for a long beach stretching approximately 2.1 miles (3.4 km) and roughly 450 feet (140 m) wide. Its schools are run independently of Los Angeles Unified School District with a separate school board based in Manhattan Beach.
