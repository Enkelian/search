Bomb the Twist
Bomb the Twist is an e.p. that was released on January 11, 1996, by The 5.6.7.8's. "Woo Hoo" was featured in the 2003 film Kill Bill Volume 1, by Quentin Tarantino. The release was recorded for the US record label Sympathy for the Record Industry.
