1972 USC Trojans football team
The 1972 USC Trojans football team represented the University of Southern California during the 1972 NCAA University Division football season. Schedule Game summaries Arkansas Tailback Rod McNeill, who sat out the previous year with a hip injury, led the Trojans with 117 yards on 28 carries while Mike Rae, a backup for two seasons, completed 18 of 24 passes for 269 yards and in the second half completed nine straight passes at one point in his first start.
