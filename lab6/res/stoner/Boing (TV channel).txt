Boing (TV channel)
Boing is a brand name used by the Global Kids & Young Adults division of Warner Bros. Entertainment — all owned by WarnerMedia — for a collection of television networks outside of the United States that are targeted at children. As of 2019, Boing-branded channels exist in Italy (its flagship service), France, Spain, and Africa, while a weekend morning programming block currently airs on the WarnerMedia-owned Chilevisión in Chile.
