Sky Cinema (Italy)
Sky Cinema is an Italian set of premium movie channels owned by Sky Italia. The Sky Cinema package includes fourteen Sky-branded channels. Sky Cinema Uno HD; Sky Cinema Due HD, Sky Cinema Collection HD, Sky Cinema Family HD; Sky Cinema Action HD; Sky Cinema Suspense HD; Sky Cinema Romance HD; Sky Cinema Drama HD; Sky Cinema Comedy HD; Sky Cinema Uno +24 HD; Sky Cinema Due +24 HD Other Sky Cinema Hits re-branded to Sky Cinema Oscar from 17 February 2014 to 3 March 2014 to mark the 86th edition of the Oscar Academy Awards.
