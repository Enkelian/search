Chinchilla News and Murilla Advertiser
The Chinchilla News and Murilla Advertiser (or simply Chinchilla News) is a weekly newspaper published in Chinchilla, Queensland, Australia. History The newspaper was first published on 14 December 1907. The newspaper traditionally covers the Chinchilla, Miles, Tara and Taroom districts.
