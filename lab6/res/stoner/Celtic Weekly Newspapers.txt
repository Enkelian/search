Celtic Weekly Newspapers
Celtic Weekly Newspapers is a series of seven weekly newspapers published in south Wales by Media Wales Ltd (formerly Western Mail & Echo Ltd), part of Reach plc. The titles in the series are: Cynon Valley Leader Glamorgan Gazette Gwent Gazette Merthyr Express Pontypridd & Llantrisant Observer Rhondda Leader Rhymney Valley ExpressThe Neath Guardian - which also had a Port Talbot edition - was part of the series until its closure in October 2009.
