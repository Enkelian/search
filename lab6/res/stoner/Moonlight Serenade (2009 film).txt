Moonlight Serenade (2009 film)
Moonlight Serenade is a 2009 romance musical directed by Giancarlo Tallarico and starring Amy Adams. Plot summary A piano player discovers that the girl at the coat-check of a jazz club is a talented singer; she persuades him to form a musical act together.
