1955 UCLA Bruins football team
The 1955 UCLA Bruins football team was an American football team that represented the University of California, Los Angeles during the 1955 college football season. In their seventh year under head coach Red Sanders, the Bruins compiled a 9–2 record (6–0 conference) and finished in first place in the Pacific Coast Conference.The Nov. 12 game with Washington was referenced in the 1989 film, Back to the Future Part II.
