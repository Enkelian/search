SD Noja
Sociedad Deportiva Noja is a Spanish football team based in Noja, in the autonomous community of Cantabria. Founded in 1963 it plays in Segunda División B – Group 1, holding home games at Estadio La Caseta, with a capacity of 3,000 seats.
