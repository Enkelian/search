Canton of Angers-5
The canton of Angers-5 is an administrative division of the Maine-et-Loire department, in western France. It was created at the French canton reorganisation which came into effect in March 2015. Its seat is in Angers.It consists of the following communes: Angers (partly) Briollay Cantenay-Épinard Écouflant Écuillé Feneu Soulaire-et-Bourg == References ==
