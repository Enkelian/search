Jennifer Barrientos
Jennifer Tarol Barrientos (born January 10, 1986 in San Mateo, Rizal), was the Binibining Pilipinas Universe titleholder for year of 2008. She was the official representative of the Philippines to the Miss Universe pageant which took place in Nha Trang, Vietnam on July 14, 2008. She did not place in the Top Fifteen Semi-Finalists.
