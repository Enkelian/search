Sandia Knolls, New Mexico
Sandia Knolls is a census-designated place (CDP) in Bernalillo County, New Mexico, United States. The population was 1,208 at the 2010 census. It is part of the Albuquerque Metropolitan Statistical Area. Geography Sandia Knolls is located in northeastern Bernalillo County, at the southern end of a small mountain known as Monte Largo, east of the Sandia Mountains. It is bordered to the north by an undeveloped portion of the town of Edgewood, and the CDPs of San Antonito and Sandia Park are 3 miles (5 km) to the west.
