1999–2000 Croatian Football Cup
The 1999–2000 Croatian Football Cup was the ninth edition of Croatia's football knockout competition. Osijek were the defending champions, and it was won by Hajduk Split. Calendar Preliminary round First round Second round Quarter-finals Semi-finals First legs Second legs Dinamo Zagreb won 6–1 on aggregate. Hajduk Split won 4–3 on aggregate. Final First leg Second leg Hajduk Split won 2–1 on aggregate.
