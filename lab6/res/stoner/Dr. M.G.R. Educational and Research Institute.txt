Dr. M.G.R. Educational and Research Institute
Dr. M.G.R. Educational and Research Institute is an institution deemed to be university located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. It received its deemed to be university status in 2003. Constituent colleges The institution includes three constituent colleges: Dr. M.G.R. Engineering College Thai Moogambigai Dental College and Hospital A.C.S. Medical college and Hospital.
