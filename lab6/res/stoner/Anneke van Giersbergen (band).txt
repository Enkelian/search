Anneke van Giersbergen (band)
Anneke van Giersbergen & Agua de Annique (formerly Agua de Annique) was the solo project of the former The Gathering vocalist, Anneke van Giersbergen. History The project was announced on 5 June 2007, the same day that both The Gathering and Anneke van Giersbergen issued separate announcements that van Giersbergen would leave the band in August.
