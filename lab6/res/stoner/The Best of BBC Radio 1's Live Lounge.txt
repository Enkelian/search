The Best of BBC Radio 1's Live Lounge
The Best of BBC Radio 1's Live Lounge is a collection of live tracks played on Jo Whiley and later Fearne Cotton's Radio 1 show. It consists of both covers and the bands' own songs. It consisted of tracks previously available on past Live Lounge compilation albums, as well as one that was previously unavailable.The second disc is over 80 minutes long making it exceed the length of almost all compact discs.
