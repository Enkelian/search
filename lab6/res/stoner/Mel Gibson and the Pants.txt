Mel Gibson and the Pants
Mel Gibson and the Pants is an underground hip hop band based in Minneapolis, Minnesota.Ryan Olson is also a member of Digitata and Gayngs. History Mel Gibson and the Pants released the debut album, A Mannequin American, in 2004.Their second album, W/ Guitar, was released in 2005.The band released the third album, Sea vs. Shining Sea, in 2007. Ira Sather-Olson of Missoula Independent said, "Sea vs. Shining Sea proves that a marriage between numerous genres can in fact create lasting results."
