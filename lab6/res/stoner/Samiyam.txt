Samiyam
Sam Baker, better known by his stage name Samiyam, is an American hip hop producer based in Los Angeles, California. Life and career Samiyam released the Return EP on Hyperdub in 2008. He also released Rap Beats Vol. 1 in the same year.His official debut album, Sam Baker's Album, was released on Brainfeeder in 2011. He returned with the album, Wish You Were Here, in 2013.In 2015, his collaborative track with Earl Sweatshirt and Budgie, titled "Quest/Power", was uploaded on SoundCloud. In 2016, he released the album, Animals Have Feelings, which featured guest appearances from the likes of Earl Sweatshirt, Action Bronson, and Jeremiah Jae.
