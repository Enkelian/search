Montreal Mirror
Montreal Mirror was a free English language alternative newsweekly based in Montreal, Quebec, Canada which was distributed every Thursday. It had a circulation of 70,000, and reached a quarter of a million readers per week.First published on June 20, 1985, the publication became a weekly in September 1989. It was bought by media giant Quebecor in 1997.
