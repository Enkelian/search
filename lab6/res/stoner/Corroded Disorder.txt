Corroded Disorder
Corroded Disorder is a compilation album by electro-industrial band Front Line Assembly. It contains tracks from three previous releases, Corrosion, Disorder, and Convergence, as well as two bonus tracks. The release leaves out "Conflict" and "The Wrack Part III – Wisdom" from Corrosion. While the former appeared on Convergence, the latter has never been released on CD.
