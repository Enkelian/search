Framed in Blood - The Very Blessed of the 69 Eyes
Framed In Blood - The Very Blessed of the 69 Eyes is a compilation album, released by the Finnish band The 69 Eyes in 2003 through Gaga Goodies / Poko Rekords. The album was released in the U.S. on August 29, 2006 by Cleopatra Records. The album contains tracks from across the band's career, including both their glam metal and gothic rock styled works. Albums represented on the compilation run from "Savage Garden" to "Paris Kills".
