BBC Radio 1's Live Lounge 2016
BBC Radio 1's Live Lounge 2016 is a compilation album consisting of live tracks played on Clara Amfo's BBC Radio 1 show, both cover versions and original songs. The album was released on 11 November 2016, and is the twelfth in the series of Live Lounge albums. It debuted on the iTunes UK chart at #6 and reached #2.
