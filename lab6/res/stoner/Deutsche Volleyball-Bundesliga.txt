Deutsche Volleyball-Bundesliga
Volleyball Bundesliga (German: Volleyball Bundesliga), is the highest level of men's volleyball in Germany, a professional league competition for volleyball clubs located in this country. It has leagues for men and for women: German: Volleyball Bundesliga der Männer, Volleyball-Bundesliga der Frauen Volleyball-Bundesliga (Men) de:Deutsche Volleyball-Bundesliga (Männer) VfB Friedrichshafen with 14 Champion League is the most successful team.
