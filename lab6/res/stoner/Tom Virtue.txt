Tom Virtue
Thomas Edwin Virtue Jr. (born November 19, 1957) is an American actor. He is known for his roles in the television series Even Stevens (2000–2003) and The Secret Life of the American Teenager (2008–2013). Life and career Virtue was born in Sherman, Texas and raised outside of New York City and in Needham, Massachusetts. He attended Northwestern University and was a member of both The Practical Theatre Company and The Second City in Chicago.
