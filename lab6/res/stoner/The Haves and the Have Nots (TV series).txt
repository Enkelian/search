The Haves and the Have Nots (TV series)
The Haves and the Have Nots is an American crime drama and soap opera created, executive produced, written, and directed by Tyler Perry. The premise of the series is based on Perry's 2011 play of the same name. The series premiered on May 28, 2013 on the Oprah Winfrey Network; It is the first scripted television series to air on the network. On October 24, 2019, a seventh season was announced, which premiered on January 7, 2020. Series overview OWN renewed the series for a second season midway into the first season. The season was originally to consist of 16 episodes and bring the series to 32 in total by the completion of season 2; however on August 21, it was announced that the network had ordered 4 additional episodes, which will bring the series to 36 episodes in total by season 2's completion.Production of season 3 began in August 2014 following a 25 episode order which will bring the episode count to 61 through 2015.
