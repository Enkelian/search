1999–2000 Azerbaijan Top League
The 1999-00 Azerbaijan Top League was contested by twelve clubs and won by FK Shamkir. Teams Stadia and locations 1Qarabağ played their home matches at Surakhani Stadium in Baku before moving to their current stadium on 3 May 2009.
