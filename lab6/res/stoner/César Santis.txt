César Santis
César Elías Santis Santander (born February 5, 1979 in Santiago, Chile) is a former footballer who played for Unión Española (1996–98, 2002–03), Espanyol (1998–2000), Real Murcia (2001–02), Everton de Viña del Mar (2004–05), Antofagasta (2006) and Audax Italiano (2006–09).In March 2007 he played in a Copa Libertadores match for Audax, against Necaxa.
