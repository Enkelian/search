1929–30 La Liga
The 1929–30 Primera División season started December 1, 1929, and finished March 30, 1930. A total of 10 teams contested the league, where Barcelona were the defending champions. As different from the previous season, the last qualified team was directly relegated. Athletic Bilbao achieved their first title ever after ending the season unbeaten.
