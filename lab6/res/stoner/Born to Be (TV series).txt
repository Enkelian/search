Born to Be (TV series)
Born to Be is a documentary television series that aired on the Canadian music station MuchMusic. The program documented and featured archival footage of interviews with household name music celebrities of their success, i.e. Gwen Stefani, Mariah Carey, Nelly Furtado, Avril Lavigne, Demi Lovato, Marianas Trench and 50 Cent. Each episode centred on an individual music celebrity's childhood, their musical influences, sources of inspiration, and their achievements that have led to their successful music career.
