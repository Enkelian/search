...At a Loss
…At a Loss is the third album by sludge metal band Buzzov*en, released on May 5, 1998, by Off The Records. Reissued in 2010 on vinyl and CD through Emetic Records. Track listing *Left Behind ends at 7:34 with the final half hour ending in one droning, vibrating guitar effect with the main riff playing very softly in the background.
