I Don't Want to Change the World
"I Don't Want to Change the World" is a heavy metal song by Ozzy Osbourne. It first appeared as the second track of the album No More Tears. Though the song was not released as a commercial single, in Britain the song was released as a promotional 12" single and a CD single (Epic XPCD 172/XPR 1745) with the tracks: I Don't Want to Change the World 04:07 Mama, I'm Coming Home 04:12 No More Tears 07:24 Live version Two years later, the song appeared in a live version on the album Live & Loud (1993). This version won Best Metal Performance at the 36th Annual Grammy Awards. == References ==
