Mateo Corbo
Mateo Corbo (born April 21, 1976 in Montevideo) is a Uruguayan former footballer who played as a left full back. Biography Corbo played for A-League club Newcastle United Jets for the inaugural A-League season. He previously played for River Plate (1996–99), Real Oviedo (1999–2000), Barnsley F.C. (2000–02, scoring once against Stoke City in the League Cup), River Plate, (2003) Olimpia Asuncion (2004), and Oxford United (2005).
