Summer of Hate
Summer of Hate is the debut studio album by the San Diego band Crocodiles. The New York Times featured the album in its Critics' Choice section, saying that Summer of Hate has "some catchy choruses and efficient low-fi landscapes."The album cover is a photo of Manson Family member Ruth Anne Moorehouse.
