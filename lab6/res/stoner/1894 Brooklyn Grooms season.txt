1894 Brooklyn Grooms season
The 1894 Brooklyn Grooms finished in fifth place in a crowded National League pennant race. Offseason January 1, 1984: Dan Brouthers and Willie Keeler were traded by the Grooms to the Baltimore Orioles for Billy Shindle and George Treadway. March 15, 1894: Danny Richardson was sold by the Grooms to the Louisville Colonels.
