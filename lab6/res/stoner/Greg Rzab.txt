Greg Rzab
Greg Rzab (( AR-zab; born 1959, Chicago) is an American bass guitar player.Greg played for several years with Otis Rush's band, with Buddy Guy's band (1986-1998), and has also had a number of stints, tours, sessions, and records with other famous blues performers. In 2000, he was a member of the touring lineup of The Black Crowes and is featured on the bonus tracks of their 2000 album Live at the Greek with Jimmy Page. He was also a touring member of Gov't Mule for tours in late 2002 and early 2003. Since 2009 he is a member of a new lineup of the Bluesbreakers. == References ==
