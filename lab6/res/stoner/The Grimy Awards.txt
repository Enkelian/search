The Grimy Awards
The Grimy Awards is the third studio album by American rapper Ill Bill. It was released on February 26, 2013. The album features guest appearances from A-Trak, H.R., Shabazz the Disciple, Lil Fame, Meyhem Lauren, Q-Unique, O.C., Cormega, Tia Thomas, El-P and Jedi Mind Tricks With production coming from hip hop veterans such as DJ Premier, Pete Rock, Large Professor, Psycho Les and Ayatollah.
