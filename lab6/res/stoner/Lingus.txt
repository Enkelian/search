Lingus
Lingus is the second studio album by the rock band Amnesia. It was released on June 25, 1998 through Supreme Recordings. Track listing All tracks are written by Brad Laner, except where noted. Personnel Beck – harmonica on "Drop Down" Larry Corbett – cello Peter Grant – cover art Brad Laner – vocals, instruments, production, engineering, mixing Josh Laner – drums, percussion Justin Meldal-Johnsen – bass clarinet on "The Sensual Corgi" Juno Watt – vocals on "Train Try" == References ==
