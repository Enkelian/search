Pacific Philosophical Quarterly
The Pacific Philosophical Quarterly is a quarterly peer-reviewed academic journal of philosophy published by Wiley-Blackwell on behalf of the School of Philosophy (University of Southern California) and is edited by the faculty there. The journal covers all major areas of philosophy in the analytic tradition, sometimes as special issues aimed at a particular topic. History The journal was established in 1920 as The Personalist by Ralph Tyler Flewelling and focused on the philosophy of personalism.
