Beaulieu-sur-Layon
Beaulieu-sur-Layon (/bo.ljə.syr.lε.jɔ̃/) is a commune in the Maine-et-Loire department in western France. The composer and organist Louis Aubeux (1917–1999) was born in Beaulieu-sur-Layon.
