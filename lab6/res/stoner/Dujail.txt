Dujail
Dujail (33°51′N 44°14′E, Arabic: الدجيل; alternate spelling: Ad Dujayl) is a Shi'a district in the Saladin Governorate. It is situated about 65 kilometers (40 mi) north of Iraq's capital, Baghdad, and has approximately 100,000 inhabitants. It was the site of the 1982 Dujail Massacre, in which thousands of people, including children, died.. The 2008 Dujail bombing also took place here. == References ==
