Bootleg -Feedbacker-
Bootleg -Feedbacker- is a DVD by Japanese band Boris. Despite its name, this is an official release by the band and Diwphalanx Records. This video is a live recording of the album Boris at Last: -Feedbacker-. The DVD has three different covers, each one depicting a band member lying in a pool of blood.
