Ourense (comarca)
Ourense is a comarca in the Galician Province of Ourense. The overall population of this local region is 144,282 (2005). Municipalities Amoeiro, Barbadás, Coles, Esgos, Nogueira de Ramuín, Ourense, O Pereiro de Aguiar, A Peroxa, San Cibrao das Viñas, Taboadela, Toén and Vilamarín.
