Fabrice Moreau
Fabrice Moreau (born 7 October 1967 in Paris) is a former French-Cameroonian footballer. Played in 17 different clubs before retiring at 36, in 2004. Born from a French father and a Cameroonian mother, he was a full international for the latter, although not in any major tournament's final stages.
