Teenage Dirtbag
"Teenage Dirtbag" is a song and debut single by American pop rock group Wheatus. It was released in July 2000 as the lead single from their eponymous debut album. The song was written by lead singer Brendan B. Brown and was inspired by a childhood experience of his. The song was massively successful in Australia, spending four weeks at number one, being certified 3Ã— Platinum and becoming the second best-selling single of 2000. It also reached number two in Ireland, Germany and the United Kingdom, where it was certified Platinum in 2013. It has sold 5 million copies worldwide as of 2014.
