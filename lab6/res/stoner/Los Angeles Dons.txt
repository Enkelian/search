Los Angeles Dons
The Los Angeles Dons were an American football team in the All-America Football Conference from 1946 to 1949, and played its home games in the Los Angeles Memorial Coliseum. The Dons were the first professional football team to play a regular season game in Los Angeles, California, two weeks before the first game of the rival Los Angeles Rams of the National Football League, who had moved from Cleveland.
