National Museum of Science and Industry
The Science Museum Group (SMG) is a collection of British museums, comprising: The Science Museum in South Kensington, London The Science and Industry Museum in Manchester The National Railway Museum in York Locomotion in County Durham The National Science and Media Museum (formerly the National Media Museum and the National Museum of Photography, Film and Television) in Bradford, and The National Collections Centre Museum in Swindon, Wiltshire.The chairman of the group is Dame Mary Archer who was appointed by Prime Minister David Cameron for the four-year term from 2015 from 2018. History The term "National Museum of Science and Industry" had been in use as the Science Museum's subtitle since the early 1920s. The Board of Trustees of the Science Museum is the corporate body of SMG and was established under the National Heritage Act (1983) for the purpose of general management of the group.
