Coco Before Chanel
Coco Before Chanel (French: Coco avant Chanel) is a 2009 biographical drama film directed and co-written by Anne Fontaine. The film stars Audrey Tautou and details the early life of French fashion designer Coco Chanel. The film was first released on 22 April 2009 in France. As of 21 December that year, it had grossed $43,832,376 worldwide. The production budget was $23 million.Instead of releasing Coco Before Chanel in the United States itself, Warner Bros. Pictures let Sony Pictures Classics handle the release there.
