2019 National League Division Series
The 2019 National League Division Series were two best-of-five-games series in Major League Baseball (MLB) to determine the participating teams of the 2019 National League Championship Series. The three divisional winners, seeded first through third, and a fourth team—determined by the NL Wild Card Game—played in two series. These matchups were: (2) Los Angeles Dodgers (West Division champions) vs. (3) Washington Nationals (Wild Card Game winner) (2) Atlanta Braves (East Division champions) vs. (3) St. Louis Cardinals (Central Division champions)The higher-seeded team of each series hosted Games 1, 2 and 5, with the lower-seeded team hosting Games 3 and 4.For the third straight year, Major League Baseball sold presenting sponsorships to all of its postseason series. This NLDS was sponsored by Utz and officially known as the 2019 National League Division Series presented by Utz Snacks. The Cardinals and Nationals won their respective series to advance to the Championship Series. Background The Los Angeles Dodgers clinched the National League West title on September 10, and secured the top-seed in the NLDS via their league-leading winning percentage.
