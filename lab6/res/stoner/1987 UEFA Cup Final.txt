1987 UEFA Cup Final
The 1987 UEFA Cup Final was a football tie played on 6 and 20 May 1987 between IFK Göteborg of Sweden and Dundee United F.C. of Scotland. Göteborg won 2–1 on aggregate, their second UEFA Cup triumph following victory in 1982. It was Dundee United's first and so far only major European final.
