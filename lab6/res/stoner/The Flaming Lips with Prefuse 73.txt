The Flaming Lips with Prefuse 73
The Flaming Lips with Prefuse 73 is a four-track collaboration EP with Guillermo Scott Herren (a.k.a. Prefuse 73) pressed on randomly colored vinyl 12" so that no two look alike. Limited to 1000-2000 copies, self-released by band and only sold at select record stores. Some copies were hand assembled and autographed by vocalist Wayne Coyne. The song "Supermoon Made Me Want to Pee" was later released on The Flaming Lips and Heady Fwends.
