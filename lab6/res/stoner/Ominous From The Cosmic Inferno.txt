Ominous From The Cosmic Inferno
Ominous From The Cosmic Inferno is an album by Acid Mothers Temple & The Cosmic Inferno, released in 2007 by Brazilian label Essence Music. The album was limited to one thousand copies and came packaged in a miniature gatefold LP sleeve with pop-up artwork.
