Manzano Springs, New Mexico
Manzano Springs is a census-designated place (CDP) in Torrance and Bernalillo counties, New Mexico, United States. The population was 137 at the 2010 census. It is part of the Albuquerque Metropolitan Statistical Area. Geography Manzano Springs is located in northwestern Torrance County and extends west into southeastern Bernalillo County. The main road through the CDP is New Mexico State Road 222. According to the United States Census Bureau, the CDP has a total area of 2.3 square miles (5.9 km2), all land. == References ==
