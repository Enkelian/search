The Light Shines On Vol 2
The Light Shines On Vol 2 is a compilation album by Electric Light Orchestra (ELO). Released in 1979 by Harvest Records, it is a compilation of their early years with the label. Track listing Al songs written by Jeff Lynne, except where noted.
