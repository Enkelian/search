Università Iuav di Venezia
Università Iuav di Venezia is a university in Venice, Italy. It was founded in 1926 as the Istituto Universitario di Architettura di Venezia, and has 3 departments. History The institution was founded in 1926 as the Istituto Universitario di Architettura di Venezia ("higher institute of architecture of Venice"), and originates in the foundation of the special course in architecture in 1923 at the Accademia di Belle Arti of Venice. It was the second architecture school in Italy after that of Rome. In 2001 a faculty of Design and Arts was added to those of Architecture and Urban and Regional Planning.
