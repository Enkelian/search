Skourta
Skourta (Greek: Σκούρτα) is a village in Boeotia, Greece. In 2011 its population was 784. Skourta is part of the municipality Tanagra. It is situated northwest of the Parnitha mountain, in a rather sparsely populated area, dominated by agriculture and forestry.
