Université Paris Lumières
Université Paris Lumières is an association of universities and higher education institutions (ComUE) for institutions of higher education, research and culture in the Île-de-France (including Paris) region of France. The association was created as a ComUE according to the 2013 Law on Higher Education and Research (France), effective December 29, 2014.
