1911 Brooklyn Trolley Dodgers season
With the 1911 season, the Superbas changed the team name to the Brooklyn Trolley Dodgers. However, the team still struggled, finishing in seventh place. Regular season Season standings Record vs. opponents Notable transactions April 1911: Hub Northen was purchased by the Dodgers from the Cincinnati Reds. September 16, 1911: Elmer Steele was purchased by the Dodgers from the Pittsburgh Pirates.
