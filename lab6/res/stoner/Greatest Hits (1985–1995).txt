Greatest Hits (1985–1995)
Greatest Hits (1985–1995) is a compilation album by American recording artist Michael Bolton, released in 1995. The album features Bolton's biggest hit singles from his five previous albums; Everybody's Crazy, The Hunger, Soul Provider, Time, Love & Tenderness and The One Thing, plus five new recordings.
