15th Critics' Choice Awards
The 15th Critics' Choice Awards were presented on January 15, 2010, honoring the finest achievements of 2009 filmmaking. The ceremony was held at the Hollywood Palladium, broadcast on VH1, and hosted by Kristin Chenoweth.
