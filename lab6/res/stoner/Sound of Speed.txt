Sound of Speed
Sound of Speed is an extended play (EP) by the Scottish rock band The Jesus & Mary Chain, released in 1993. The EP was released by Blanco y Negro Records on 7-inch vinyl, 10-inch vinyl, cassette single and CD single. It reached number 30 on the UK Singles Chart. William Reid and Jim Reid were the producers for all the tracks.
