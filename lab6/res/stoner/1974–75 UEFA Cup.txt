1974–75 UEFA Cup
The 1974–75 UEFA Cup was won by Borussia Mönchengladbach over Twente on aggregate. The third club was revoked to Scotland and Belgium, and it was assigned to the Netherlands and Austria. First round First leg Second leg Internazionale won 3–0 on aggregate. Rába ETO Győr 4–4 PFC Lokomotiv Plovdiv on aggregate. Rába ETO Győr won 5–4 on penalties. Napoli won 3–1 on aggregate. Juventus won 4–2 on aggregate. Fortuna Düsseldorf won 4–2 on aggregate. Second round First leg Second leg Juventus won 8–2 on aggregate. Fortuna Düsseldorf won 3–2 on aggregate. Amsterdam won 2–1 on aggregate. Napoli won 2–0 on aggregate. Third round First leg Second leg Baník Ostrava won 3–1 on aggregate. Ajax 2–2 Juventus. Juventus won on away goals rule. Quarter-finals First leg Second leg Juventus won 2–0 on aggregate. Semi-finals First leg Second leg Twente won 4–1 on aggregate. Final First leg Second leg Borussia Mönchengladbach won 5–1 on aggregate.
