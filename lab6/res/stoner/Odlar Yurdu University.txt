Odlar Yurdu University
Odlar Yurdu University is a private university located in Baku, Azerbaijan. It was founded in 1995, and became a degree granting institution in 1999. The name is based on the official Azeri national motto, Odlar Yurdu, which is translated as "Land of The Eternal Fire". Admission to the Master of Business Administration (MBA) Program established in partnership with Georgia State University (Atlanta, USA).
