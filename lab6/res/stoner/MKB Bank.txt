MKB Bank
MKB Bank (formerly Magyar Külkereskedelmi Bank (Hungarian Foreign Trade Bank)) is the fourth-biggest commercial bank in Hungary. Its privatisation started in 1994, when BayernLB purchased a minority stake. Hungarian government bought it back in 2014.The MKB Bank is the main sponsor of the MKB Veszprém handball club.
