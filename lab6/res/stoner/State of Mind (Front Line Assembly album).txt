State of Mind (Front Line Assembly album)
State of Mind is the second full-length studio album by electro-industrial artists Front Line Assembly. It was originally released in 1988 by Dossier, on both Compact Disc and LP format. State of Mind was later released on Cleopatra Records with alternate artwork and a bonus track, "Inside Out". Band leader Bill Leeb said about choosing the label Dossier: "We just did it because I liked a lot of the other artists on the label." Release Cleopatra re-issued State of Mind on vinyl on April 29, 2016. This edition contains only 8 tracks. Track listing All tracks are written by Bill Leeb.
