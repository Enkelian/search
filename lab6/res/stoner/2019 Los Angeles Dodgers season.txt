2019 Los Angeles Dodgers season
The 2019 Los Angeles Dodgers season was the 130th season for the franchise in Major League Baseball, and their 62nd season in Los Angeles, California. They played their home games at Dodger Stadium. On September 4, the Dodgers broke the National League record for most home runs in a season with 250th homer (breaking the old mark set by the 2000 Houston Astros). The Dodgers clinched their seventh straight National League West title on September 10, the earliest they had clinched in franchise history.
