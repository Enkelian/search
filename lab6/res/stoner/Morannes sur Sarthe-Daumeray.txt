Morannes sur Sarthe-Daumeray
Morannes sur Sarthe-Daumeray is a commune in the department of Maine-et-Loire, western France. The municipality was established on 1 January 2017 by merger of the former communes of Morannes-sur-Sarthe (the seat) and Daumeray. See also Communes of the Maine-et-Loire department == References ==
