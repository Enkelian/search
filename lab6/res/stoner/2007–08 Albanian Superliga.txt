2007–08 Albanian Superliga
The 2007–08 Albanian Superliga was the 72nd season of top-tier football in Albania and the tenth season under the name Kategoria superiore. The season began on 25 August 2007 and ended on 17 May 2008. KF Tirana were the defending champions, having won their twenty-third title the previous season.
