2007–08 Liechtenstein Cup
The 2007–08 Liechtenstein Cup was the sixty-third season of Liechtenstein's annual cup competition. Seven clubs competed with a total of sixteen teams for one spot in the first qualifying round of the UEFA Cup. Defending champions were FC Vaduz, who have won the cup continuously since 1998.
