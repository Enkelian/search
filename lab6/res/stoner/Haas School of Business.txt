Haas School of Business
The Walter A. Haas School of Business, also known as the Haas School of Business or Berkeley Haas, is the business school of the University of California, Berkeley. The school is housed in four buildings surrounding a central courtyard on the southeastern corner of the UC Berkeley campus. It was the United States' first business school at a public university and is ranked as a global top-10 business school by The Economist, Financial Times, QS World University Rankings, U.S. News & World Report, and Bloomberg Businessweek. Berkeley Haas is ranked as a leading worldwide business school for entrepreneurship, technology innovation, and finance; and is among the top five most selective business schools worldwide.
