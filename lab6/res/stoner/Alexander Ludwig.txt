Alexander Ludwig
Alexander Ludwig (born May 7, 1992) is a Canadian actor, singer and model. His notable film credits include The Seeker: The Dark Is Rising, Race to Witch Mountain, The Hunger Games, Grown Ups 2, and Bad Boys for Life. He appears in the History Channel series Vikings. Early life Ludwig was born in Vancouver, British Columbia. He has three younger siblings: a sister and brother named Nicholas and Natalie, who are twins, and a younger sister, Sophia.
