California's 37th State Senate district
California's 37th State Senate district is one of 40 California State Senate districts. It is currently represented by Republican John Moorlach of Costa Mesa. District profile The district encompasses central Orange County, centered on the city of Irvine. The district contains a mix of coastal beaches, suburban sprawl, and the Santa Ana Mountains.
