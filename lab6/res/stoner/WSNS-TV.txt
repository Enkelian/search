WSNS-TV
WSNS-TV, virtual channel 44 (UHF digital channel 33), is a Telemundo owned-and-operated television station licensed to Chicago, Illinois, United States. The station is owned by the Telemundo Station Group subsidiary of NBCUniversal, as part of a duopoly with NBC owned-and-operated station WMAQ-TV (channel 5); NBCUniversal, a Comcast subsidiary, owns both networks, along with regional sports network NBC Sports Chicago. WSNS-TV and WMAQ-TV share studios at the NBC Tower on North Columbus Drive in the city's Streeterville neighborhood and transmitter facilities atop the Willis Tower on South Wacker Drive in the Chicago Loop.
