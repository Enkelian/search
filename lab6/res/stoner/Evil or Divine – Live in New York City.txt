Evil or Divine – Live in New York City
Evil or Divine – Live in New York City is a video album by the American heavy metal band Dio, recorded in New York City in 2002 and released on DVD in 2003. It was also released on CD as a live album in 2005. DVD track listing All lyrics by Ronnie James Dio, music as stated.
