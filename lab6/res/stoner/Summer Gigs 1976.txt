Summer Gigs 1976
Summer Gigs 1976 was a short tour by Queen, consisting of four shows that took place before A Day at the Races was released. The last concert in Hyde Park was a free gig, and gathered a crowd of 150,000 people. It was also filmed professionally, and is now widely available on unofficial releases.
