1999–2000 Swiss Cup
The 1999–2000 Swiss Cup is the 75th season of Switzerland's annual football cup competition. It began on 7 August 1999 with the first games of Round 1 and ended on 28 May 2000 with the Final. The winners of the competition qualified for the 2000-01 UEFA cup first round.
