2020 UCLA Bruins football team
The 2020 UCLA Bruins football team will represent the University of California, Los Angeles during the 2020 NCAA Division I FBS football season. The team will be led by third-year head coach Chip Kelly. The Bruins finished 4–8 in 2019, 4–5 in conference play.
