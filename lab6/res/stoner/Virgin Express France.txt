Virgin Express France
Virgin Express France, originally Air Provence Charter, was a French subsidiary of the airline Virgin Express, with its head office on the grounds of Charles de Gaulle International Airport in Tremblay-en-France. They flew the Boeing 737-300 and the -400 The airline founded as Air Provence Charter in 1978.
