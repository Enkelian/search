Wall of Sound Records
Wall of Sound is an independent record label based in London, England and was founded by Mark Jones in 1994. History The label gained international recognition and residencies were set up in cities around the globe including a yearly summer residency at Ibiza's "Manumission" (an annual club party).Human League released their first album Credo for Wall of Sound in 2011. In September they released the album on vinyl.
