Tapiola Bank
Tapiola Bank Ltd is a Finnish bank and a part of Tapiola Group, now LähiTapiola. Tapiola was originally only in the insurance business, but entered also the banking market in 2004 with Tapiola Bank. Loyalty benefits (50–100% discount from service fees) are given to customers who have both insurance contracts and bank accounts at Tapiola. As of 2013, it's being merged into S-Bank. == External links ==
