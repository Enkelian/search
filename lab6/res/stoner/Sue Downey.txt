Sue Downey
Sue Ann Downey (born May 8, 1945) is an American former model and beauty queen who has held the Miss USA title and competed in the Miss Universe pageant. Downey won the Miss Ohio USA title and then the Miss USA 1965 title. She later competed in the 1965 Miss Universe pageant, where she placed Second Runner-up and the recipient of the Best National Costume award.
