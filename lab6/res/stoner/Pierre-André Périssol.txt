Pierre-André Périssol
Pierre-André Périssol, born April 30, 1947 in Nice, Alpes-Maritimes, is a French politician, former Minister of Housing, and former Deputy in the National Assembly of France. Périssol is currently serving his third term as the mayor of Moulins, Allier.In June 2010 Périssol was named president of the French Development Agency. References External links Personal website
