Washington Hispanic
Washington Hispanic is a Spanish-language newspaper in the Washington DC area. The company Washington Hispanic Inc. has its headquarters in Silver Spring, MarylandThe paper was founded in 1994 by Johnny Yataco. As of 2019 the circulation in the DC area was 45,000.
