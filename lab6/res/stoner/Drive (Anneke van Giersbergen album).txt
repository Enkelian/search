Drive (Anneke van Giersbergen album)
Drive is the fifth studio album by the Anneke van Giersbergen and the second released under Anneke van Giersbergen's name, instead of "Agua de Annique", released in the Benelux Union on 23 September 2013 and internationally on 15 October.
