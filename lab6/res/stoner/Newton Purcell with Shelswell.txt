Newton Purcell with Shelswell
Newton Purcell with Shelswell is a civil parish in Oxfordshire, England. It was formed in 1932 by merger of the parishes of Newton Purcell (grid reference SP6230) and Shelswell (grid reference SP6030). Sources Lobel, Mary D. (ed.) (1959).
