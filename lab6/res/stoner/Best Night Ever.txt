Best Night Ever
Best Night Ever is a 2013 American found footage comedy film written and directed by Jason Friedberg and Aaron Seltzer and produced by Jason Blum, Friedberg and Seltzer. The film took place in Boston, Massachusetts and Las Vegas, Nevada. The parody film is about The Town. Plot Four young women have a series of wild and raucous adventures during a bachelorette party in Las Vegas.
