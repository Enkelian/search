Des Kensel
Des Kensel is the Grammy Award winning former drummer of the stoner metal band High on Fire.In the early 1990s, Des Kensel played drums for the New Haven, CT band Mindwar. Des Kensel is married and has a son. He plays Tama drums, Evans drumheads, Meinl cymbals, and Vic Firth drumsticks.
