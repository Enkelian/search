Shushi Province
Shushi Province (Armenian: Շուշի) is a province of the de facto Republic of Artsakh. The Republic has limited international recognition. It is de jure part of the Republic of Azerbaijan. The province has 7 communities of which 1 is considered urban and 6 are rural. Sites of interest Shushi, its largest community. "The rock" of Shushi, below which is the village of Karin Tak (Քարին Տակ; Under the Rock).
