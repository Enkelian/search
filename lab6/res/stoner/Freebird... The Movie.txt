Freebird... The Movie
Freebird... The Movie is an in-depth look at Southern rock band, Lynyrd Skynyrd. Released on August 30, 1996, it is part documentary and part concert footage. Charlie Daniels was involved as "creative consultant". Performances Most of the footage is from the band's appearance at the Knebworth festival, on August 21, 1976, while the last performance is from a show in Oakland, California. Soundtrack A soundtrack of the film was released by MCA Records, including most of the recordings of the performances from the film.
