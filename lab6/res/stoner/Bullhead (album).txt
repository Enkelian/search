Bullhead (album)
Bullhead is the third studio album by the Melvins released in 1991 through Boner Records. This album has longer songs than previous Melvins albums. Before this, most of their songs were under two or three minutes. The Japanese experimental rock-drone-metal band Boris took their name from the first track on this album. Release Bullhead was originally released in 1991 on vinyl, CD and cassette. Boner Records re-released it on vinyl in 2015, paired with the previous album Ozma.
