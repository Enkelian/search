Pink Lady Lemonade ~ You're from Outer Space
Pink Lady Lemonade ~ You're From Outer Space is an album by Acid Mothers Temple & The Cosmic Inferno, released in 2008 by Riot Season. It is their only album with Afrirampo's Pikachu on vocals and drums.
