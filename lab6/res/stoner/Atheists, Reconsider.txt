Atheists, Reconsider
Atheists, Reconsider is a split EP between two Brooklyn groups: Oneida and Liars. Each band contributes two original songs, plus one cover of a composition by the other band. The album art was designed by Karl LaRocca/Kayrock Screenprinting.
