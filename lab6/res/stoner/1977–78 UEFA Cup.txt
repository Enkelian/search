1977–78 UEFA Cup
The 1977–78 UEFA Cup was won by PSV Eindhoven on aggregate over SEC Bastia. The third club was revoked to Hungary and Romania, and it was assigned to Switzerland and Poland. First round First leg UEFA invalidated this game and awarded a 3–0 victory to Schalke 04 as Fiorentina fielded an ineligible player, Gianfranco Casarsa. Second leg AEK Athens won 3–1 on aggregate. Torino won 4–1 on aggregate. Zürich won 2–1 on aggregate. Second round First leg Second leg Bastia won 5–2 on aggregate. Lens won 6–2 on aggregate. Torino won 3–2 on aggregate.
