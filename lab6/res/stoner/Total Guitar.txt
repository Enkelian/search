Total Guitar
Total Guitar is a monthly magazine based in the United Kingdom. The magazine is owned by Future plc, who publish many other magazines ranging from drums and video games to technology magazines.Total Guitar regularly contains tablature for rock, acoustic, punk, Blues, and Metal, as well as profiles of guitarists and their specific techniques and playing styles. Total Guitar is aimed at players who would like to learn how to play guitar and people who would like to develop their playing style, with tutorials in acoustic and electric guitar. Total Guitar also has an gear reviews section that looks at the latest guitar equipment. It also has interviews with guitarists from all kinds of genres and levels of playing, and has an in-depth features section. The Learn To Play section of Total Guitar covers songs from beginner level through to intermediate. The magazine also comes with a free CD in each issue that contains backing tracks and demonstration tracks to accompany the magazine's tablature.
