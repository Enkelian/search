École nationale supérieure des ingénieurs en arts chimiques et technologiques
The École nationale supérieure des ingénieurs en arts chimiques et technologiques, or INP-ENSIACET (or A7), is a high-level selective Grande École, located in Toulouse, France. 10 years of perspective for an over one hundred-year-old school allows the Labege-Toulouse platform to be considered as the largest European engineering school for the fields of matter transformation, energy and related services.
