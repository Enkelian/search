Wrecking Ball (Dead Confederate album)
Wrecking Ball is the first full-length album from the band, Dead Confederate. It was recorded in Austin, TX with producer Mike McCarthy (Spoon, ...And You Will Know Us by the Trail of Dead, Heartless Bastards) in January 2008.
