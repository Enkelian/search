Cee-Lo Green and His Perfect Imperfections
Cee-Lo Green and His Perfect Imperfections is the debut studio album by American hip hop musician Cee Lo Green, released on April 23, 2002. The album features guest appearances from Jahalla, Kirkland Underground, John Popper (of Blues Traveler), Joey Huffman and fellow Dungeon Family rappers Big Gipp and Backbone.
