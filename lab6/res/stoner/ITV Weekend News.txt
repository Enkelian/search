ITV Weekend News
ITV Weekend News is the national news bulletins on the British television network ITV at a weekend, although it is often referred to on guides simply as ITV News. It is produced by ITN. History The three broadcasts which air at lunchtime, early evening and late-night feature British national and international news stories, as well as a round-up of the weekend's sports news. All are followed by a national and regional ITV Weather forecast. The ITV Weekend News is presented by a pool of on air staff from across the ITV News portfolio.
