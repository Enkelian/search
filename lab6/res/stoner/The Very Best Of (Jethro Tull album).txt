The Very Best Of (Jethro Tull album)
The Very Best Of (2001) is a greatest hits album by Jethro Tull. It includes some of the band's biggest hits from 1969 to the present day. Ian Anderson selected the tracks himself, approving edits necessary for timing purposes, e.g. "Heavy Horses". Track listing All tracks are written by Ian Anderson, except where noted.
