Fernand Bouisson
Fernand Bouisson (French: [fɛʁnɑ̃ bwisɔ̃]; 16 June 1874 – 28 December 1959) was a French politician of the Third Republic, who served as President of the Chamber of Deputies from 1927 to 1936 and briefly as Prime Minister in 1935.
