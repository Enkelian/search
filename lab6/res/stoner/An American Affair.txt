An American Affair
An American Affair is a 2009 American independent period comedy-drama film directed by William Olsson and starring Gretchen Mol, James Rebhorn, Noah Wyle, Perrey Reeves, Mark Pellegrino, and Cameron Bright. It was released theatrically by Screen Media Films on February 27, 2009.The film was produced in 2008, written by Alex Metcalf, and produced by Kevin Leydon.
