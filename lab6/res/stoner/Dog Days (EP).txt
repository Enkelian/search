Dog Days (EP)
Dog Days is an EP released by Goatsnake on Southern Lord Records. It consists of material recorded from 1997-1999. It was repressed by Southern Lord in 2004 on vinyl only and limited to 500 copies. This repress includes a cover of Black Sabbath's "Who Are You." Also in 2004, Southern Lord repackaged Dog Days with Goatsnake Vol. 1 on a single CD.
