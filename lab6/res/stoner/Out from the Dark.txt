Out from the Dark
Out from the Dark is a demo album by the Norwegian black metal band Mayhem. It consists of a rehearsal from 1989, but it was not officially released until 1996, by Black Metal Records. This is one of the few recordings Mayhem did with vocalist Dead before his 8 April 1991 suicide.
