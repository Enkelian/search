French Kissin' (song)
"French Kissin" (released in certain countries as "French Kissin in the USA") is a song by American singer Debbie Harry from her second solo studio album, Rockbird (1986). It was released in November 1986 as the album's lead single. The song was written by Chuck Lorre, who would later create a series of successful sitcoms. Song information The song was released as the album's lead single in November 1986, and became a moderate radio hit in the United States, peaking at number 57 on the Billboard Hot 100. The song was also Harry's biggest success in the United Kingdom, where it reached number eight and became her only solo top-10 single there to date. For promotion, Harry did a live performance of "French Kissin" and "In Love with Love" on Saturday Night Live, as well as a handful of television interviews in the UK and US in late 1986. A music video (with a cameo by actress Katey Sagal) was also made and subsequently played on music video channels.
