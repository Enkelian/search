Sakuyo Junior College of Music
Sakuyo Junior College of Music (作陽音楽短期大学, Sakuyō Ongaku Tanki Daigaku) is a private junior college in Kurashiki, Okayama, Japan. History Sakuyo Junior College of Music was founded in 1951. In 1996, part of the campus was opened in the city of Okayama. The Tsuyama campus closed in 1999.
