2000 Meistriliiga
The 2000 Meistriliiga was the tenth season of the Meistriliiga, Estonia's premier football league. Levadia won their second title. League table Relegation playoff Kuressaare were awarded the playoff after Tervis withdrew due to unavailability of players active for the Estonian U-18 team.
