University of Corsica Pasquale Paoli
The University of Corsica Pasquale Paoli (Corsican and Italian: Università di Corsica Pasquale Paoli, French: Université de Corse Pascal Paoli) is a French university, based in Corte, Haute-Corse, with secondary campuses in Ajaccio, Biguglia and Cargèse. Founded in 1765, closed in 1769, and re-founded in 1981, it is the only university on the island of Corsica. See also List of public universities in France Pasquale Paoli
