1944 Brooklyn Dodgers season
The 1944 Brooklyn Dodgers saw constant roster turnover as players left for service in World War II. The team finished the season in seventh place in the National League. Regular season Season standings Record vs. opponents Notable transactions June 6, 1944: Bob Chipman was traded by the Dodgers to the Chicago Cubs for Eddie Stanky. August 12, 1944: Frank Drews was traded by the Dodgers to the Boston Braves for Mike Sandlock and cash.
