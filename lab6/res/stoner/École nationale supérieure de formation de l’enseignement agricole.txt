École nationale supérieure de formation de l’enseignement agricole
Located in the campus of Université fédérale de Toulouse Midi-Pyrénées in France, École nationale supérieure de formation de l’enseignement agricole is a renowned Agricultural school, with roots back to 1963 as the École nationale de formation agronomique, re-organised in 2016 as École nationale supérieure de formation de l’enseignement agricole. It is one of the Toulouse Tech school. It is one of the national teacher training school in Agronomy in France. ENSFEA also offers bachelor's degree, Master's and PhDs.
