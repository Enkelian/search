Under the Skin of the City
Under the Skin of the City (Persian: زیر پوست شهر‎, Zir-e poost-e shahr, also released as Under the City's Skin) is a 2001 Iranian drama film directed by Rakhshan Bani-E'temad. It was entered into the 23rd Moscow International Film Festival where it won the Special Golden St. George.
