Hollywierd
Hollywierd is a 1999 film directed by Penelope Spheeris. It is a documentary focusing on the making of the 1999 horror film Blood Dolls and features interviews with many of the people involved with the film. The film has never been released on any video format, although a promotional trailer for the film can be seen on the DVD release of Blood Dolls. External links Hollywierd on IMDb
