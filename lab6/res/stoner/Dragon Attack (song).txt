Dragon Attack (song)
"Dragon Attack" is a song by the British rock band Queen and written by the band's lead guitarist, Brian May. The song is the second track on Queen's eighth studio album The Game and was also featured on the B-side of the UK release of "Another One Bites the Dust". Thrash metal band Testament covered this song as a bonus track on their 2012 album Dark Roots of Earth. Queen comments about the record Bass guitarist John Deacon claimed it to be his favourite Queen song.
