Sky Nature
Sky Nature is an upcoming British pay television channel owned and operated by Sky, a division of Comcast, to be launched in 2020.The channel was supposed to launch on 7 April 2020. However, due to the ongoing 2019–20 coronavirus pandemic, the launch has been delayed. Sky Nature will broadcast original Sky content, content from Canadian channel Love Nature, and Sky's collection of David Attenborough programming.
