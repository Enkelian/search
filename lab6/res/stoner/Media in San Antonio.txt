Media in San Antonio
This is a list of media outlets in San Antonio, Texas. Television stations San Antonio is currently ranked as the 36th largest market by Nielsen. Despite the relatively large size of both the city proper and the metropolitan area, San Antonio has always been a medium-sized market. This is mainly because the nearby suburban and rural areas are not much larger than the city itself. By comparison, the other two Texas cities with populations of over a million people, Houston and Dallas, are among the 10 largest markets.
