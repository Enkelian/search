North Burnet–Gateway, Austin, Texas
North Burnet–Gateway (NBG) is a 2,300 acre neighborhood in northwest Austin, Texas. History In 2006, the City of Austin established the North Burnet/Gateway 2035 Master Plan. It adopted the North Burnet/Gateway Neighborhood Plan on November 1, 2007, and the North Burnet/Gateway Regulating Plan in 2009.
