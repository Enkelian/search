United Soccer League
The United Soccer League (USL), formerly known as United Soccer Leagues, is the organizing body of several soccer leagues with teams in the United States and Canada. It includes men's and women's leagues, both professional and amateur. Leagues currently organized are the USL Championship, USL League One, USL League Two and the youth Super Y League. It is directly affiliated with the United States Soccer Federation and the United States Adult Soccer Association and is headquartered in Tampa, Florida.
