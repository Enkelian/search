Paddy Pulzer
Patrick (Paddy) Pulzer is a drummer/percussionist from Oxford, England who has been in numerous British indie bands. He started out in 1984 with the band Shake Appeal, who eventually evolved into Swervedriver after Pulzer's departure. Pulzer left Shake Appeal in 1989 to join the band Jack. He has also performed in the bands Rosita, and The Boyfriends. == References ==
