Ardian Đokaj
Ardijan Đokaj (Serbian Cyrillic: Ардијан Ђокај, Albanian: Ardian Gjokaj; born 23 May 1979) is a Montenegrin former professional footballer who played as a winger. During his journeyman career, Đokaj played in Serbia and Montenegro, Spain, Turkey, and Germany.
