Blondie discography
This is the discography of American new wave band Blondie. Since 1976 they have released 11 studio albums, 4 live albums, 14 compilation albums, 3 remix albums and 38 singles. The band has sold an estimated 40 million albums. Albums Studio albums * The band's debut album, Blondie, did not chart in the UK following its original release, but charted in March 1979 after it was re-released by Chrysalis Records. † Ghosts of Download was released as part of a 2-disc set titled Blondie 4(0) Ever, which includes the album along with a disc titled Greatest Hits Deluxe Redux, which contains new re-recordings of the band's biggest hits. Live albums Compilation albums † Greatest Hits Deluxe Redux was released as part of a 2-disc set titled Blondie 4(0) Ever, which includes Ghosts of Download, the band's 10th studio album. Remix albums Box sets Singles * "One Way or Another" was not released as a single in the UK, but charted from downloads in 2013 after a cover/mash-up of the song was released by One Direction and reached number one.
