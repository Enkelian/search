1981 World Series
The 1981 World Series was the championship series of Major League Baseball's (MLB) 1981 season. The 78th edition of the World Series, it was a best-of-seven playoff played between the American League (AL) champion New York Yankees and the National League (NL) champion Los Angeles Dodgers. It marked their third meeting in the Series in five years as well as a record eleventh Series meeting overall and last Series meeting to date. The Dodgers won the Series in six games in a mirror image of the two teams' last two Series meetings in 1977 and 1978; it was the Dodgers' first title since 1965, their first victory over the Yankees since 1963, and third World Series win over the Yankees, overall. This is the last World Series that a team won after losing the first two games on the road.
