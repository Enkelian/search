Revolution (The Cult song)
"Revolution" is a song by the British rock band The Cult. It was the third single from The Cult's 1985 album Love, written by Ian Astbury and Billy Duffy. The song has been described as a "power ballad". Song construction It has been noted that the chord progression in the song is the same as She Sells Sanctuary and Rain, the first two singles from the Love album.
