1994 UCLA Bruins football team
The 1994 UCLA Bruins football team represented the University of California, Los Angeles in the 1994 NCAA Division I-A football season. The Bruins began the season ranked No. 14. By the end of the season, the team tied for fifth place in the Pacific-10 Conference.
