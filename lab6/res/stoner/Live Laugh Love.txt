Live Laugh Love
Live Laugh Love is the debut EP released by Stockholm-based Post-hardcore band Urma Sellinger in 2010. The EP was recorded in 2010 together with Matt Hyde at Bro Studio in London, United Kingdom and Lunatic Music in Gävle, Sweden.Live Laugh Love features three tracks titled “Good Times”, “Far from Sandra” and “Rise to the Challenges That Life Presents You”. Last two songs are on the track list of the debut album called Urma Sellinger which was released on January 26, 2012. All three songs are aired at The Pit FM.
