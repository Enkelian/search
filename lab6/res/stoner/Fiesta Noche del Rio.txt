Fiesta Noche del Rio
Fiesta Noche del Rio is a seasonal outdoor performance which features the songs and dances of Mexico, Spain, Argentina, and the U.S. states of California and Texas. The performance is based at the Arneson River Theater in the San Antonio River Walk, and has been in operation since 1958, making it the oldest outdoor performance in the United States.The current director and choreographer of the seven act flamenco and folklorico performance is Kathleen Rodriguez Hall.
