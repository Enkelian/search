Aerials (song)
"Aerials" is a single by the heavy metal band System of a Down. It was released in 2002 from their second album Toxicity, which earned the band its second Grammy Award nomination for Best Hard Rock Performance in 2003. The song hit number one on both the Billboard Alternative Songs and Mainstream Rock chart. It was System of a Down's first number-one hit. Music video The music video was directed by Shavo Odadjian and David Slade.
