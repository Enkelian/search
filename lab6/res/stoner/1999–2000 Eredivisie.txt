1999–2000 Eredivisie
The Dutch Eredivisie in the 1999–2000 season was contested by 18 teams. PSV won the championship. League standings Results table Promotion/relegation play-offs In the promotion/relegation competition, eight entrants (six from the Eerste Divisie and two from this league) entered in two groups. The group winners were promoted to (or remained in) the Eredivisie.
