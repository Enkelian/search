Ministry of Justice (Israel)
The Justice Ministry (Hebrew: מִשְׂרָד הַמִשְׁפָּטִים, Misrad HaMishpatim; Arabic: وزارة العدل‎) is the Israeli government ministry that oversees the Israeli judicial system. Israel's current Minister of Justice is Amir Ohana. List of ministers The Justice Minister (Hebrew: שַׂר הַמִשְׁפָּטִים, Sar HaMishpatim) is the political head of the ministry.
