2022 UEFA Europa League Final
The 2022 UEFA Europa League Final will be the final match of the 2021–22 UEFA Europa League, the 51st season of Europe's secondary club football tournament organised by UEFA, and the 13th season since it was renamed from the UEFA Cup to the UEFA Europa League. The match will be played at the Puskás Aréna in Budapest, Hungary in May 2022.The winners will earn the right to play against the winners of the 2021–22 UEFA Champions League in the 2022 UEFA Super Cup. Venue The Puskás Aréna was selected as the final host by the UEFA Executive Committee during their meeting in Amsterdam, Netherlands on 2 March 2020.The match will be the first UEFA Cup/Europa League final to be held in Budapest, and the second final in the competition's history to be held in Hungary after the 1985 first leg. The final is also the second UEFA club competition final to be held in Budapest after the 2019 UEFA Women's Champions League Final, making it the third overall UEFA club final in Hungary.
