Hickey & Boggs
Hickey & Boggs is a 1972 American neo-noir crime film written by Walter Hill and directed by Robert Culp. Plot Culp and Bill Cosby, formerly Culp's co-star on I Spy, play weary, hard-luck private eyes Al Hickey and Frank Boggs hired to find a missing woman. Their inquiries bring death to almost everyone around them, culminating in a violent conclusion.
