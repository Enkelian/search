June 2010 Rehearsal
June 2010 Rehearsal is an extended play by the American rock band Primus released on August 5, 2010 worldwide. The EP has been released digitally from Primus' official website for free. It is the first Primus release to feature original drummer Jay Lane, who left the band before their debut, Suck on This. Track listing All lyrics are written by Claypool; all music is composed by Primus (guitar melodies on "Harold of the Rocks" and "Pudding Time" by Todd Huth).
