Floyd County Productions
Floyd County Productions is an animation studio in Atlanta, GA. It is best known for producing Archer, Unsupervised, and Chozen, plus animation for various live-action shows. The company was founded in 2009 by Adam Reed and Matt Thompson after they closed 70/30 Productions following the cancellation of the company's shows. Floyd County Productions grew from a small eight-person studio into a competitive animation house.
