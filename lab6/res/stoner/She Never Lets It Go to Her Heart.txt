She Never Lets It Go to Her Heart
"She Never Lets It Go to Her Heart" is a song written by Chris Waters and Tom Shapiro, and performed by American country music artist Tim McGraw. It was released in June 1996 as the fourth single from McGraw's All I Want album. The song reached Number One on the Billboard Hot Country Singles & Tracks (now Hot Country Songs) charts. Single and album edits The single edit to "She Never Lets It Go to Her Heart," released for retail sale and radio airplay, features an alternate vocal track by McGraw that is slightly different from the original album version. In addition, the backing vocals were remixed, eliminating the "stereo width" effect heard in the album version. This was also Tim's first single to not have an accompanying music video.
