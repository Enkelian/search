EOG Resources
EOG Resources, Inc. is a company engaged in hydrocarbon exploration. It is organized in Delaware and headquartered in the Heritage Plaza building in Houston, Texas. The company is ranked 181st on the Fortune 500.The company was named Enron Oil & Gas Company before its separation from Enron in 1999.
