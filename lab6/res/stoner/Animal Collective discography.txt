Animal Collective discography
The discography of Animal Collective, an American experimental pop group, consists of 10 studio albums, 4 live albums, 2 video albums ("visual album"), extended plays and singles. The group consists of musicians Avey Tare (David Portner), Panda Bear (Noah Lennox), Deakin (Josh Dibb), and Geologist (Brian Weitz). They run the record label Paw Tracks, on which they have released much of their own material.
