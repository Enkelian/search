2000 Armenian Premier League
Statistics of Armenian Premier League in the 2000 season. FC Dinamo Yerevan and FC Mika-Kasakh are promoted. FC Mika-Kasakh is renamed FC Mika Tsement changed their name to FC Araks Ararat. FC Yerevan and Erebuni-Homenmen both withdrew before the start of the season. As a result, Kilikia FC who were originally relegated kept their place.
