The Rhapsody Tour
The Rhapsody Tour is the ongoing worldwide concert tour by British rock band Queen and American singer Adam Lambert. The tour was announced following the success of the biopic film Bohemian Rhapsody. The North American dates of the tour sold out in April 2019.
