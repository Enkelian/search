Korpilombolo
Korpilombolo (Swedish pronunciation: [kɔrpɪˈlɔ̌mːbɔlɔ]) is a locality situated in Pajala Municipality, Norrbotten County, Sweden with 529 inhabitants in 2010.The band Goat claim to be from this town.The dungeon synth project Skymning is from Korpilombolo.
