Jerde became well known as an innovator in the design of malls and related spaces. His firm has grown into a multi-disciplinary firm with offices in Los Angeles, Orange County, CA, Hong Kong, and Shanghai. Career Born in Alton, Illinois, Jerde was a graduate of the School of Architecture at the University of Southern California.
