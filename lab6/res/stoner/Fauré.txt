Fauré
Fauré (French: [fɔʁe] is an Occitan family name, a variant of Faure. Notable people with the surname include: Amédée Fauré or Victor-Amédée Faure (1801–1878), French painter Gabriel Fauré (1845–1924), French composer Cédric Fauré (b. 1979), French football striker See also Faure Foray, Foray (surname)
