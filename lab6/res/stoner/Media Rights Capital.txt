Media Rights Capital
MRC (formerly an abbreviation of Media Rights Capital) is an American independent film and television studio founded by Mordecai (Modi) Wiczyk and Asif Satchu. Its work includes the television programs House of Cards and Ozark as well as the films Knives Out, Ted and Baby Driver. Company history Investors MRC's investors include Guggenheim Partners, AT&T, WPP Group, Goldman Sachs and ABRY Partners. The network returned the Sunday timeslots to its affiliates as of the 2009-10 season. Media Rights Capital's other projects have included House of Cards, Ozark, Ted, and Baby Driver.
