The Little Death (2014 film)
The Little Death (known as A Funny Kind of Love in the UK) is a 2014 Australian comedy film written and directed by Josh Lawson. It deals with the secret lives of five suburban couples living in Sydney revealing both the fetishes and the repercussions that come with sharing them.
