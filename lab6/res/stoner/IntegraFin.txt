IntegraFin
IntegraFin Holdings plc (IntegraFin) is a British company which provides platform services to UK financial advisers and their clients. It is listed on the London Stock Exchange and is a constituent of the FTSE 250 Index. History The company was founded by Mike Howard who started the company in a flat above the Firehouse restaurant at Tabernacle Street in Shoreditch in London in April 1999.
