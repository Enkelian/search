Disorder (EP)
Disorder is an EP by Canadian electro-industrial band Front Line Assembly released in 1988. This was the band's first EP which was only released on Vinyl. Later Disorder was released on a record called Convergence, along with the Corrosion album. Track listing All tracks are written by Bill Leeb and Michael Balch.
