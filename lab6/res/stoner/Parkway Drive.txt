Parkway Drive
Parkway Drive is an Australian heavy metal band from Byron Bay, New South Wales, formed in 2003. As of 2018, Parkway Drive have released six full-length albums, one EP, two DVDs, a split album and one book, titled Ten Years of Parkway Drive. The band's latest four albums have reached the top 10 of the Australian ARIA Charts, with Ire reaching number 1 in October 2015, and Reverence in May 2018. The band's line-up has been consistent since the addition of bassist Jia O'Connor in 2006, with Brett Versteeg having left in 2004 and Shaun Cash in 2006.
