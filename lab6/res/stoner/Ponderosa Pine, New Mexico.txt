Ponderosa Pine, New Mexico
Ponderosa Pine is a census-designated place (CDP) in Bernalillo County, New Mexico, United States. The population was 1,195 at the 2010 census. It is part of the Albuquerque Metropolitan Statistical Area. Geography Ponderosa Pine is located in southeastern Bernalillo County. It is bordered to the north by the Cedro census-designated place, and to the west by Cibola National Forest. New Mexico State Road 337 leads through the community, leading north 10 miles (16 km) to Tijeras and Interstate 40.
