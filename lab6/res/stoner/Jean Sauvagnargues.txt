Jean Sauvagnargues
Jean Sauvagnargues (2 April 1915 – 6 August 2002) was a French politician who served as Minister of Foreign Affairs under Valéry Giscard d'Estaing from 1974 to 1976 and was Ambassador to Ethiopia, Tunisia, West Germany, and the United Kingdom. Sauvagnargues died on 6 August 2002 in Lausanne, aged 87. Honours Commandeur de la Légion d’Honneur, Commandeur de l’ordre national du Mérite, Croix de guerre.
