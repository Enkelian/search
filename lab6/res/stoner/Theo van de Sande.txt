Theo van de Sande
Theodorus Amandus Maria van de Sande (born 10 May 1947 in Tilburg, Netherlands) is a Dutch cinematographer. He graduated from the Netherlands Filmacademy in Amsterdam in 1970, and has been working as a cinematographer since 1972.He won Golden Calf for Best Cinematography in 1982 and 1987.He has been a member of the American Society of Cinematographers since 1991.
