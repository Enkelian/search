Cherry Flavor Night Time
Cherry Flavor Night Time is the debut album of Amnesia, released on March 11, 1997 through Supreme Recordings. A limited edition bonus disc contained a remix of the entire album by Brad Laner under his moniker Electric Company. Track listing All tracks are written by Brad Laner, except "Drained" co-written by Matt Devine and Josh Laner and "Cherry Flavor Night Time" co-written by Matt Devine.
