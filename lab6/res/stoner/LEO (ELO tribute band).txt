LEO (ELO tribute band)
L.E.O. is a 2006 project by Boston musician Bleu (musician) and collaborators which produced Alpacas Orgling, a 2006 album of original songs similar in style to Electric Light Orchestra-style music and 1970s AM radio pop music.The project was initiated by an onstage cover of ELO's "Telephone Line" by Bleu and Mike Viola. The album features major contributions from Matt Mahaffey of Self, Andy Sturmer of Jellyfish, Hanson, Mike Viola, and members of Chicago.
