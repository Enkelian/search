2000 Armenian Cup
The 2000 Armenian Cup was the ninth edition of the Armenian Cup, a football competition. In 2000, the tournament had 16 participants, out of which only 1 was a reserve team. Results First round The first legs were played on 18 March 2000. The second legs were played on 29 March 2000. Lori and Nairi withdrew from the competition. Quarter-finals The first legs were played on 9 April 2000. The second legs were played on 19 April 2000. Semi-finals The first legs were played on 4 May 2000. The second legs were played on 13 May 2000.
