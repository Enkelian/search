Canal+ Afrique
CanalSat Afrique is an African version of CanalSat, available mainly in the francophone countries of Central and West Africa, as well as some non-francophone countries such as Sierra Leone, Nigeria , Ghana and Cape Verde. The Service is broadcast off the Satellite NSS 7 at 22° West.
