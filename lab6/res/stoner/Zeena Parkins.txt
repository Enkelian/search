Zeena Parkins
Zeena Parkins is an American harpist active in free improvisation and jazz. Parkins plays standard harps, as well as several custom electric harps; she also plays piano and accordion. She is currently a guest faculty member for composition courses at Mills College. Life and career Born in 1956 in Detroit, Michigan, she studied at Bard College and moved to New York City in 1984.Her work ranges from solo performance to large ensembles.
