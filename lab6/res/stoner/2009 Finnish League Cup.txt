2009 Finnish League Cup
The 2009 Finnish League Cup was the 13th season of the Finnish League Cup, Finland's second-most prestigious cup football tournament. The cup consisted of two stages. First there was group stage that involved 14 Veikkausliiga teams divided into two groups.
