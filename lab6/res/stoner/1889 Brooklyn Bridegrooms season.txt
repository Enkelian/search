1889 Brooklyn Bridegrooms season
The 1889 Brooklyn Bridegrooms won the American Association championship by two games over the St. Louis Browns. Offseason December 23, 1888: Al Mays and Dave Orr were purchased from the Bridegrooms by the Columbus Solons. Prior to 1889 season: Paul Radford was purchased from the Bridegrooms by the Cleveland Spiders.
