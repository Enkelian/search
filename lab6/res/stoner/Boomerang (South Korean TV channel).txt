Boomerang (South Korean TV channel)
Boomerang (Korean: 부메랑; RR: Bumerang) is a South Korea version of the original American Boomerang television channel in the United States and was a pay television channel created by Turner Broadcasting which primarily shows animated programming. It was launched on 14 November 2015.The channel airs Korean animated series, along with Warner Bros, Cartoon Network, and Hanna Barbera Series.
