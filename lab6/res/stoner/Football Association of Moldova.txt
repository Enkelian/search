Football Association of Moldova
The Moldovan Football Federation (Romanian: Federația Moldovenească de Fotbal, FMF) is the governing body of football in Moldova. It organizes all the football leagues, including the top tier league Moldovan National Division (Divizia Națională), and the Moldova national football team. It is based in Chișinău.
