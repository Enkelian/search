I Loved You at Your Darkest
I Loved You at Your Darkest is the eleventh studio album by Polish blackened death metal band Behemoth, released on October 5, 2018. Reception Like its predecessor, The Satanist, I Loved You at Your Darkest was met with universal acclaim from music critics. Wall of Sound gave the album a perfect 10/10 review and said: "They have taken their sound, their style and their dynamic, as well as the best of their influences and put forth something that is beyond otherworldly."
