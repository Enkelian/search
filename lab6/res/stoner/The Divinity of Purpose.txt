The Divinity of Purpose
The Divinity of Purpose is the sixth studio album from American metalcore band Hatebreed. The album was released in North America on January 29, 2013 through Razor & Tie and in Europe on January 25, 2013 through Nuclear Blast Records. The album was co-produced by Hatebreed, Zeuss and Josh Wilbur. According to band frontman Jamey Jasta, the album will be “all pit, no shit.” On November 12, 2012 the group debuted a track from the album titled "Put It to the Torch" on YouTube.The Divinity of Purpose debuted at a career high No. 20 position on the Billboard 200 with over 17,400 copies sold in its first week. It has sold 55,000 copies in the US as of April 2016. Reception The aggregate review site Metacritic assigned an average score of 75 to the album based on 5 reviews, indicating "generally favorable reviews".In a positive review by Allmusic's James Christopher Monger, the reviewer characterized the record as "Meatier and more hardcore-centric than their last offering".
