Music from the Motion Picture Wayne's World 2
Wayne's World 2: Music from the Motion Picture is the soundtrack album for the 1993 comedy sequel film Wayne's World 2. It features two live tracks from Aerosmith, and a version of "Louie, Louie" by Robert Plant.
