Liberate (song)
"Liberate" is a song by the American heavy metal band Disturbed. The song was released as the third and last single from their second studio album, Believe. The single peaked at number 21 on the Bubbling Under Hot 100 Singles chart, number 4 in the Mainstream Rock Tracks chart and number 22 in the Modern Rock Tracks chart. Chart performance Personnel David Draiman – lead vocals, backing vocals Dan Donegan – guitar Steve Kmak – bass guitar Mike Wengren – drums Other appearances "Liberate" was featured in the video game Tony Hawk's Underground 2.
