1903 Brooklyn Superbas season
The 1903 Brooklyn Superbas season was a season in Major League Baseball. The Superbas began their slide from contention in the National League by finishing in fifth place. Offseason January 30, 1903: Jack Doyle was purchased by the Superbas from the Washington Senators. February 17, 1903: Henry Thielman was purchased by the Superbas from the Cincinnati Reds. February 17, 1903: Rube Vickers was purchased by the Superbas from the Cincinnati Reds. Regular season Season standings Record vs. opponents Notable transactions September 5, 1903: Tom McCreery was purchased from the Superbas by the Boston Beaneaters.
