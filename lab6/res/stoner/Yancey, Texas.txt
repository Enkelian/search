Yancey, Texas
Yancey is an unincorporated community in Medina County, Texas, United States. It is approximately 12 miles south of Hondo and is part of the San Antonio Metropolitan Statistical Area. According to an online community profile, Yancey has a population of 649 people. It has an Oil Business and a RV repair business. Also, it has a Restaurant called "Red's Place". Not only that, Yancey has many more businesses in their town.
