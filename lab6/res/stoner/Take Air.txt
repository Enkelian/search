Take Air
Take Air is an all-you-can-fly airline based in Martinique. It operates scheduled services in the Caribbean. History In December 2006 a Let L-410 UVP-E20 was delivered to Take Air and by March 2007 had logged well over 300 flight hours. A second aircraft was delivered on 18 March 2007 and will join Take Air's fleet immediately after going through the necessary formalities. Destinations Take Air operates scheduled charter flights connecting Martinique with Saint Martin, Antigua, Guadeloupe, Dominica, Saint Lucia, St Vincent, Canouan and Union Island.
