Cambridge Tower
Cambridge Tower is a building in Austin, Texas, United States, that opened in 1965 as a luxury apartment tower. The building was designed by Dallas-based architect Thomas E. Stanley. It currently operates as a condominium. It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places on June 20, 2018.
