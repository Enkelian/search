Good Friendly Violent Fun
Good Friendly Violent Fun is a live album by thrash metal band Exodus. The performance on this live album was from the band's 1989 tour, but not released until two years later. The name is derived from a lyric found in the Exodus song "The Toxic Waltz", from the band's 1989 album Fabulous Disaster.
