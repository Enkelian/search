Heartkiller
"Heartkiller" is a song by Finnish rock band HIM. It is the third track on their seventh studio album Screamworks: Love in Theory and Practice (2010), and was released in December 2009 as the second single from the album. Music video The music video for "Heartkiller" premiered on the band's MySpace page on January 7, 2010, and appeared on their official website and YouTube page a day later.
