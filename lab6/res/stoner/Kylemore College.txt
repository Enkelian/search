Kylemore College
Kylemore College is a secondary school in Ballyfermot, Dublin, Ireland. It is a gender mixed school and is a CDETB school. It also offers PLC courses. Kylemore College was established in 1965 by the City of Dublin Vocational Education Committee (CDVEC) as a vocational school.
