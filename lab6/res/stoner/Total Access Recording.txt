Total Access Recording
Total Access Recording is a recording studio located in Redondo Beach, California, owned and operated by sound engineer and founder Wyn Davis. Total Access opened for business in December 1980. The first clients of the studio were bands signed with SST Records, whose owner Greg Ginn had made a "bulk recording deal" with Davis. Renowned producer Ken Scott is also a client of the studio.
