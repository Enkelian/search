Sadus
Sadus is an American thrash metal band from Antioch, California, active from 1984 to 2015, and again since 2017. Originally a quartet, they are known for the unique, borderline death/black metal growling of vocalist Darren Travis, and bassist Steve Di Giorgio. Over the course of their 36-year career, Sadus has released five studio albums, one compilation album and four demos, and had only one lineup change, when guitarist Rob Moore left the band in 1993; instead of replacing him, the band has since continued as a trio.
