Pronounced 'Lĕh-'nérd 'Skin-'nérd Tour
Pronounced 'Lĕh-'nérd 'Skin-'nérd Tour was the first major concert tour by American Southern rock band Lynyrd Skynyrd. The tour was mainly played out in the United States with a single show in Canada. The 11 cities on the tour from November 20 to December 6 saw the band opening for the British rock group The Who. Typical setlist "I Ain't the One" "Tuesday's Gone" "Gimme Three Steps" "Simple Man" "Poison Whiskey" "Sweet Home Alabama" "Free Bird"Toward the end of the year, "Call Me The Breeze", "Workin' For MCA", and "Woman Of Mine" were added to the setlists.
