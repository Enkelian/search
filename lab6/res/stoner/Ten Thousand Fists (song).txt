Ten Thousand Fists (song)
"Ten Thousand Fists" is a song by the American rock band Disturbed. The song was released in 2006 as the fifth single from their third studio album, Ten Thousand Fists. It is also featured as soundtrack in the video game Madden NFL 06. Themes According to Disturbed's vocalist David Draiman, the song "signifies strength, unity, conviction, power, and the exhilaration that you feel when you get to see that at one of our shows. It's one of my favorite moments, and people know that I have an affinity for asking people to put their fists in the air, and it's just, it's exhilaration to be able to see ten thousand raised fists or more."
