Tweseldown Racecourse
Tweseldown Racecourse in Fleet, Hampshire was originally a National Hunt steeplechasing venue and the home of the equestrian dressage and eventing competitions in the 1948 Summer Olympics.Until 2012 the venue hosted Point to point racing. Lack of funds caused the owners to cease racing from the 2013 season. The interior of the course continues to be used for cross country eventing.
