My Drink n My 2 Step
"My Drink n My 2 Step" is a song by American hip hop recording artist Cassidy, released as the lead single from his third studio album B.A.R.S. The Barry Adrian Reese Story (2007). The song features vocals and production from longtime collaborator Swizz Beatz. Background The song is Cassidy's first single after his hiatus from music following a car accident and amnesia. The song samples Doug E. Fresh's "Nuthin'". Both Cassidy and Swizz Beatz use the Auto-Tune effect. The song was officially remixed featuring new verses from American recording artists, Kanye West and Ne-Yo.
