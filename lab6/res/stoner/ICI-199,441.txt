ICI-199,441
ICI-199,441 is a drug which acts as a potent and selective κ-opioid agonist, and has analgesic effects. It is a biased agonist of the KOR, and is one of a relatively few KOR ligands that is G protein-biased rather than β-arrestin-biased.[1] See also U-47700 U-50488 U-69,593 == References ==
