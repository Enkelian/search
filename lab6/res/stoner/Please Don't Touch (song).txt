Please Don't Touch (song)
"Please Don't Touch" is the debut single by English rock and roll group Johnny Kidd & the Pirates, released in 1959 reaching number 25 on the UK Singles Chart. Personnel Johnny Kidd – vocals Mike West, Tom Brown – backing vocals Alan Caddy – lead guitar Tony Doherty – rhythm guitar Johnny Gordon – bass Ken McKay – drums (McKay was replaced during the session by Don Toye) Cover versions In 1980, Lemmy chose to record the song as the A-side of the Headgirl (Motörhead and Girlschool collaboration) St. Valentine's Day Massacre EP. In 1989, The Meteors on their recording, Attack of the Chainsaw Mutants. In 2003 by Throw Rag on their Desert Shores album. == References ==
