Dawson, Austin, Texas
Dawson is a neighborhood in South Austin, Texas. Dawson comprises ZIP codes 78704. The area is bordered by Oltorf Road on the North, Ben White/290 on the South, South 1st on the West, and Congress ave on the East. The busiest H.E.B. in Austin is in the Dawson Neighborhood at the corner of South Congress and Oltorf. Dawson Mosaics There are several Mosaic murals in the neighborhood created by a local artist.
