University of Marne-la-Vallée
The University Paris-Est Marne-la-Vallée (Université Paris-Est Marne-la-Vallée, UPEM, formerly UPEMLV, and originally UMLV, University of Marne-la-Vallée) is a French university, in the Academy of Créteil. The main campus is located at Champs-sur-Marne. History The University Paris-Est Marne-la-Vallée was founded in 1991. From 2020, the University Paris-Est Marne-la-Vallée will merge with some other facilities in the Descartes Campus to become Gustave Eiffel University.
