Resolution Tour
The Resolution Tour was a current concert tour by American heavy metal band Lamb of God in support of the group's seventh studio album, Resolution, which was released in January 2012. Overview The tour begin on January 22, 2012 with a concert at the band's hometown of Richmond, Virginia. After a North American leg, the band toured Asia, Australia (performing on the Soundwave Festival), and Latin America.
