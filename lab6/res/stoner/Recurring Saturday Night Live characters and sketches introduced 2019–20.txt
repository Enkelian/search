Recurring Saturday Night Live characters and sketches introduced 2019–20
The following is a list of recurring Saturday Night Live characters and sketches introduced during the forty-fifth season of SNL, which began on September 28, 2019. Chen Biao Chinese government official Chen Biao (Bowen Yang) visits Weekend Update to discuss the US–China trade war. Instructor Auditions Two people (often Ego Nwodim and another cast member) work out at a fitness facility as multiple bizarre candidates audition to be instructors there (often including Bowen Yang, Heidi Gardner, Cecily Strong, others, and the host). Sisters and the Soldier Two sisters (Kate McKinnon, Aidy Bryant) scheme to kill their attractive sibling (played by the host), who is distracting the object of their affections (Beck Bennett), who will choose to be with one them. == References ==
