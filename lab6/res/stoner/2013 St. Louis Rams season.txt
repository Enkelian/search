2013 St. Louis Rams season
The 2013 St. Louis Rams season was the franchise's 76th season in the National Football League, the 19th overall in St. Louis and the second under head coach Jeff Fisher. The Rams equaled their win total from 2012, but missed the playoffs for a ninth consecutive season. Week 7 was Sam Bradford's last game in a Rams uniform, as he tore his ACL in a game against Carolina.
