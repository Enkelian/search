Fuentealbilla
Fuentealbilla (Spanish pronunciation: [fwen.te.alˈβi.ʎa]) is a village and municipality in the province of Albacete, Castile-La Mancha, Spain. It is located to the northeast of the city of Albacete and had a population of 2,071 in 2009. Vissel Kobe and Spain midfielder Andrés Iniesta, who scored the winning goal in the 2010 FIFA World Cup Final, was born in Fuentealbilla. External links Official website of Fuentealbilla
