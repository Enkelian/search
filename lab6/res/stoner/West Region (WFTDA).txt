West Region (WFTDA)
The Women's Flat Track Derby Association's (WFTDA) West Region was formed in 2006. In 2009, teams from the eastern part of the region were moved into the North Central and South Central Regions.For 2011, the region was reduced in size, with teams in New Mexico moving to the South Central Region.
