1962–63 La Liga
The 1962–63 La Liga was the 32nd season since its establishment. The season started on September 16, 1962, and finished on April 21, 1963. Team locations League table Results Relegation play-offs Español won their series against Mallorca after a tie-break match where Catalans won by 1–0.
