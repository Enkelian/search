Ajiona Alexus
Ajiona Alexus Brown (born March 16, 1996) is an American actress known for her roles in The Rickey Smiley Show, Empire, 13 Reasons Why, Acrimony, and Breaking In. She started her career at the age of 12. Early life Alexus was born on March 16, 1996 in Tuskegee, Alabama. She studies Theater Arts at the Alabama School of Fine Arts.
