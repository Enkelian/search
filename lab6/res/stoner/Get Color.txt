Get Color
Get Color is the second studio album by American electronic noise rock band Health, released in 2009. It received generally favorable reviews from critics. The album debuted at number 17 on the Billboard Heatseekers Albums chart, selling 1,500 copies in its first week. It has sold 11,000 copies as of July 2015.
