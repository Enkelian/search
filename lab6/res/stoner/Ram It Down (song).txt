Ram It Down (song)
"Ram It Down" is the opening track on British heavy metal band Judas Priest's 1988 album, Ram It Down, and was released as a single. The song was performed live every night of the tour, but never since.The song features both a powerful high pitched scream by Rob Halford that opens the song and a dual guitar solo by Glenn Tipton and K.
