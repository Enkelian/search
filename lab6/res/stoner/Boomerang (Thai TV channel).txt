Boomerang (Thai TV channel)
Boomerang is a Thai television channel owned by Warner Bros. which primarily shows animated programming. It was launched on 14 August 2013.Unusually for a channel in the TBS Asia-Pacific region, Boomerang features the 2004 European Boomerang logo in addition to the 2012 EMEA Boomerang branding package, which was only used on Boomerang feeds operated by Turner Broadcasting System Europe from 2012 to 2015.
