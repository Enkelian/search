Early life Bob Hunter was born March 19, 1913, and went to Huntington Park High School in Huntington Park, California. After attending the University of Southern California, he went to Southwestern Law School in Los Angeles. Career His career as a baseball writer began in the late 1930s at the Post Record in Los Angeles, where he covered the Los Angeles Angels and Hollywood Stars of the Pacific Coast League.
