Ringkøbing Landbobank
Ringkøbing Landbobank is a bank headquartered in Ringkøbing, Denmark. It was founded in 1886. The bank currently (late 2017) has a share buyback programme.It is the most popular bank in Denmark based on customer reviews. Building The head office is at Torvet 1 in Ringkøbing.
