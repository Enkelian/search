Don't Rob Another Man's Castle
"Don't Rob Another Man's Castle" is a song written by Jenny Lou Carson. The song was first performed by Eddy Arnold who reached No. 1 on the Folk Best Seller charts in 1949. Later that same year, Ernest Tubb and The Andrews Sisters along with The Texas Troubadors, took their version of the song to #10 on the Folk Best Seller List.
