2008 Faroe Islands Cup
The Faroe Islands Cup 2008 was played between March 15 and June 14, 2008. It was won by EB/Streymur, who successively defended their title. Only the first teams of Faroese football clubs were allowed to participate. The First Round involved only teams from second and third deild. Teams from the highest two divisions entered the competition in the Second Round.
