Pierre Paul de Méredieu, baron de Naillac
Pierre Paul de Méredieu, baron de Naillac (born 17 August 1737 Périgueux - 1813) He was the son of Eymeric Méredieu, lord of Amboise and Elisabeth Vaucocour. Offices Captain of cavalry - Chevalier de Saint-Louis Minister of Foreign Affairs of 13 June 1792 to 18 June 1792 in the Government of Louis XVI.
