2007–08 Slovenian Football Cup
The 2007–08 Slovenian Football Cup was the 17th season of the Slovenian Football Cup, Slovenia's football knockout competition. 20 lower league teams played in the first two rounds and the Slovenian PrvaLiga teams joined in the Round of 16.
