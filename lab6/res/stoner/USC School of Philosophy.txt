USC School of Philosophy
The School of Philosophy is an academic division in the Dana and David Dornsife College of Letters, Arts, and Sciences at University of Southern California. It is one of the largest centres for the study of philosophy in the United States. Rankings The Philosophical Gourmet Report lists the school at 7th in the US and 8th in the English-speaking world.
