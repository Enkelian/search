Homeserve
HomeServe PLC is a multinational home emergency repairs and improvements business based in Walsall in the United Kingdom. Established in 1993, it is now listed on the London Stock Exchange and is a constituent of the FTSE 250 Index. HomeServe operates in the United Kingdom, United States, Canada, France, Spain and Japan. History The Company was originally established by Richard Harpin in 1993 as a joint venture between South Staffordshire Water and himself under the name of Fastfix to offer an emergency plumbing service. In 1994, the company diversified offering plumbing and drainage policies under the name "HomeServiceScheme".
