Families Belong Together
Families Belong Together are a series of protests against Trump administration family separation policy which culminated in a key note event on June 30, 2018 in Washington, D.C., New York City, and 700 other cities and towns in the United States. Participants Many of those protesting had participated in previous anti-Trump protests and actions, others were new to immigration activism with parents who felt called into action based on the reported stories of separations. Some protesters compared the detainment of children by the U.S. government to the Nazi concentration camps.
