Castle Face Records
Castle Face Records is an American independent record label, founded in 2006 by John Dwyer, Matt Jones and Brian Lee Hughes.The label was initially formed to release Sucks Blood, the sixth studio album by Dwyer's band, Thee Oh Sees.
