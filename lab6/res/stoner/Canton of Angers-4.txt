Canton of Angers-4
The canton of Angers-4 is an administrative division of the Maine-et-Loire department, in western France. It was created at the French canton reorganisation which came into effect in March 2015. Its seat is in Angers.It consists of the following communes: Angers (partly) Avrillé Longuenée-en-Anjou (partly) Montreuil-Juigné == References ==
