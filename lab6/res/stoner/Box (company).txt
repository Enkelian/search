Box (company)
Box, Inc. (formerly Box.net), based in Redwood City, California, is a cloud content management and file sharing service for businesses. Official clients and apps are available for Windows, macOS, and several mobile platforms. Box was founded in 2005. History Box was originally developed as a college project of Aaron Levie while he was a student of the University of Southern California in 2004. Levie left school to run the company full-time in 2005. Levie became CEO, while his childhood friend Dylan Smith became CFO.
