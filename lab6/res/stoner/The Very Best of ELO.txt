The Very Best of ELO
The Very Best of ELO is a music video collection featuring thirteen Electric Light Orchestra (ELO) promotional videos, released in 1991 on VHS by Telstar Video Entertainment. The videos are taken from A New World Record, Out of the Blue and Discovery This is so far the only ELO compilation video ever released and includes three non-single videos. Track listing "Livin' Thing" "Mr. Blue Sky" "Wild West Hero" "Need Her Love" "Wishing" "Turn to Stone" "Shine a Little Love" "Confusion" "Last Train to London" "Tightrope" "Telephone Line" "The Diary of Horace Wimp" "Rockaria!"
