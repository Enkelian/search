Lamb of God (album)
Lamb of God is the upcoming tenth studio album by American heavy metal band Lamb of God, due for release on May 8, 2020. It will be the first studio album of all-new material by the band since 2015's VII: Sturm und Drang, making it the longest gap between their albums, and the first to feature Art Cruz as the replacement of original drummer Chris Adler, who left Lamb of God in July 2019.
