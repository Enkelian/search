Gerhard Poschner
Gerhard Hans Poschner (born 23 September 1969) is a German former professional footballer who played as a defensive midfielder. He appeared in 286 games in the Bundesliga during 12 seasons, scoring 22 goals for VfB Stuttgart and Borussia Dortmund. He also played professionally in Italy, Spain and Austria.
