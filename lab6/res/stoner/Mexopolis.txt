Mexopolis
Mexopolis (also known as Mexopolis Animation Studio) is an American production company founded in 1999 by Jorge R. Gutierrez and Sandra Equihua. It produced the television series El Tigre: The Adventures of Manny Rivera and all of Jorge R. Gutierrez's short films. The company is located in Burbank, California.
