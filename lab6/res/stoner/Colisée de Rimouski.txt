Colisée de Rimouski
The Colisée Financière Sun Life (in English, Sun Life Financial Coliseum) is a 4,285-seat (total capacity 5,062) multi-purpose arena in Rimouski, Quebec, Canada, built in 1966. It is home to the Rimouski Océanic ice hockey team, and the arena hosted the 2009 Memorial Cup. Gallery == References ==
