Time Tour
The Time Tour was a 1981–82 world concert tour by the English group Electric Light Orchestra (ELO). The tour began on 15 September in Austin, Texas and ended on 5 March in Munich. The band for the first time in their history took to the stage without cellists but Mik Kaminski was still present with his violin.
