Plaza (album)
Plaza is the third studio album by American psychedelic indie rock band Quilt, released on February 26, 2016 on Mexican Summer. Release The album was preceded by the singles "Eliot St." and "Roller". A music video for "Roller" was released on February 11, 2016 and a music video for "Padova" was released on May 26, 2016. Critical reception Plaza received favorable reviews from contemporary music critics. But for all its reverence of what's come before, the psych-rock album is as much a product of today as it is a throwback to flower power.
