Agnes Pihlava
Agnieszka "Agnes" Pihlava (born Polish: Agnieszka Ćwiklińska; born 19 March 1980 in Leszno, Poland) is a singer who placed fourth in Idols Finland 2, the Finnish version of Pop Idol.
