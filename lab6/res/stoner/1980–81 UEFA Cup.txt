1980–81 UEFA Cup
The 1980–81 UEFA Cup was won by Ipswich Town on aggregate over AZ. According to the 1979 UEFA decision, Fairs Cup entry criteria were finally abolished, and UEFA ranking was introduced.Albania did not join, and it ceded its place to the West German title holders.
