A Barcala
A Barcala is a comarca in the province of A Coruña, Galicia, Spain. Its capital town is Negreira. It contains two municipalities (Negreira and A Baña) and 11,385 inhabitants (INE 2005) in an area of 213.5 km². The comarca is located in northwest Spain. It borders three other comarcas: Xallas to the north; Santiago to the south and east; and Noia to the west. There is the Barcala river (Río Barcala) in the province of A Coruña, Galicia, Spain.
