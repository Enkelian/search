NAACP Image Award for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Motion Picture
This page lists the winners and nominees for the NAACP Image Award for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Motion Picture. The award was introduced in 1970 and was awarded sporadically until its permanent feature from 1995 onwards. There are several performances by actresses that won or were nominated for Academy Awards, they are listed alongside a symbol. Angela Bassett currently holds the record for most wins in this category, with three. Winners and nominees Winners are listed first and highlighted in bold.
