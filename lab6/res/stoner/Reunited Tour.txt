Reunited Tour
The Reunited Tour was a 2004 concert tour by British heavy metal band Judas Priest. It ran from 2 June 2004 until 2 September 2004. This tour celebrated the return of Rob Halford since his departure in 1992, replacing vocalist Tim "Ripper" Owens. The band also began performing in Eâ™­ tuning during this tour, which would since be their primary sound. The 25 June show from Valencia was filmed and used as part of a mini documentary entitled, Reunited, which is featured on the DualDisc release of the 2005 album Angel of Retribution.
