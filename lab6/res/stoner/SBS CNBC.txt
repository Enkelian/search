SBS CNBC
SBS CNBC is a 24-hour business news channel in South Korea. History On August 13, 2009, SBS took over all stocks of a sports TV channel Xports, which were shared by CJ Media (70%) and IB Sports (30%). After a partnership agreement with CNBC which taken on October 22, 2009, Xports was shut down and replaced by SBS-CNBC on December 28, 2009. Most of its programme are being made in High Definition.
