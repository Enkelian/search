Reaching Out (Queen + Paul Rodgers song)
Reaching Out / Tie Your Mother Down is a song by rock band Queen + Paul Rodgers released in 2005 from the album Return of the Champions. Track listing Reaching Out / Tie Your Mother Down (Live) – 5:39 Fat Bottomed Girls (Live) – 5:48 Credits Bass – Danny Miranda Drums – Roger Taylor Guitar – Jamie Moses Keyboards – Spike Edney Lead Guitar – Brian May Vocals – Brian May, Paul Rodgers, Roger Taylor Other versions In 2009 Eminem wrote a song called "Beautiful", where the beginning and the end samples this song.
