Somerset Live
Somerset Live is a website covering news, entertainment and sport in Somerset and nearby areas in Dorset and Wiltshire. It is owned by Reach plc (formerly Trinity Mirror), with headquarters in Yeovil.The Somerset Live website moved onto a new platform in March 2017, and subsequently took over the websites of additional regional daily newspapers.It operates as the website for a number of local newspapers, including: Bath Chronicle Blackmore Vale Magazine Central Somerset Gazette Cheddar Valley Gazette Frome Standard and Somerset Guardian Shepton Mallet Journal Wells Journal Western Gazette == References ==
