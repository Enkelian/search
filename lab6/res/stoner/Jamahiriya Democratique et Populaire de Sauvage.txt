Jamahiriya Democratique et Populaire de Sauvage
Jamahiriya Democratique et Populaire de Sauvage is the third studio album by American post-punk band Savage Republic, released in 1988 by Fundamental Records. It was reissued on Mobilization Records with bonus instrumental tracks in 2002. Track listing All tracks are written by Philip Drucker, Greg Grunke, Thom Furhmann, Brad Laner, Bruce Licher and Ethan Port, except where noted.
