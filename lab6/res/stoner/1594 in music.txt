1594 in music
== Events == Thomas Ravenscroft joins the choir of St Paul's Cathedral, London. Alfonso Fontanelli and Carlo Gesualdo visit Venice, Florence, Naples, and Venosa. Publications Carlo Gesualdo – Madrigali libro secundo Thomas Morley – Madrigalls To Foure Voyces ...
