Jean-Marc Janaillac
Jean-Marc Janaillac (born 26 April 1953) is a French businessman best known for being a former CEO of Air France–KLM. Early life and education Jean-Marc Janaillac was born on 26 April 1953 in Saint-Sulpice-de-Roumagnac, a village in the Dordogne department, in southwest France. He holds a Licence in law. He graduated from HEC Paris in 1975 and the École nationale d'administration - a graduate school entrusted with the selection and initial training of senior French officials - in 1980.
