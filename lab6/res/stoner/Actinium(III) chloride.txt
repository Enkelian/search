Actinium(III) chloride
Actinium(III) chloride is a chemical compound containing the rare radioactive element actinium. It has the formula AcCl3. Molecular weight of the compound is 333.378 g/m ol. Reactions AcCl3 + H2O → AcOCl + 2 HCl Synthesis 4 Ac(OH)3 + 3 CCl4 → 4AcCl3 + 3CO2 + 6H2O.
