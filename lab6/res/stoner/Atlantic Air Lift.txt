Atlantic Air Lift
Atlantic Airlift was a French regional airline. It provided the only scheduled service to the Île d'Yeu Aerodrome, on the Île d'Yeu off the west coast of France. The service linked the Île d'Yeu with Nantes Atlantique Airport. The airline made its first flight in April 2005. It operated two daily return flights, using a nine-seat Cessna 208B Grand Caravan.
