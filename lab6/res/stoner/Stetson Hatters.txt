Stetson Hatters
The Stetson Hatters are composed of 18 teams representing Stetson University in intercollegiate athletics. The Hatters compete in the NCAA Division I Football Championship Subdivision (FCS) and are members of the Atlantic Sun Conference for most sports, except for the football team, which competes in the Pioneer Football League. Sports sponsored A member of the Atlantic Sun Conference (ASUN), Stetson University sponsors teams in seven men's and ten women's NCAA sanctioned sports.
