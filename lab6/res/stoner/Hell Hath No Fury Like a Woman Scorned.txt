Hell Hath No Fury Like a Woman Scorned
Hell Hath No Fury Like a Woman Scorned is a 2014 American musical play created, written, produced, and directed by Tyler Perry. It stars Cheryl Pepsii Riley and Patrice Lovely. Plot Anita, a single woman, meets a man online thanks to encouragement from a friend. At first he treats her like a queen, they get married, and things change. Anita then turns into a woman that she never knew she could or would become.
