Field hockey at the 1984 Summer Olympics
Field hockey at the 1984 Summer Olympics in Los Angeles took place from 29 July to 11 August 1984 at the Weingart Stadium, in Monterey Park. Men's tournament Preliminary round Group A Group B Medal round Final standings Pakistan West Germany Great Britain Australia India Netherlands New Zealand Spain Kenya Canada Malaysia United States Women's tournament The United States and Australia played out a penalty shoot-out for the bronze medal after finishing equal on points and goal difference in the round robin stage, which the United States won 10–5. Pool Final Standings Netherlands West Germany United States Australia Canada New Zealand Medal summary Medal table * Host nation (United States) Medalists Penalty stroke competition for the bronze medal: United States defeated Australia 10-5. Both teams had a 2-2-1 record and 9 goals for and 7 goals against. Canada also had a 2-2-1 record but had 9 goals for and 11 goals against. West Germany finished just ahead of the tied teams with a 2-1-2 record to claim silver.
