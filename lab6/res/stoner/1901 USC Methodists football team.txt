1901 USC Methodists football team
The 1901 USC Methodists football team was an American football team that represented the University of Southern California during the 1901 college football season. The team competed as an independent without a head coach.The team lost its only intercollegiate game of the year to Pomona College. The team also played a game against an All-Southern California all-star team, losing by a 45 to 0 score. Schedule == References ==
