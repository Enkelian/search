Eurojet Airlines
Eurojet Airlines was an airline based in France founded in 2003. It was closed on January 2, 2004 due to bankruptcy. Fleet The company fleet consisted of a Boeing 737-400 (registration code F-GNAO), leased from a Swiss aircompany Avione which was later on March 4, 2004 acquired by Islandsflug.
