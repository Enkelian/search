William Jolitz
William Frederick Jolitz (born February 22, 1957), commonly known as Bill Jolitz, is an American software programmer best known for developing the 386BSD operating system from 1989 to 1994 along with his wife Lynne Jolitz . Jolitz received his BA in Computer Science from UC Berkeley. He and his wife reside in Los Gatos, California with their children Rebecca, Benjamin and Sarah.
