Hand Over Fist
Hand Over Fist is a collaborative album by Minnesota rapper Mike Mictlan and hip hop producer Lazerbeak, both members of Minneapolis hip hop collective Doomtree. The album was released on Doomtree Records on October 14, 2008. Music Hand Over Fist is entirely produced by Doomtree producer Lazerbeak.
