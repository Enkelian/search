Graft (1931 film)
Graft is a 1931 American pre-Code thriller film, directed by Christy Cabanne, starring Regis Toomey and future talent agent Sue Carol, and featuring Boris Karloff, who appeared in Frankenstein during the same year. Cast Regis Toomey as Dusty Hotchkiss Sue Carol as Constance Hall Dorothy Revier as Pearl Vaughan Boris Karloff as Joe Terry George Irving as Robert Hall Richard Tucker as Carter Harrison William B.
