The Bay Strikes Back
The Bay Strikes Back was a 2020 concert tour featuring Bay Area thrash metal bands Testament, Exodus and Death Angel. The tour took place in Europe from February 6, 2020 to March 11, 2020, with a possible North American leg to follow. It saw Death Angel continuing to promote their ninth album Humanicide, while the tour predated the release of Testament's upcoming thirteenth studio album Titans of Creation by over a month-and-a-half.
