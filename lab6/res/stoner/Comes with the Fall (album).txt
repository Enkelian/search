Comes with the Fall (album)
Comes with the Fall is the eponymous debut studio album released by the American rock band Comes with the Fall. It is the only album they recorded as a quartet with second guitarist Nico Constantine, who was also a member back when the band was known as Madfly. This album was recorded in Atlanta, Georgia before the group relocated to Los Angeles, California and released through DuVall's DVL Records. Track listing All lyrics are written by William DuVall.
