Flightless (record label)
Flightless is an Australian independent record label, founded in Melbourne in 2012 by King Gizzard & the Lizard Wizard member Eric Moore. Originally founded to release both King Gizzard's music and that of associated acts such as The Murlocs, Flightless has since signed other Australian bands, including Tropical Fuck Storm, Stonefield and Amyl and the Sniffers. History Flightless was started in 2012 in Melbourne by drummer Eric Moore as a label for his band King Gizzard & the Lizard Wizard. The label's earliest releases were shipped in pizza boxes.In 2018, Flightless formed a distribution partnership with ATO Records.
