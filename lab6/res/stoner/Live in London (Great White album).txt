Live in London (Great White album)
Live in London is a Japan-only live album released by the American hard rock band Great White in 1990. The album was recorded live at Wembley Arena in London, UK. Track listing "Move It" - 5:57 "Heart the Hunter" - 5:23 "On Your Knees" - 4:06 "House of Broken Love" - 6:12 "Face the Day" - 5:24 "All Over Now" - 5:02 "Once Bitten, Twice Shy" - 5:43 Band members Jack Russell - vocals Mark Kendall - lead guitar Michael Lardie - rhythm guitar, keyboards Tony Montana - bass Audie Desbrow - drums
