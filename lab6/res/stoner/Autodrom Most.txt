Autodrom Most
Autodrom Most is a motorsport race track in the city of Most in the northwest of the Czech Republic. The racing circuit was built between 1978 and 1983. However, the Most district was known by organizing motorcycle and car races earlier. Autodrome is used for races of cars, trucks, motorcycles and free circuit rides, but also for test rides of developed cars, training of drivers of fire engines, ambulance and police cars, as well as training of drivers in crisis situations, etc. External links Official website (in Czech and English) Most at racingcircuits.info
