Karol Castillo
Karol Castillo (29 August 1989 – 10 April 2013) was a Peruvian model and beauty pageant titleholder. She was crowned Miss Perú Universo 2008 and competed in the Miss Universe 2008 pageant, hosted in Nha Trang, Vietnam. Death On 10 April 2013, Castillo was found dead in her bed while on a trip to Australia, having suffered from a cardiac arrest.
