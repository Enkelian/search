Decree (band)
Decree is a Canadian industrial band from Vancouver that was founded by Chris Peterson and John McRae in 1991. However, writing and gathering ideas started around 1989. Both musicians have also been working together at the beginning of the 1990s in the electronic music band Will. Discography Wake of Devastation (1996, Decibel, Off Beat) Moment of Silence (2004, Metropolis, Minuswelt Musikfabrik, Irond) Fateless (2011, Artoffact) Band members Current members Chris Peterson – keyboard, programming, percussion, engineering Sean Lawson – vocals, noise Ross Redhead – guitar, bass, cello Matt Pease – percussion Former members John McRae – vocals Television Appearance The band is featured in "Dance of the Dead", the third episode of the first season of Tobe Hooper's Masters of Horror, performing a song in the "Doom Room" bar.
