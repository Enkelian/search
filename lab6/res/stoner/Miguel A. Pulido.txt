Miguel A. Pulido
Miguel A. Pulido (born 1956) is an American politician. He is the mayor of the city of Santa Ana, California. He was first elected to the City Council on November 4, 1986. He has been the first and only Latino mayor for the city of Santa Ana, serving in the position since November 8, 1994. He is a member of the Democratic Party. External links City of Santa Ana
