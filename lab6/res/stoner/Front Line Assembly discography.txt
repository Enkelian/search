Front Line Assembly discography
Front Line Assembly, a Canadian Vancouver-based electro-industrial band, has released sixteen studio albums, three live albums, numerous singles and compilations, and two video game soundtracks. With Bill Leeb being the founder and sole permanent member of the band, Michael Balch, Rhys Fulber and Chris Peterson were acting as long-time members during different periods of time.
