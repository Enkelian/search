Live Oak, Texas
Live Oak is a city in Bexar County, Texas, United States. The population was 13,131 at the 2010 census. It is part of the San Antonio Metropolitan Statistical Area. Geography According to the United States Census Bureau, Live Oak has a total area of 4.7 square miles (12 km2), of which, 4.7 square miles (12 km2) of it is land and 0.04 square miles (0.10 km2) of it (0.64%) is water. Demographics As of the census of 2000, there were 9,156 people, 3,429 households, and 2,592 families residing in the city. The population density was 1,956.7 people per square mile (755.4/km²). There were 3,518 housing units at an average density of 751.8 per square mile (290.2/km²).
