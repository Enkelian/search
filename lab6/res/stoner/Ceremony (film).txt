Ceremony (film)
Ceremony is a 2010 American film directed by Max Winkler, in his feature film directorial debut. The film stars Michael Angarano, Uma Thurman, Lee Pace, Rebecca Mader and Reece Thompson. It premiered at the Toronto International Film Festival in September 2010. The film was released on VOD on March 4, 2011 and opened in theaters April 8, 2011. Cast Michael Angarano as Sam Davis Uma Thurman as Zoe Reece Thompson as Marshall Schmidt Lee Pace as Whit Coutell Jake Johnson as Teddy Rebecca Mader as Esme Ball Reception The movie received a 40% rating at Rotten Tomatoes.
