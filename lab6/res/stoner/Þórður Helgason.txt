Þórður Helgason
Þórður Helgason (born Reykjavík, 5 November 1947) is an Icelandic writer and educationalist. Life In 1977, Þórður graduated as a Cand. mag. in Icelandic literature from the University of Iceland. He is now a docent in Icelandic at the Iceland University of Education. He was also editor of the Icelandic poetry magazine Són 2003–2011. Works Gamalt og nýtt eftir Þorgils gjallanda.
